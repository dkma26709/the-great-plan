# Savage Orc Boar Boyz

> Savage Orcs who have tamed and broken a War Boar to ride into battle. The combat fingerprint is brutal even by Greenskin cavalry standards: same Orc-on-Boar shock chassis as the Common Orc Boar Boyz (W 7+ combined biology, Tusker Charge Impact D3 at S 5 AP -1 on the charge), but with the Savage Orc identity overlay — **intrinsic Frenzy** (+1 A, +1 MA, -1 MD, must charge, no disengage), **War-Paint Ward 6+** (the only defensive save the rider has — no worn armour, no shield), and the Orc-tribe **Loves a Scrap** sustained-combat +1 A from round 2. A unit of five Savage Boar Boyz on the charge delivers: Tusker Charge Impact (5 × D3 hits at S 5 AP -1) + rider Choppa-with-Frenzy A 2 at MA 5 + Boar Tusks A 1 at MA 3 — all at the cost of a glass-cannon defence layer (mount NA 6+ chitin and rider Ward 6+ are the entire defensive stack; no armour, no shield, and Frenzy makes them easier to hit in return at MD 2). The army-list role: a forward shock unit you point at the enemy line and accept will probably not be alive at the end of the battle. Different defensive profile from Common Boar Boyz (steady armour vs frenzied ward) at the same price.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines charge / self-hits at the rider's S 4.
- **Loves a Scrap** *(Orc-specific faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. Stress relief on melee engagement + sustained-combat +1 A on a single weapon profile from round 2 onwards.
- **Frenzy** *(Psychology — see §8)*. Intrinsic from the moment the army deploys. F4 fingerprint: +1 A on a single weapon profile, +1 MA, -1 MD, must charge nearest visible enemy if able, no voluntary disengagement, must follow up + pursue, Immune to Psychology, suppresses Wavering and Broken state effects. **Frenzy is lost permanently** when the unit loses a combat round (strict loss) or is forced to flee while Broken. Once lost, the rider reverts to baseline Common Orc Boar Boy combat output but retains War-Paint.
- **War-Paint** *(Savage Orc identity rule)*. Each rider is daubed in fungal-mineral war-paint pigments. **The unit has a Ward Save of 6+** against every wound suffered (per §8 Ward Save — unmodified D6 roll). Always-on, no conditions, no degradation. Applies to wounds suffered by either rider or mount (the mount is painted too — a War Boar with painted flanks is the iconic Savage Orc Boar Boy image).
- **Tusker Charge** *(Orc Boar Boyz unit identity rule, inherited from Common Boar Boyz baseline)* — On the turn this unit successfully charges into melee, before the Combat phase begins, resolve the following Impact attack (per §8 Impact):
  - **D3 Impact hits per model** at **S 5** (mount S 4 + 1) | **AP -1** (tusks) | **D 1**
  - Resolved against the charged unit's models within Reach of any charging model in base contact.
  - Damage does not carry between models. Failed charges produce no Impact attacks.
  - *(Frenzy interaction: Frenzy's +1 MA improves the to-hit roll of the *rider's* attacks in the subsequent Combat phase, but does not modify the Tusker Charge Impact step — Impact is a flat-hit mechanic with no to-hit roll involved.)*

## Options

**No worn armour options.** Savage Orc cultural lock; war-paint requires bare skin. The Savage Boar Boy cannot take Light Armour or Medium Armour. The War Boar mount keeps its biological NA 6+ tough hide (NA is biology, not worn armour, and survives the cultural lock).

**No shield option.** Same cultural lock — the shield would block the war-paint and disable the Ward 6+ save.

**Equipment swap options (replace Choppa, pick at most one):**
- **Spear** (+1 pt/model) — 1H, 2" reach, replaces Choppa. Cavalry-charge-spear; on charge round gains +1 S and AP -1 (per the WAP-canonical Boar Boy spear charge bonus, inherited from Orc Boar Boyz).
- **Pair of Choppas** (+2 pts/model) — second hand weapon; +1 A on weapon (A 2); cannot pair with shield (shield is unavailable anyway).

**No Great Choppa option.** Cavalry-tier Boar Boyz do not carry two-handed great weapons — the Boar's bouncing gait and the lance / spear utility of cavalry leave no room for the heavier Great Choppa. Matches the Orc Boar Boyz weapon kit (Choppa / Spear / Pair of Choppas only).

**Command options:**
- **Boss (Leader)** (+5 pts) — Savage Orc Boar Boy champion
- **Standard Bearer** (+10 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 25 pts)
- **Musician** (+5 pts)
