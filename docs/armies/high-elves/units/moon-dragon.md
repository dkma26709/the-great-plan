# Moon Dragon

> The middle tier of Ulthuan's Dragons — the mature lineage. "Those Dragons that surpass the Sun Dragons in might and enlightenment are known as Moon Dragons." Where the Sun Dragons are young and hot-tempered, and the Star Dragons are ancient as the firmament itself, the Moon Dragons are *mature* — centuries old, settled into their full physical and intellectual maturity, but not yet bearing the millennia of accumulated power that marks the Star Dragons. Mid-Lord-mount tier — ridden by Princes and senior High Elf nobles, often the workhorse Dragon-mount of Caledorian armies. Drafted here as the **apex Monster Mount profile** (W 10 tier, between Sun Dragon W 8 and Star Dragon W 12). Sister-pieces: Sun Dragon (Young, mid-Monster), Moon Dragon (Mature, apex), Star Dragon (Ancient, super-apex). The High Elf battlefield read: bind the Moon Dragon to a Prince-tier Lord, deploy it on a flank where the Lunar Breath's S 5 / AP -1 template can disrupt enemy infantry blocks while the Lord's swordwork carves through whatever survives.
