# Common Goblin Archers

> The bow-and-sling cousin of the Common Goblin spear-block. Same scrawny green frame, same cringing courage-by-arithmetic, but armed for the dishonest range-band instead of the dishonest melee. A Goblin Archer regiment loosing volleys from the second rank isn't trying to drop an enemy unit on its own — it's trying to soften an exposed flank, harass a wavering line into breaking, or pin down a fast-moving target while the rest of the army arrives. Goblin opportunism extends to range as readily as melee: a Goblin Archer is *more accurate* when his target is already turning to run (**Stab in the Back — +1 to Wound applies to both melee and ranged attacks**, triggered by rear-arc engagement or by the target being Shaken/Wavering). The volume is what matters; twenty Goblins each loosing a Shortbow into a single enemy block produces enough hits, on enough turns, that the Stab-in-the-Back trigger has time to fire. As with every Goblin unit, the courage holds while the mob is twenty bodies deep (**Stubborn**), and collapses once attrition pulls it below half (**-1 Res for the rest of the battle**).

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines whether the unit charges nearest enemy or takes D3 self-hits at S 4.
- **Stab in the Back** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. **Applies to both melee and ranged attacks.** This unit gains **+1 to Wound** against any target that is either (a) being attacked in its **rear arc** *(melee only — ranged attacks have no rear-arc concept and never trigger this clause)* or (b) currently **Shaken or Wavering** *(triggers for both melee and ranged)*. Does not stack with itself.
- **Cowardly Numbers** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. While this unit has **20 or more models**, it is **Stubborn** (per §8 — ignores the state effects of Shaken and Wavering). Once this unit is reduced to **fewer than half its starting strength**, it suffers **-1 Res** for the rest of the battle.
- **No Loves a Scrap** *(Goblin sub-species — see §13)*. Common Goblin Archers do not inherit the Greenskin faction-wide Loves a Scrap rule. Brawling drains Goblins; it does not refresh them.

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Hand Weapon** *(melee profile — baseline kit, 1H)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — short blade or club carried at the belt; the Goblin Archer's actual melee profile if a charge connects. Shield is not available — the bow / sling occupies the off-hand `[1H Blade]`

### Ranged

- **Shortbow** *(default ranged)* — **18" range** | **A 1** | **S 3** | **AP 0** | **D 1** — the rank-and-file Goblin bow; light, simple, drawn quickly. Fires while moving at the standard -1 to-hit penalty (no Move-or-Fire restriction; no Quick to Fire either). The first Shortbow profile in the ruleset — establishes the cheap-S 3-volume-fire baseline for future bow-armed rank-and-file drafts.
- **Sling** *(swap option, ranged — replaces Shortbow, -1 pt/model)* — **12" range** | **A 1** | **S 3** | **AP 0** | **D 1** | **Quick to Fire** (see §8) — a leather sling and a pouch of stones. Trades the Shortbow's 6" range advantage for fire-on-the-move freedom and a Stand-and-Shoot reaction without the standard -1 to-hit penalty. Useful in lists where the Goblin Archer block expects its movement to be repeatedly disrupted (Animosity, repositioning) — the Sling-armed variant continues to threaten despite the chaos.
