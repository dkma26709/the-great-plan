# Bretonnia Magic Items


> Bretonnian magic items are *relics of the Lady* — weapons sanctified at her altars, armour blessed in her sacred forests, banners woven from threads sworn at the Lake. Where Lizardmen items are *artefacts of the Old Ones* (cosmic geometry made manifest), Bretonnian items are *gifts of the Lady* (divine grace channelled into mortal craft). Each piece interacts with the **Blessing of the Lady** (per §17 Faction Mechanics) — improving the Ward, gating effects on its presence, or punishing those who lose it. The chivalric ladder runs through the items: standard items for Knight's-Vow knights, special items for Crusader's / Questing Vow tiers, apex items for Grail Vow characters and Lord-tier nobility.

Items follow the §11.5 framework: six categories, slot discipline (one item per category per character), Lord 100 / Sub-Lord 75 / Hero 50 budgets, item costs are flat and unscaled. Per-item restrictions ("Lord only," "Knights Errant only," "Wizard only," etc.) appear inline in the Effect column.

> **Lance-on-charge convention.** A Bretonnian character wielding a mundane **Heavy Lance** (or Light Lance) AND a **Magic Weapon pinned to Hand Weapon** may **choose at the start of each combat round which to wield** for that round. The chivalric lance-discipline overrides the §11.5 magic-precedence rule for this specific Lance + Hand-Weapon combination — the player may use the lance on the charge round (where its charge spike fires) and switch to the magic Hand-Weapon for subsequent rounds, or commit to either weapon throughout. Magic Weapons pinned to Heavy Lance (e.g. Silver Lance of the Blessed) replace the mundane Heavy Lance per normal pinning rules — no choice mechanism, the magic lance is always wielded.

#### Magic Weapons

| Item | Effect | Pts |
|---|---|---|
| **Silver Lance of the Blessed** | Pinned to **Heavy Lance**. Inherits the wielder's Heavy Lance profile (reach, A, S, D — including charge spike). **While the wielder retains the Blessing of the Lady** (per §17 — i.e. has not fled from combat): the wielder may **reroll failed To Hit rolls** with this weapon, and the target must **reroll successful Armour Saves and Ward Saves** against any wound from this weapon. **If the wielder transitions to the Broken state and flees** (per §5) at any point in the battle: they immediately suffer **D6 hits at S 4** with **Flaming Attacks** and **Ignores Armour Saves** as the Lady withdraws her gift in disgust — the wielder may rally and fight on as normal afterwards, but the Lance's blessing is gone. The apex chivalric weapon — silver-shafted, Lake-forged, the Lady's mark visible along its length | 70 |
| **Sword of Heroes** | Pinned to **Hand Weapon**. Inherits the wielder's hand-weapon profile (reach, A, S, D). **Against targets with Toughness 5 or greater**, the weapon's effective Strength is **+2** (e.g. S 4 → S 6) and the weapon gains **Multiple Wounds (D3)** for that combat. Against T 4 or lower targets, the weapon profile is unmodified — the hero's blade finds its purpose only against the truly mighty. A chivalric anti-Ogre / anti-Monster / anti-champion sword, blessed for the slaying of greater foes | 20 |

#### Magic Armour

| Item | Effect | Pts |
|---|---|---|
| **Armour of Agilulf** | Heavy armour and shield (combined save 3+ on foot, integrates with mount armour as normal per §8 Armour Save Stacking; capped at 2+). **The wearer's MA and MD are both set to 10** (each independently overrides the wearer's profile value; a base stat already above 10 is unaffected). **The wearer cannot wield Two-Handed weapons** (no Great Weapon, no 2H Polearm — restricted to one-handed weapons that pair with the shield). Forged in the Bretonnian forges of legend; the armour itself is the school — fights any foe with the precision of a master duellist | 20 |
| **Helm of the Dragon Slayer** | Counts as **Light Armour** (one step) for §8 Armour Save Stacking — combines with worn armour and shield per the standard rule. The wearer is **immune to Breath Weapon attacks** (cone-exhalation attacks such as dragon breath, Salamander Spout Flames, the Wyrmbreath Vial — distinguished from Flaming-keyword weapons, which still affect the wearer normally). A helm fashioned for the slaying of dragons — purified against the great wyrms' breath, blessed for the chivalric duel. *(Note: Breath Weapon is currently informal in the ruleset — only referenced in weapon profiles. Promotion to a §8 keyword is queued as a follow-up task; the descriptive scope here is forward-compatible.)* | 20 |

#### Talismans

| Item | Effect | Pts |
|---|---|---|
| **Sirienne's Locket** | **Bretonnian Lord only.** The wearer is **immune to Killing Blow** and **immune to Multiple Wounds (X)** — incoming attacks with these special rules deal damage as if the rule were not present. In addition, the wearer **cannot suffer more than 3 wounds in any single phase** — once 3 wounds have been applied in a phase, all subsequent wounds in that same phase are ignored. The wearer may still be removed by attacks framed as "instant kill" / "remove from play" effects that bypass the Wound system entirely. A locket of woven moonsilver containing a single tear of the Lady — the wearer's body itself refuses to fall faster than the Lady permits | 50 |
| **Lucky Heirloom** | **Once per Combat phase**, the bearer may **reroll a single failed dice roll** of one of the following types: a To-Hit roll (the bearer's own attack), a To-Wound roll (the bearer's own attack), an Armour Save roll (the bearer defending in melee), or a Ward Save roll (the bearer defending in melee). The choice is made at the moment the failed roll is announced. A modest family-pendant carried for generations, blessed with the small grace of timing — when it matters most, the dice favour the bearer just once | 15 |

#### Arcane Items

| Item | Effect | Pts |
|---|---|---|
| **Prayer Icon of Quenelles** | **Wizard only.** The bearer's **Blessing of the Lady Ward improves by one step** for both the bearer and any Lady-Blessed members of the unit the bearer is attached to (per §1 Character Attachment), for the duration of the battle. Stacks with the bearer's vow tier (Knight's Vow Blessing 6+/5+ → 5+/4+; Grail Vow Blessing 5+/4+ → 4+/3+). Members of the unit who do not have the Blessing of the Lady (e.g. peasant Men-at-Arms in a Damsel-led block) do not gain the Ward — the Icon improves an existing Blessing rather than granting one. A relic of the great cathedral at Quenelles, the bearer carries the Lady's nearer presence; her Blessing falls more readily on those gathered around the icon | 45 |
| **Sacrament of the Lady** | **Wizard only. Once per battle.** At the start of any Magic phase, the bearer may invoke the Sacrament. When invoked: roll **2D3** and add the result to your **shared Magic pool** for that phase. The bearer **may not cast any spells that Magic phase** (the bearer's own personal Cast pool is unspent for the phase; Channeling and Dispelling are unaffected). Other friendly wizards may freely use the shared pool dice as normal. A consecrated cup of Lady-water carried into battle by the most senior caster — drinking it suspends the bearer's own channel for the phase but floods the army's wider pool with the Lady's grace | 50 |

#### Magic Banners

| Item | Effect | Pts |
|---|---|---|
| **The Grail Banner** | All friendly **Bretonnia units** within **12"** of the unit carrying this banner gain **+2 to their Resolve** for stress-threshold purposes (per §5). Shaken, Wavering, and Broken thresholds all shift by +2. Stacks with Inspiring Presence (Res-override + this banner's +2 buff both apply: a unit using a Res-10 General's IP within both auras is treated as Res 12 for thresholds). The Lady's banner held high, the chivalric host fights with one heart — a 12" aura of unbroken resolve | 40 |
| **Errantry Banner** | **Knights Errant only.** On any turn the unit charges, every Knight Errant in the unit gains **+1 Strength** for that combat round (rider attacks only — applies to the wielder's S, not the mount's S). The young knights ride harder under the errantry banner — eager to prove themselves, harder to restrain | 25 |

> **Lore notes:** First-pass Bretonnia magic items list — two items per category (12 total) to anchor the framework. Items selected from the WAP Bretonnia catalogue, prioritising Lady-themed picks that translate cleanly into our §8 Special Rules vocabulary and exercise the Blessing-of-the-Lady interaction layer (Silver Lance gates on Blessing, Helm and Prayer Icon improve the Ward, Grail Banner stacks with morale auras). The **Antlers of the Great Hunt** key off the §4 Pursuit mechanic drafted in the same pass — the two land in lockstep.
