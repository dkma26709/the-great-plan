# Sunblood


> An ancient Saurus glowing with the azure energies of the Celestial Realm — a demigod-tier combat specialist whose deeds date back to the Age of Myth. Where the Oldblood considers strategy and the Scar-Veteran fights at the apex of mortal Saurus skill, the Sunblood walks the realms with one purpose: destroy Chaos and its followers. Burning with starlight, weapons wreathed in halos of azure fire, the Sunblood perceives enemy weaknesses as other creatures might scent blood upon a wounded animal — and answers with a blood-chilling roar that sends his Saurus kin into a tearing frenzy. Often entrusted to defend vital ziggurat sites, especially the sacred spawning pools.

  - **Sunblood's Roar** *(identity rule, once per battle)* — at the start of any friendly Combat phase, the Sunblood may unleash its blood-chilling roar. All friendly **Saurus** units with at least one model within **12"** of the Sunblood gain **+1 to all melee to-wound rolls** for that combat phase. Captures the lore-direct "blood-chilling roar causing his kin to instinctually respond by falling upon the chosen enemy and tearing them to bloody tatters."

> **Lore notes:** Demigod-tier Saurus combat specialist, ancient enough that his deeds date to the Age of Myth. The celestial form is mechanically real: NA 4+ (denser than normal Saurus scales), Ward 6+ (partially insubstantial, Azyr-touched), and Magical Attacks + Flaming on the Sunblood Warblade (weapons "wreathed in halos of starlight"). **Predator's Eye** captures the lore-direct "perceive enemy weaknesses as other creatures might scent blood upon a wounded animal" — re-roll failed melee to-hit against any unit that has already taken unsaved wounds in the battle. **Sunblood's Roar** is the once-per-battle Saurus-army buff: +1 to wound for friendly Saurus within 12", lore-direct "kin instinctually respond by falling upon the chosen enemy and tearing them to bloody tatters." Specialist Hero — not a strategic commander, not a banner-bearer, just a battle-genius defender wreathed in azure starlight.
