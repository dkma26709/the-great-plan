# Aggradon Lancers


> Elite Crested Saurus on predatory saurian mounts. The Aggradons are frothing, bloodline-old reptiles that only a Saurus of the crested temple-cohort can control — and even then, only until the first taste of blood. Once an Aggradon has killed, the rider is no longer steering: they are pointing. The rest is fury, fangs, and celestite.

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurus, Monstrous Cavalry, Special
- **Unit type:** Monstrous Cavalry
- **Unit size:** 3-6
- **Combined pool per model:** W6 (rider 2 + mount 4). Attackers use highest WS (4) and highest T (5) when resolving hits against this unit, per §1 *Rider and Mount Profiles*
- **Natural Armour:** Aggradon hide **5+**
- **Worn armour (rider):** light armour
- **Combined save:** **3+** (NA 5+ + light armour + shield, per §8 Armour Save Stacking — 3 sources)
- **Equipment (default — whole unit picks one weapon):**
  - **Celestite Spear + shield**, OR
  - **Celestite Club + shield**
- **Command options:**
  - **Patrol Leader** (champion, +10 pts, no stat buff) — acts as unit leader for Rally, challenges, and Leader-snipe purposes
  - **Standard bearer** (+15 pts — may carry a **Magic Standard up to 50 pts**, per Saurus elite-cavalry convention)
  - **Musician** (+10 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls
- **Special rules:**
  - Stubborn (see §8)
  - Predatory Fighter (see §8)
  - **Blood Frenzy** — once this unit's Aggradon mount attacks inflict one or more unsaved wounds in a combat phase, the unit gains the **Frenzy** special rule (see §8) for the rest of the battle. This Frenzy **cannot be lost** — not by losing a combat round, nor by any other means that would ordinarily strip Frenzy. The mount has tasted blood and will never stop chasing it. A guided missile, armed.

> **Lore notes:** Replaces the legacy Cold One Riders as the heavy Saurus cavalry Special slot — Aggradons are the current-edition mounts, with proper models and an identity that doesn't lean on a Stupidity mitigation scaffold. Saurus rider stats match the Saurus Warriors baseline (W2, Ld10, T4) with Stubborn + Predatory Fighter inherited from the Saurus keyword. The Aggradon's W4 brings pooled durability to W6 per model, priced through the quadratic wound factor at ~120 pts. Blood Frenzy is the identity rule — a one-way trip into committed aggression. Once the first unsaved wound is dealt, the unit cannot be held back; the mount is decides for everyone. Against elite targets and large prey especially, Aggradon Lancers are designed to commit fully and finish what they start.
