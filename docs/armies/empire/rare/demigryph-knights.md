# Demigryph Knights


> The Empire's Rare-slot shock cavalry. Demigryphs are griffon-dogs — lesser kin of the great Griffon, tameable where the true Griffon will not bear a rider unless it accepts you. The Reiksguard and certain Elector Count households keep Demigryph kennels. When they charge, they're claws-and-beak as much as lance — Monstrous Cavalry scale, able to run down knights and monstrous infantry alike. The Empire's answer to Chaos Knights, Ogres, and anything else that outmasses a Warhorse. Ridden only by Inner Circle Knights — no junior knight earns a Demigryph.

- **Keywords:** Empire, Human, Monstrous Cavalry, Rare
- **Unit type:** Monstrous Cavalry
- **Unit size:** 3-6
- **Wounds per model:** **W3** — Demigryph mount W3 + rider conversion (Inner Circle Knight W1 → +0 step), per §1 *Combined Wounds*. Attackers use highest WS-D (4) and highest T (4)
- **Natural Armour:** —
- **Worn armour (default):** Full plate + Heavy Shield + barding (per §8 Armour Save Stacking, one step per source; cap 2+)

  - Disciplined (see §8)

Lore: Demigryphs hunt larger prey than Warhorses would dare — the beast's instinct sharpens against worthy quarry

> **Lore notes:** Empire's Rare-slot shock cavalry — Inner Circle Knights mounted on griffon-dogs, lesser kin of the great Griffon. Monstrous Cavalry scale: claws-and-beak as much as lance, able to run down knights and monstrous infantry alike. The faction parallel to Seraphon's Aggradon Lancers — both are Monstrous Cavalry, both ridden by elite warriors. Demigryphs are *fast / better-armoured / lighter-hitting* (Aggradons are *slow / tanky / harder-hitting*). **Griffon-kin Instinct** is the identity hunt-the-bigger-prey rule (+1 to wound on mount attacks vs Monstrous Infantry / Monstrous Cavalry / Monster / Warbeast keywords) — Demigryphs hunt larger prey than Warhorses dare. Combined 2+ save via full plate + shield + barding; M 8 with barding (drops from M 10). **Fear** differentiates from Imperial Knights (no Fear). Knightly Order upgrades cascade from Imperial Knights with the same menu and costs.
