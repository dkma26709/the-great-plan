# Seraphon (Lizardmen)

*The cold-blooded servants of the Old Ones march to the Great Plan.*

The proof-of-concept army for The Great Plan ruleset — the most heavily iterated faction.

## Faction identity

- **Predatory Fighter** signature on Saurus units — extra attacks generated on unmodified rolls of 6 to hit (Saurus-caste signature, reserved to Lizardmen)
- **Cold-Blooded** Recovery 2 across the army — faster stress recovery than other factions
- **Blessed Spawnings** — list-build customisation menu drawing from the nine Old Ones (Chotec, Huanchi, Itzl, Quetzl, Sotek, Tepok, Tlazcotl, Xahutec, Xhotl). Applies to the Lizardman race (Saurus, Skink, Kroxigor) only — not to mounts, beasts, or Monster-keyword units
- **Apex magic** — Slann at the top of the ruleset's caster ladder. Mastery of All Winds, ~28 spells of Slann-exclusive faction magic across the two Slann lores
