# Black Orcs

> Chaos-Dwarf-bred warrior-elite of the Greenskin race — "biggest, meanest, strongest, most intelligent and most brutal of all the Greenskin races." Black Orcs originated as a Dawi-Zharr breeding experiment: the Chaos Dwarfs sought a more reliable slave-force and used Dark Magic on the strongest captured Greenskins to create a new strain. The experiment succeeded "far beyond their expectations" — Black Orcs proved disciplined enough to *lead* the eventual slave rebellion, escaping the Mountains of Mourn and integrating into Greenskin society. Today they remain the cult-elite anchor of any Orc warlord's army. **Disciplined** in a way no other Orc is — they conduct *military drills*, **clean and repair their wargear after battle**, and "refuse to accept Animosity behaviour in their presence." **Quell Animosity** is the lore-defining identity rule: an Orc Warboss who learns to "get a Black Orc to bellow orders and threats at the lesser Orcs — and even administer a good beating from time to time to keep the gits in line" finds their entire army more reliable. **Armed to da Teef** is the second defining identity: Black Orcs carry a heavy black plate harness and **multiple weapons** — a Choppa with shield, a paired-Choppa load, and a heavy Great Weapon — and switch between them freely at the start of each Combat phase as the tactical situation demands. **Stubborn** captures their lore-essential resilience: "endure the thickest of enemy fighting" without flinching. The combat fingerprint that emerges: an Orc cult-elite at MA 5 / MD 4, Res 8, 3+ save (with shield), the rare Disciplined Orc, and a 6" anti-Animosity aura that buys the rest of the Greenskin army its tactical reliability.

## Equipment

- **Equipment (default, fixed):** Black Orc Choppa + Pair of Black Orc Choppas + Great Weapon + Heavy armour + Shield. The Black Orc is "armed to da teef" — all three weapon profiles are carried simultaneously, and the active profile is declared at the start of each Combat phase per **Armed to da Teef** (see Special Rules). Save varies with the active weapon profile.

## Special Rules

- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines charge / self-hits. *(Black Orc manifestation: "Get on with it!" — a Black Orc squabble is impatience to fight, not lazy bloodlust. Mechanically identical to Common Orc Animosity.)*
- **Disciplined** *(Psychology — see §8)*. **The only Orc sub-tribe to break the Greenskin Undisciplined default.** +1 to Recovery rolls. Lore-explicit: Black Orcs conduct military drills, clean wargear, and "refuse to accept Animosity behaviour in their presence" — the only Greenskin unit type to honour the cross-faction Disciplined-cult convention.
- **Loves a Scrap** *(Orc-specific faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. Stress relief on melee engagement + sustained-combat +1 A from round 2 onwards. Orc-sub-species inheritance — Black Orcs are still Orcs, and they thrive on the brawl as much as any other.
- **Stubborn** *(Psychology — see §8)*. The Black Orc ignores state effects of Shaken and Wavering — MA / MD / BS penalties and the cannot-charge restriction do not apply. Lore-supported: "endure the thickest of enemy fighting" + military drills + cult discipline. Combined with Disciplined + Res 8, Black Orcs are the **morale-anchor** of any Greenskin army.
- **Quell Animosity** *(Black Orcs unit identity rule)* — *"Quit yer squabblin'!"* Friendly **Greenskin** units with any model within **6"** of this unit at the start of the Movement phase **automatically pass their Animosity roll this turn** (treat the D6 as a 2-6 result — no squabble triggered). The Black Orc's presence bellows orders at the lesser Orcs and Goblins, keeping their small minds on the enemy ahead. **Range 6"**; **Black Orcs cannot suppress their own Animosity via this rule** (they still roll Animosity for themselves per §8). The lore-defining mechanical contribution Black Orcs make to a Greenskin army: a single Black Orc unit anchors several nearby Orc / Goblin / Snotling units against the Animosity tax.
- **Armed to da Teef** *(Black Orcs unit identity rule)* — Black Orcs carry the **Black Orc Choppa + Shield**, the **Pair of Black Orc Choppas**, AND the **Great Weapon** simultaneously, "armed to da teef" per lore. At the **start of each Combat phase**, the controlling player declares which of the three weapon profiles is active for this unit *for that Combat phase*. The active profile determines the unit's Attacks, Strength bonus, AP, and shield availability for the duration of that phase. The Black Orc can switch profiles between Combat phases — no commitment, no penalty — letting the unit adapt to whatever is in melee range each round.

## Options

**No equipment swap options.** The full "armed to da teef" kit (Choppa+Shield / Pair of Choppas / Great Weapon) is baked into the base profile via Armed to da Teef; players don't choose at deployment — they choose each Combat phase.

**No worn armour options.** Heavy armour is the cult-elite kit; the Black Orc identity does not bend to list-build downgrade.

**Command options:**
- **Boss (Leader)** (+5 pts) — Black Orc champion
- **Standard Bearer** (+10 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 50 pts) — elite banner budget, matching Greatswords / Knights / Chaos Warriors tier
- **Musician** (+5 pts)

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat — declared at start of each Combat phase per Armed to da Teef

- **Black Orc Choppa + Shield** *(option 1 — 1H with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 4 | AP -1 | D 1 | **Save 3+ (heavy armour + shield)** — the defensive mode. Black Orc shields are large bossed steel and bonespike affairs, plundered from Dwarfs or Men or forged in the Black Orc's own barracks-forge. The 3+ save absorbs most rank-and-file fire and most return-strikes. Choppa is the iconic Black Orc weapon — "their choppas are the best, plundered from Dwarfs or Men." `[1H Blade]`
- **Pair of Black Orc Choppas** *(option 2 — 2× 1H paired, no shield)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 4 | AP -1 | D 1 | **Save 4+ (heavy armour only)** — the multi-strike mode. The Black Orc grips a heavy choppa in each fist, doubling the swings at the cost of the shield. Best against light-armoured rank-and-file infantry where the +1 A volume outpaces the lost save. `[Paired]`
- **Great Weapon** *(option 3 — 2H, no shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S+2 (= S 6) | AP -1 | D 1 | **Save 4+ (heavy armour only)** | **-1 Initiative when used** | Two-Handed — the heavy-strike mode. A massive cleaver, ship's-anchor, or maul wielded in two hands. **S 6** wounds T 4 on 2+ and T 5 on 3+ — meaningful against heavy-armoured opponents that the Choppa can't crack. **AP -1** held (per the Orc weapon ladder, "+S not +AP" past Choppa baseline). **-1 I when used** per the system-wide Great Weapon I-penalty principle — the heavy weapon slows the swing; the Black Orc strikes at effective I 2 with the Great Weapon mode active. Best against Chaos Warriors, heavy cavalry, and Monsters. `[2H]`
