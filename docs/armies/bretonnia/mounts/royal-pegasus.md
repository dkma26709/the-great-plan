# Royal Pegasus *(Lord, Paladin)*


The duke's personal stable selects only the finest pegasi from the Forest of Loren and the Grey Mountain peaks for the Royal Pegasus designation — larger, faster, and meaner than the common-stable pegasi ridden by Pegasus Knights. A Royal Pegasus carries the duke or his most senior champion alone; it does not stable with the regimental pegasi, and it does not bear lesser knights.

**Royal Pegasus (barded):**

| M | WS | BS | S | T | W | I | Ld |
|---|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|
| 6 | 4  | 0  | 5 | 4 | 3 | 4 | 6  |

*(Royal Pegasus baseline ground M 8, reduced to M 6 by barding per §8 Barding rule. Not a Bretonnian destrier — Pegasus is a winged magical creature, not a warhorse, and does not have Purebred Warhorse.* **Fly 14"** *— see special rules below.)*

- **Available to:** Bretonnian Lord (+45 pts), Paladin (+30 pts). NOT available to Damsel or Prophetess (per WAP — Lady's Chosen take Pegasus, Unicorn, or Sacrosanctum, not Royal Pegasus)
- **Combined pool with rider:** rider W + 3. Attackers use highest WS and highest T per §1
- **Worn armour:** barding (per the rider's character entry kit)
- **Special rules (intrinsic to Royal Pegasus):**
  - **Fly (14")** (see §8) — faster than the regular Pegasus's Fly 12. Captures the WAP "Swift as the Wind" rule — Royal Pegasi are bred from Loren / Grey Mountain wild stock specifically for *speed*, the duke's personal mounts above the common stable
  - **Iron-hard Hooves** *(intrinsic)* — the mount's Hooves and Bite weapon profile gains **+1 S** baseline (S 4 → S 5). Royal pegasi are bred for harder bone, denser hoof, sharper bite
  - **NOT Purebred Warhorse** — Pegasus is a magical winged creature, not a destrier; barding's -2 ground M penalty applies normally. Fly 14" is the operational movement and is unaffected by barding

**Mount weapon profile:**

- **Royal Pegasus's Hooves and Bite** *(mount natural)* — 1" reach | **A 3** | S 5 | AP 0 | D 1 — three attacks per Royal Pegasus at S 5 (Iron-hard Hooves baseline). Compare to regular Pegasus's A 2 / S 4 — the Royal Pegasus is meaningfully more dangerous in close combat

> **Stat upgrade summary vs regular Pegasus:** W 2 → 3 (+50% durability), A 2 → 3 (+50% mount attacks), Hooves S 4 → 5 (+1 wounding tier), Fly 12 → 14 (+2" reach per Movement phase). The Royal Pegasus is a meaningful step up — +15 pt premium for the Lord (+30 vs +45) buys real durability and attack-output gains. Combined with a Lord (W 5) for unit W 8, or with a Paladin (W 4) for unit W 7 — substantial flying-platform durability.
