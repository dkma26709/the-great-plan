# Grail Knights

> The apex of the chivalric order — knights who have personally completed the Quest for the Grail, encountered the Lady in person, drunk from her cup, and returned transformed. Each Grail Knight is a singular hero in his own right, his presence on the battlefield a sign of the Lady's full favour. They ride the duchy's finest destriers, wield the heaviest lances, and fight with the *certain* calm of those who have seen the Lady and need fear nothing in this mortal world. The Grail Knight is not a younger hero proving himself; he is a finished man, his life's purpose fulfilled, riding out at the duke's request to bless one final campaign with the saint's presence. Where Knights of the Realm are the hammer and Questing Knights the anvil, Grail Knights are the army's *crown* — rare, holy, and decisive.

## Special Rules

- Disciplined
- **Grail Vow** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — intrinsic. Confers **Improved Blessing of the Lady** (5+/4+), **Stubborn**, and **Immune to Psychology**. The chivalric ladder's apex tier
- **Blessing of the Lady (Improved)** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — via the Grail Vow. Ward Save **5+ vs S ≤ 4, 4+ vs S ≥ 5**, applying to rider and mount alike. One step better than the standard Knight's / Crusader's / Questing Vow Blessing of 6+/5+. Lost on flee from combat
- **Stubborn** (see §8) — ignores the state effects of Shaken and Wavering. Their certainty transcends ordinary morale
- **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) — stress from Fear, Terror, Panic-source events, and any other psychology-source trigger does not accumulate on this unit. Already-accumulated stress is unaffected; only new psychology stress is blocked
- **Lance Formation eligible** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — counts as Lance whenever the unit's geometry forms a triangular wedge of 6+ models in 3+ rows; +2" reach + +1 AP on melee weapons during the charge round
- **Purebred Warhorse** (see §8) — applies to the mount profile. Negates the -2 M penalty for barding; Grail Knights retain M 10 with full barding
- **Hallowed Steel** *(identity rule)* — the unit's melee weapons (Heavy Lance) count as **Magical Attacks** (see §8) at all times. Every Grail Knight's lance is the lance he carried into the Quest, blessed at the Lady's altar before he set out and sanctified by his return. Useful against Ethereal targets, against any rule requiring magical-attack source, and as a passive identity layer. Distinct from Questing Knights' Anointed Steel — same mechanic, different lore source (Grail completion vs Damsel's blessing)
- **Vision of the Grail** *(identity rule, once per battle)* — once per battle, the controlling player may invoke this unit's Vision. Choose **one** of the following effects when invoking:
  - **Before any of this unit's charge distance rolls:** reroll the charge distance roll (Formed: reroll the D6; Loose: reroll one D6 of 2D6, take the second result)
  - **At the start of any combat round in which this unit is engaged:** reroll all failed To Hit rolls for the unit's attacks in that round
- The Grail Knight steadies himself with the memory of the Lady's chapel — the moment of certainty sharpens his lance-craft or quickens his charge. The two options serve different tactical roles: the charge reroll secures a critical assault; the to-hit reroll converts a clutch combat round. Once per battle — this is a *vision*, not a constant capability

## Options

**Command options:**
- **Leader** (+5 pts) — *Grail Champion*; the senior Grail Knight, often a duke's chosen who has held the cup longest
- **Standard bearer** (+10 pts — Magic Standard up to 50 pts; the Grail-tier banner budget, one step above standard chivalric to capture the Grail Banner / Banner of the Lady's Grace tier)
- **Musician** (+5 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Heavy Lance** *(default, 1H with shield — fixed kit, no swap)* — **2" reach | A 2 | on charge (first round): S+2 (= S 6), AP -2, D 2 | Magical Attacks** (per Hallowed Steel); **subsequent rounds: S+0 (= S 4), AP 0, D 1 | Magical Attacks** — the apex chivalric shock weapon, blessed at the Lady's altar. A 2 reflects the Grail Knight's apex skill (every veteran knight has fought past simple single-attack training; the second attack captures the Lord-tier-character A 2 baseline applied to the rank-and-file Grail brotherhood). With Lance Formation +2" reach extension on the charge round, becomes 4" reach + AP -3 in the wedge `[1H Lance]`
- **Warhorse's Hooves and Bite** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — standard barded-warhorse profile `[Natural]`
