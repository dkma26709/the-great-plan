# Saurus Oldblood


> Ancient — thousands of years old — the apex of the Saurus caste. Not physically *stronger* than a Scar-Veteran; not *faster*; not wielding a better class of weapon. What the Oldblood possesses is **age**. The latent strategic knowledge of war, implanted at spawning by the Old Ones, unlocks steadily across a Saurus's lifetime. The Oldblood carries millennia of unlocked understanding. He cannot articulate a manoeuvre in words — but when he moves, the entire Lizardmen line reorients around his decisions. Attended by scores of Skinks who adorn him with bronze armour-plates, warpaint, oils, and the shrunken heads of sacrificed foes.

> **Lore notes:** Thousands of years old — the apex of the Saurus caste. Crucially, *not* physically stronger than a Scar-Veteran; what the Oldblood possesses is **age**. The Old Ones implanted latent strategic knowledge of war at spawning that unlocks across a Saurus's lifetime; the Oldblood carries millennia of unlocked understanding, cannot articulate a manoeuvre in words, but when he moves, the entire line reorients around his decisions. Differentiation from Scar-Veteran sits on WS 7 (the absolute top melee skill in the roster), W 8 (centuries of survival), NA 3+ thickened scales, and weapon Damage +1 — *precision* of placement, not greater strength (S 5 / T 5 / I 3 unchanged). **Primeval Command** is the identity rule: 18" Saurus Res aura + per-turn stress-strip on a nominated unit, the latent strategic knowledge enacted as morale anchor. The **Light Armour** upgrade is the lore-direct "scores of Skinks secure bronze armour-plates" — Skink-attended battle-prep made mechanically explicit. Inherits Locked Jaws and Tail Sweep from the species pass.
