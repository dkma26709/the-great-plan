# Gor-Rok


> The Great White Lizard, the Scarred One — among the greatest Saurus in living memory. Spawned at Itza with pure albino colouring and lone arrival, marked from his first breath as a future champion. Now an apex Sunblood-tier combatant: the **solid centre** around which Saurus battle lines advance, the rock on which Itza's enemies are broken. Heavily scarred body, oversized frame thick-corded with muscle, scales tough enough to turn most blade-strikes. He has survived horrendous wounds — pulled an Elven assassin's lance through his own chest to bite the wielder's throat — and never has injury hindered him from duty. Gifted by the Skink-priesthood with the Mace of Ulumak (whose hand the Old Ones themselves guide) and the Shield of Aeons (a massive volcanic-stone slab a dozen Kroxigor were needed to haul). Gor-Rok knows neither pain nor fear; in combat he is unrelenting, always seeking the next opening, never himself stepping backwards.

**Points:** **336** base (intrinsic regalia + Mark of the Old Ones included; no upgrades available)

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurian, Saurus, Sunblood, Character, Hero, Named
- **Unit type:** Infantry (Character)
- **Natural Armour:** **3+** (oversized scales, "tough enough to turn most blade-strikes" — bumped from Sunblood baseline 4+)
- **Worn armour:** **Heavy Shield** (Shield of Aeons — see Special Rules)
- **Combined save:** **2+** baseline (NA 3+ + Heavy Shield +1 step), **2+** vs ranged (Heavy Shield class +1 vs ranged on top of baseline; capped at 2+ minimum roll). Per §8 *Shield Class*.
- **Ward Save:** **6+** (Sunblood celestial form baseline)
- **Equipment (intrinsic — cannot be removed or swapped):** Mace of Ulumak, Shield of Aeons (Heavy Shield class)
- **Mount/Equipment options:** none (named character)
- **Special rules:**
  - **Sunblood baseline package** — Disciplined, Cold-Blooded, Predatory Fighter, Fear, Locked Jaws, Stubborn, Predator's Eye (re-roll failed to-hit vs units that have suffered unsaved wounds), Sunblood's Roar (once-per-battle +1 to wound aura for friendly Saurus within 12")
  - **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) — lore-direct "knows neither pain nor fear." Stress from Psychology-keyword rules does not accumulate.
  - **Mark of the Old Ones** *(intrinsic — albino spawning, baked-in)* — at the start of each battle, Gor-Rok generates **D3+1 Old Ones' Favour** tokens. Each token may be spent to **reroll any single D6** rolled by Gor-Rok or by a unit he is part of. Re-rolled result stands. Maximum one Mark of the Old Ones per army (Gor-Rok in the army means no other character can take this spawning).
  - **Mace of Ulumak** *(intrinsic — Old-Ones-Guided)* — Gor-Rok **rerolls all failed to-hit rolls in melee**. The lore-canonical "Old Ones themselves guide the hand of one who wields the Mace" — Gor-Rok's strikes always find their mark.
  - **Shield of Aeons** *(intrinsic — massive volcanic-stone slab, Heavy Shield class)* — when Gor-Rok is **charged** by an enemy unit, the charging unit immediately suffers **D3 hits at S 5 / AP -1 / D 1** before any other charge effects resolve. The shield is an *obstacle* — chargers slam into it before reaching Gor-Rok. Lore: "his shield acts like an obstacle for those foolishly brave enough to charge him with full force." (Shield Class: Heavy. Save bonus + ranged bonus already encoded in the Combined Save line above.)
  - **The Scarred One** *(passive identity rule)* — Gor-Rok takes **1 less damage** from any attack with damage greater than 1 (minimum 1 damage dealt). Multi-wound effects (D 2, D D3, D D6, etc.) all have their damage reduced by 1. A single-damage attack still hits for 1; a D 3 attack becomes D 2; a D D6 becomes D D6−1. Captures lore-direct "survived horrendous wounds, monstrous scars, lance impaled through chest" — Gor-Rok always reduces the *severity* of multi-wound hits.

> **Lore notes:** The Great White Lizard, the Scarred One — apex Sunblood-tier combatant whose role is the *solid centre* around which Saurus battle lines advance. Pure albino spawning at Itza, marked by the Old Ones from the moment of his arrival. Gor-Rok is the canonical character carrying **Mark of the Old Ones** (D3+1 reroll tokens per battle); no other character may take this Mark when Gor-Rok is fielded. **Mace of Ulumak** rerolls all failed melee to-hit (Old-Ones-Guided — the Old Ones literally guide the wielder's hand). **Shield of Aeons** is a massive volcanic-stone slab (Heavy Shield class, combined 2+ save) that punishes chargers — D3 S 5 / AP -1 / D 1 hits before contact, captured lore-direct as "an obstacle for those foolishly brave enough to charge him." **The Scarred One** is a passive multi-wound damage reduction (-1 D, min 1) — captures the canonical "survived horrendous wounds" framing without bolting on a once-per-battle clutch save. **Immune to Psychology** is direct lore: "knows neither pain nor fear."
