# Lore of Fire


> Pure destruction. Where Heavens is celestial manipulation and Beasts is feral empowerment, Fire is unrelenting burning — the lore of open destruction. Every damage spell is Flaming. Many spells Remain In Play, leaving the battlefield alight with persistent threats an opponent must commit dispelling power to put out.

**Lore Attribute — Kindled Flame:** when this wizard casts a Fire spell targeting a unit that has **already been targeted by a Fire spell this Magic phase** (by any caster, friendly or enemy), the spell's casting value is reduced by **1**. Rewards pouring fire onto the same target — once something's burning, it's easier to burn it more. Minimum casting value 0.

**Signature Spell — Fireball**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 7 | 24" | Magic Missile | Instant |

D6 hits at **S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**. Every Fire wizard can call down a Fireball.

#### Basic (Tier 1) — LA 1+

**Flame Breath**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 7 | 12" | Template (flame) |

Uses the **flame template** (see §7.4 for template mechanics). All models under the template take one hit at **S3, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. A gout of fire from the caster's hands or mouth. CV reflects scale — a tightly-packed unit can take 10+ hits from a single template, so the spell is much stronger than its modest profile suggests.

**Ignite** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 7 | 24" | Hex, Remains In Play |

Target enemy unit gains the **Flammable** special rule (see §8) while this spell remains in play. Sets up follow-up Fire spells and any other Flaming attack. RIP makes this a multi-turn investment — a Fire wizard who lands Ignite and survives accelerates every subsequent Flaming attack against the marked target.

**Piercing Bolts of Burning**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 9 | 24" | Magic Missile |

D6 hits at **S5, AP 0, D1, Flaming, Ward Piercing (1)**. Bolts of fire that cut through magical protection as readily as flesh. Ward Piercing is rare at Basic tier — a Fire wizard who lands this on a Ward-saving target accelerates damage past the protection most other lores can't bypass at this tier.

**Flame Cage** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 10 | 24" | Hex, Remains In Play |

Target enemy unit is caged in circling fire. While this spell is in play, whenever the target unit performs any movement action (Advance, March, Charge, Wheel, voluntary Flee, Elusive's reactive move), it suffers **D6 hits at S4, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. The unit is not prevented from moving — it simply pays the price in fire each time it tries. Strong-for-tier RIP — punishes every type of movement and scales with how mobile the target is, so the CV is held at the top of Basic.

#### Intermediate (Tier 2) — LA 2+

**Burning Head**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 10 | 18" | Template (small round), Travel |

Pick a point within range and a direction. Place the **small round template** at the aimed point. Roll 2D6 — the template travels that many inches in the chosen direction, resolving in a straight line. Every model whose base is touched by the template's path takes one hit at **S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**. A flaming skull launched like a bowling ball.

**Cascading Fire-Cloak** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 10 | 18" | Augment, Remains In Play |

Target friendly unit is wreathed in defensive fire. While this spell is in play, at the start of each Combat phase, each enemy unit with any model within **3"** of the target suffers **D3 hits at S4, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. Punishes anyone engaging or surrounding the protected unit. Strong-for-tier defensive RIP — every enemy committing to the cloaked unit's fight pays a tax, and the spell scales with how many engagements the unit attracts.

**Molten Strike**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 11 | 18" | Magic Missile |

D3 hits at **S7, AP -3, D2, Flaming**. A concentrated lance of liquid fire — bores through heavy armour and elite infantry alike. The anti-plate answer in the lore's toolkit. Very-strong-for-tier — S7 AP-3 D2 cracks heavy cavalry, monsters, and elite infantry that no other Intermediate Fire spell can touch. Range 18" (vs Piercing Bolts' 24") is the strength-for-range trade — Molten Strike hits harder but the wizard has to commit further forward.

#### Advanced (Tier 3) — LA 3+

**Flame Storm** *(RIP, template, drifting)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 12 | 18" | Template (large round), Remains In Play |

**On cast:** aim a point within range. The template **scatters D3"** in a random direction from the aimed point and lands there — the placement is never perfectly reliable. Every model whose base is under the template's final position suffers **1 hit at S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**.

**Each subsequent controlling-player Magic phase while in play:** the template **moves D6"** in a random direction. Every model whose base is touched by the template at **any point along this movement path** suffers **1 hit at S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**. If the final resting position would overlap a model not already touched by the movement, shift the template the smallest distance needed to clear those bases (this shift does not produce additional hits).

A firestorm that drifts across the battlefield, lethal to anything in its path. Not escalating — just consistently dangerous each turn until dispelled or burned out.

**Sun's Wrath**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 14 | 36" | Magic Missile |

D6+2 hits at **S6, AP -2, D2, Flaming, Ward Piercing (2)**. The Advanced offensive capstone — concentrated solar heat called down on a single target. Designed to crack characters, monsters, and heavily warded elite units. Very-strong-for-tier: D6+2 hits averaging 5.5, S6 AP-2 D2 with WP2 punches through Ward saves that Piercing Bolts can't reach. Range 36" lets the spell strike across the battlefield — the lore's longest-reach option, befitting its capstone status before the Master tier.

**Conflagration** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 13 | 24" | Hex, Remains In Play |

Target enemy unit becomes the seat of a spreading fire. While this spell is in play, each controlling-player Magic phase the target suffers **D3 hits at S4, AP -1, D2, Flaming**, and every other enemy unit with any model within **3"** of the target at that moment suffers **D3 hits at S3, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. The fire consumes the target and jumps to anything near it. Strong RIP with multi-target collateral — every turn it survives, the fire keeps damaging the target *and* anything nearby; CV held high in Advanced.

#### Master (Tier 4) — LA 4+

**Burning Sun**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 18 | 24" | Direct Damage, Instant |

Target enemy unit suffers **2D6 hits at S 6, AP -2, D 2, Flaming, Ward Piercing (1)**. All other enemy units with at least one model within **6"** of the target suffer **D3 hits at S 5, AP -1, D 1, Flaming**. The wizard summons a fragment of the sun directly above the target — instant, blinding, devastating to the unit beneath and scorching anything nearby. Apex single-target damage with splash; the splash component punishes tightly-deployed armies that try to support the primary target. CV 18 reflects the sheer damage potential — 2D6 hits at S6 D2 Ward Piercing plus splash on every adjacent unit is more raw output than any Heavens spell.

**Wall of Fire** *(RIP, placed barrier)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 16 | 24" placement | Hex (placed), Remains In Play |

*Remains In Play.* Place a **12"-long line of flame** anywhere within 24" of the caster (the line must clear impassable terrain and cannot be placed in melee). While in play, **any model** (friendly or enemy — Fire is impartial) **that touches the line during a movement action** — by crossing it, or by ending its movement with its base in contact — suffers **1 hit at S 4, AP -1, D 1, Flaming**. Each model resolves at most one such hit per movement action it performs.

The wall denies a corridor of the battlefield until dispelled; per-model resolution means small units cross relatively safely while large formations pay heavily — a 30-strong infantry unit that marches across loses 30 hits' worth of fire in one movement. Friendly-fire risk is the trade for hard zone-control: the wizard who calls the wall has to accept she may have blocked her own line of advance as well.

> **Design note on Lore of Fire.** Range identity is **mid-range** — templates short (12" Flame Breath), magic missiles 18-24" with the strength-for-range trade explicit (Molten Strike at 18" S7 vs Piercing Bolts at 24" S5), RIPs and hexes 18-24", with Sun's Wrath at 36" as the iconic single long-reach capstone. CV calibration after this pass: Sig 7 (must be reliable, also benefits from Kindled Flame Lore Attribute when chained), Basic 7-10 with strong-effect spells (Flame Cage, Piercing Bolts) at the top of band, Intermediate 10-11 with Molten Strike at the high end, Advanced 12-14 with Sun's Wrath at the cap. Master 16-18: **Burning Sun** for apex single-target burst with splash, **Wall of Fire** for persistent battlefield-control. RIP density is deliberately heavy in this lore (Ignite, Flame Cage, Cascading Fire-Cloak, Flame Storm, Conflagration, Wall of Fire all RIP) — Fire's identity is "leave the battlefield burning"; an opponent must commit dispelling power to put out persistent threats, and a Fire wizard can layer multiple RIPs at once. Lore Attribute (Kindled Flame: -1 CV when targeting a unit already hit by Fire this phase) remains unchanged and rewards stacking spells onto the same target.
