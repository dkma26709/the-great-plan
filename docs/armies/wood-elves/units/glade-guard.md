# Glade Guard

> *"Once a Wood Elf comes of age, he or she is inducted into their household's Glade Guard and is given the responsibility of patrolling a section of the forest."* The Glade Guard are the core of the Asrai battle-line — every adult Wood Elf serves in the household guard for at least a generation, the longest-trained and most numerous tier of the Asrai warrior tradition. They are the **first response** to intruders in Athel Loren: silent, accurate, lethal. They wield the **Asrai Longbow** — the apex longbow tradition in the Old World, a 30" mithril-tipped weapon engraved with the household's runes — and shoot from formed ranks behind the skirmisher screen, raining arrow-storms onto enemies who have not yet realised the forest is closing around them. The cult fingerprint: BS 5 (apex archer baseline), AP -1 elf-craft arrows, **Hunter's Prowess** (the Asrai channel of the elven reflex framework — reroll to-Hit 1s with the bow), **Forest Stride** (no penalty in dense forest terrain), and the cross-elf **Elven Grace** (Ward 6+ in melee against attacks at the elf's I or lower). Lightly armoured — Asrai cultural disdain for plate — and reliant on volley-fire from range, supported by the woodland itself, the Glade Guard kill intruders *before* the intruders know to look up.

## Special Rules

- **Elven Grace** *(faction-wide elf species trait — see §8 Defence)*. Ward 6+ in melee against attacks made at the elf's Initiative or lower. At I 5, the elf catches most opponents' strikes — only apex-I attackers (cult-elite Asur, Druchii Executioners, certain Hero / Lord characters) bypass the Ward.
- **Hunter's Prowess** *(Asrai faction-wide species trait — see §8 Combat)*. Reroll to-Hit 1s with ranged attacks. The Asrai channel of the elven reflex framework — the bow rather than the sword. At BS 5 (hit on 2+), the reroll catches the only natural-1 misfires; the Glade Guard hits its mark unless the world itself conspires against the shot.
- **Forest Stride** *(Asrai-distinct faction trait — working title; pending §8 lift)*. This unit treats **forest terrain** as Open ground — no movement penalty, no Dangerous Terrain test, full M 5 advance through dense undergrowth, thorn-fields, and Athel Loren bramble. The forest does not impede the forest-folk. *(Pending §8 lift to the Asrai faction-wide rule slot when the second Asrai unit is drafted; mechanically the rule is inert against non-forest terrain.)*
- **Asrai Way of War** *(Wood Elf faction-wide identity rule — see FACTION.md)*. While in **Loose** state (per §6), this unit gains the **benefits of the Skirmisher special rule** (per §8): **Coherency 2", +1 Movement, Fire on the move, Free pivots**. The "Cannot Form" clause does NOT apply — Glade Guard may freely toggle between Formed (rank-discipline volley line, depth-discipline Resolve bonus, command-aura access) and Loose (skirmisher-style mobile archers, fire-after-marching, dispersion -1 to-Hit from incoming ranged). The player chooses at deployment and each activation. *(Lore-canonical: Glade Guard fight as the forest fights — formed when the line must hold, fluid and dispersed when speed and evasion matter more than the volley.)*

## Options

**Worn armour upgrade option:**
- **Light armour** (+1 pt/model) → 6+ save (cuirass of woven bark and worked leather, Asrai-craft; the heaviest armour culturally acceptable to the Asrai).

**Magic Arrow upgrades** *(unit-wide upgrade, mutually exclusive — the unit may take at most one arrow upgrade; replaces the standard Asrai Longbow shot type for the battle)*:
- **Trueflight Arrows** (+1 pt/model) — the Asrai Longbow gains **No to-Hit Modifiers** for the battle: long range, moving, soft / hard cover, and any other to-Hit penalty is **ignored**. The arrow flies true to its mark. Pairs with the unit's Hunter's Prowess reroll-1s — the combined effect is near-perfect ranged accuracy.
- **Hagbane Tips** (+1 pt/model) — the Asrai Longbow gains **Poisoned Attacks** (per §8: +1 to-Wound) for the battle. Trades the elf-craft accuracy of the standard arrow for thorn-extract venom. Especially effective against high-T targets (Trolls, Monsters) where the +1 to-Wound is decisive.
- **Starfire Shafts** (+0.5 pt/model) — the Asrai Longbow gains **Flaming Attacks** for the battle. Starfire-blessed shafts ignite at the moment of release. Useful against Regeneration-keyword targets (Trolls, Treemen — the Asrai's own Forest Spirits notwithstanding, Starfire Shafts are blessed against Forces of Destruction by Athel Loren ritual) and Undead.

**Command options:**
- **Lord's Bowman (Leader)** (+5 pts) — Glade Guard champion, a senior household-archer with greater accuracy or longer standing.
- **Standard Bearer** (+8 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 25 pts). *Asrai Banners — woven from Athel Loren spider-silk, pending Magic Standard system — held for later treatment.*
- **Musician** (+4 pts) — Glade Guard horn-bearer; sets the volley cadence.

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Hand weapon** *(default 1H melee)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — Asrai-craft short sword or hatchet, the household-issued sidearm. Not the unit's primary tool; Glade Guard fight in melee only when their volley line has been overrun. `[1H Blade]`

### Ranged

- **Asrai Longbow** *(default ranged — apex elf-craft longbow)* — **30" range | AP -1 | D 1** — the apex longbow tradition in the Old World. S follows the wielder (S 3 — slim elf biology); the bow's strength is reach and precision, not raw power. **AP -1** captures the elf-craft mithril arrow-tips — Asrai arrows pierce mail and cloth where peasant-issue longbows skip off. The 30" range matches the Bretonnian longbow's traditional reach. Paired with the unit's **Hunter's Prowess** (reroll to-Hit 1s) at **BS 5**, the Glade Guard's accuracy is the apex of the codebase — the unit hits on a natural 2+, rerolling 1s, ignoring most to-Hit penalties through the arrow upgrades. *(With magic arrow upgrades taken, the profile gains the upgraded keyword: Trueflight = No to-Hit Modifiers; Hagbane = Poisoned; Starfire = Flaming.)*
