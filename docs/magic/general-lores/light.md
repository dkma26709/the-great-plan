# Lore of Light


> Divine radiance. A protective lore first, a damage lore second — the light steadies the faithful, wards against darkness, and burns the corrupt where it falls upon them. Where Heavens is celestial and impersonal, Light is holy and disciplined. Its wizards stand still to channel properly, and their magic rewards troops that hold their ground rather than those that run ahead.

**Lore Attribute — Burning Purity:** this wizard gains **+1 to their casting roll** on a Light spell if they did **not move** during this turn's Movement phase (neither Advanced, Marched, Charged, Wheeled, nor voluntarily Fled). The disciplined, contemplative wizard channels light more effectively. Most wizards want to move, so this is a genuine trade-off — the bonus is earned by committing to stillness.

**Signature Spell — Shield of Light**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains **Ward vs Spells (6+)** until start of next turn. A modest magical shielding — the cornerstone of Light's protective identity.

#### Basic (Tier 1) — Lore Access 1+

**Steady the Faithful**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 7 | 18" | Augment, Instant |

Target friendly unit immediately removes **D3 stress**. The light steadies their resolve — morale that was wavering finds focus. Distinct from Life's physical healing: this mends the mind, not the body.

**Guiding Light**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 7 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit may reroll failed to-hit rolls of 1 in both melee and ranged attacks until start of next turn. A subtle clarity — the wizard illuminates targets, gives the unit sight of their foe.

**Pha's Protection**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 9 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains **+1 to all armour and ward saves** until start of next turn. Walls of brilliance deflect incoming blows. Subject to the normal Armour and Ward stacking caps (2+ armour, 4+ ward from stacking). Strong-for-tier — boosts every save the unit holds simultaneously, so the CV is held at the high end of Basic.

#### Intermediate (Tier 2) — Lore Access 2+

**Net of Amyntok**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 10 | 24" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit's **Movement is halved** (round up) and the unit cannot declare a **March** action until start of next turn. Bands of golden light anchor them to the ground. Pairs thematically with the disciplined caster — the light judges those who move too freely. Strong dual-effect movement hex — combines Movement halving with March denial, so the CV is held at the top of Intermediate.

**Timewarp**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 11 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains **+2 Movement and +2 Initiative** until start of next turn. Time dilates around them — they move faster, react faster. A major positional and strike-order buff. Strong-for-tier dual-stat boost — +2 to two characteristics is among the largest single-cast augments in the lore, so the CV holds high in Intermediate.

**Speed of Light**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 9 | Self | Augment (caster only), Instant |

The caster and their unit (if attached to one) may immediately make a free **M + D6 move** directly away from the nearest enemy model. A bolt of brilliance that carries them to safety. Does not prevent the caster from casting again this phase; does not trigger any pursuit, Elusive-style penalty, or stress from Fleeing (this is a controlled magical escape, not flight).

**Sanctuary of the Faithful**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 10 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit suffers **-1 to-Hit against all incoming attacks** (melee and ranged) until start of next turn. A halo of dim radiance distorts sight-lines around the unit — every blow misjudges its mark. Distinct from Pha's Protection (which boosts saves) and Pha's Vigil (which strips stress) — Sanctuary denies the to-Hit roll itself, layering a *third* defensive axis. Stacks with Pha's Protection on the same unit for a turn of near-uncrackable defence (boosted saves *and* attackers missing more often), at the cost of two casts and the wizard standing still to claim Burning Purity on both.

#### Advanced (Tier 3) — Lore Access 3+

**Banishment** *(RIP, template, stationary)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 12 | 24" | Template (large round), Remains In Play |

On cast: aim a point within range. The template **scatters D3"** in a random direction and lands. Every model whose base is under the final template position takes **1 hit at S5, AP -2, D2**.

Each subsequent controlling-player Magic phase while in play: the template **remains stationary**. All models currently under it take **1 hit at S5, AP -2, D2**.

A pillar of searing light fixed in place. Devastating to anything caught beneath it and impossible to ignore — the opponent must dispel it, detour around it, or eat the damage every turn. Unlike Fire's drifting Flame Storm, Banishment's threat is predictable, and that predictability shapes the opponent's movement.

**Pha's Judgement**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 13 | 24" | Magic Missile |

**D6 hits at S5, AP 0, D2, Ward Piercing (3)**. Against units with the **Daemon** or **Undead** keyword: the spell instead deals **D6+2 hits** (same profile). The Advanced anti-unnatural capstone — Ward Piercing 3 strips virtually any ward save, and the extra hits against the corrupt push the damage further. Strong-for-tier; CV held high in Advanced.

**Light of Battle** *(RIP, aura)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 12 | Self | Augment (aura), Remains In Play |

*Remains In Play.* While in play, friendly units with any model within **12"** of the caster are **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) and may **reroll failed recovery and rally rolls**. A standing aura of clarity and resolve — being within the light holds an army together through horror. The caster becomes a morale anchor, and positioning them near the main line is a major tactical consideration.

**Beacon of Absolution**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 13 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit immediately **removes all stress**, becomes **Immune to Psychology** (see §8), and gains **Ward vs Spells (5+)** until start of next turn. A total mental and magical cleansing — the light rewinds the damage of fear and magic in one absolute moment. Ideal for a unit about to break, a target under magical pressure, or any desperate save-the-crucial-unit moment. Three-effect emergency reset — strong-for-tier; CV held high in Advanced.

#### Master (Tier 4) — Lore Access 4+

**Light of Phakth**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 18 | 24" | Direct Damage, Instant |

Target enemy unit suffers **2D6 hits at S 5, AP -2, D 2, Ward Piercing (3)**. Against units with the **Daemon**, **Undead**, or **Chaos** keyword, instead **2D6+4 hits** at the same profile. The wizard channels the dawn-light over the target — pure radiance burns through any Ward, and the corrupt take far worse. Pushes Light's anti-corruption identity to its apex: WP3 strips virtually any Ward save and the +4 hits against Daemon / Undead / Chaos make this the lore's specific answer to those armies. CV 18 reflects the raw output ceiling — against a Vampire Counts or Daemon target, 2D6+4 hits at S5 D2 with WP3 is more than enough to break a unit outright.

**Pha's Vigil** *(RIP, aura)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 16 | 18" (self-radius) | Augment (aura), Remains In Play |

*Remains In Play.* At the start of **every Magic phase** (both the controlling player's and the opposing player's), all friendly units wholly within **18"** of the caster **remove 1 stress** each. The wizard becomes a beacon of holy resolve — every magical pulse from the caster sweeps fear from the army around her, and the steady light keeps allies from breaking under sustained pressure.

Triggers twice per game turn — while held, the bubble nets ~2 stress shed per turn. Powerful counter to stress-stacking armies (Frenzy-heavy Beasts, Terror-causing Vampire Counts, charge-heavy Bretonnia) and to the Light wizard's own buffed troops who would otherwise be accumulating stress from cycled charges. RIP makes the spell dispellable; the caster has to commit to the line to keep the bubble over the army's main body. Mirrors how the Sig (Steady the Faithful, single-target D3 stress remove on cast) scales at Master: army-wide, persistent, smaller per-trigger but compounding across the game.

> **Design note on Lore of Light.** Range identity is **mid-range** anchored by the wizard's stillness — Burning Purity Lore Attribute rewards not moving, so the spell ranges (18-24" throughout) are calibrated around a wizard who plants and channels rather than maneuvers. The single self-aura RIP (Light of Battle, 12" radius) and the two new self-aura concepts (Pha's Vigil, also 12-18") all reinforce this stationary-channeller identity. CV calibration after this pass: Sig 6 (must be reliable). Basic 7-9, with Pha's Protection at the top of band as a near-Intermediate-strength defensive layer. Intermediate 9-11, with Timewarp's dual-stat boost and the new Sanctuary of the Faithful (-1 to-hit incoming) anchoring the band. Advanced 12-13, with Pha's Judgement (anti-unnatural WP3) and Beacon of Absolution (three-effect emergency reset) at the cap. Master 16-18: **Light of Phakth** for apex anti-corruption damage, **Pha's Vigil** for persistent army-wide stress relief. Lore identity remains protective-first, damage-second, anti-corruption when offensive — three of the four main offensive options (Pha's Judgement, Light of Phakth, and to a lesser extent the implicit framing of Banishment) explicitly punish Daemons, Undead, or Chaos. Lore Attribute (Burning Purity) remains unchanged.
