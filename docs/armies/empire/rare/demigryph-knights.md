# Demigryph Knights

> The Empire's Rare-slot shock cavalry. Demigryphs are griffon-dogs — lesser kin of the great Griffon, tameable where the true Griffon will not bear a rider unless it accepts you. The Reiksguard and certain Elector Count households keep Demigryph kennels. When they charge, they're claws-and-beak as much as lance — Monstrous Cavalry scale, able to run down knights and monstrous infantry alike. The Empire's answer to Chaos Knights, Ogres, and anything else that outmasses a Warhorse. Ridden only by Inner Circle Knights — no junior knight earns a Demigryph.

## Equipment

- **Worn armour (default):** Full plate + Heavy Shield + barding (per §8 Armour Save Stacking, one step per source; cap 2+)

## Special Rules

- Disciplined
- **Fear** (see §8) — Demigryphs are griffon-kin; their scent and predatory motion unsettle lesser creatures
- **Griffon-kin Instinct** *(identity rule)* — mount's **Demigryph Claws** and **Rending Beak** attacks both gain **+1 to wound** when targeting any model with the **Monstrous Infantry, Monstrous Cavalry, Monster**, or **Warbeast** keyword. Lore: Demigryphs hunt larger prey than Warhorses would dare — the beast's instinct sharpens against worthy quarry

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Polearm (Empire Halberd)** *(rider default, 1H with shield)* — **2" reach | A 1 | S+1 (= S 5) | AP -1 | D 1** — consistent damage every round; the polearm's +1 S on S 4 rider brings strikes to S 5. Note: the rider's Polearm carries the same **Killing Blow (Cavalry / Monstrous Cavalry, 2)** trait as the State Troops Halberdiers' weapon — the hook works whoever swings it `[1H Reach]`
- **Heavy Lance** *(rider option, +6 pts — replaces Polearm, shield retained, 1H)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | on charge (first round): S+2 (= S 6), AP -2, D 2; subsequent rounds: S+0 (= S 4), AP 0, D 1
- **Demigryph Claws** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 4 | AP -1 | D 1 — slashing forelimbs, the consistent-damage half of the Demigryph's natural arsenal `[Natural]`
- **Rending Beak** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 4 | AP -2 | **D D3** — the Demigryph's hooked beak tears flesh in long strips; lore-faithful spike-damage attack matching the "rending" identity. Griffon-kin Instinct applies, making the beak especially lethal against MonstInf/MonstCav/Monster/Warbeast targets `[Natural]`
