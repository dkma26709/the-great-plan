# Forest Goblin Spider Riders

> Forest Goblins mounted on Giant Spiders — the spider-cult sub-tribe's signature cavalry unit. Forest Goblins live in jungle and forest canopies, breed cattle-sized spiders for war, and dip every weapon their tribe wields in milked spider-venom. The Spider Riders are mid-tier Goblin cavalry — slower than Wolf Riders (M 7 vs M 9), tougher (mount chitin NA 6+ vs the Wolf's bare hide), and tactically far more mobile in terrain-heavy battles (the Spider crawls over walls, cliffs, and rough ground as if it were open field — **Wall-crawler**, per §8). Every attack the unit makes, melee or ranged, rider or mount, is **Poisoned** — the rider's blade and arrows are venom-dipped, and the spider's bite carries the natural arachnid toxin. The combat fingerprint that emerges: a Forest Goblin Spider Rider unit moves through ground that should slow or stop other cavalry, harasses with poisoned ranged volume, and bites holes in tougher targets via the +1-to-Wound Poisoned bump on every attack. Used well, the unit flanks through woods, vanguard-deploys onto cliffs the enemy can't reach, and picks off wounded characters or fleeing units with Stab-in-the-Back + Poisoned damage stacks.

## Equipment

- **Equipment (default):** Hand Weapon (rider) + Spider Bite (mount, natural). No worn armour, no shield, no shortbow — the default Spider Rider is a bare-Goblin-on-a-Spider, with the chitin NA 6+ as the only protection.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines charge / self-hits at the rider's Strength.
- **Stab in the Back** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. **Applies to both melee and ranged attacks.** +1 to Wound against any target being attacked in its rear arc *(melee only)* or currently Shaken or Wavering *(both melee and ranged)*. Does not stack with itself. *(Stacks additively with Poisoned Attacks — a Forest Goblin Spider Rider shooting at a Wavering enemy gets +1 to Wound from Stab in the Back AND +1 to Wound from Poisoned Attacks for a +2 to Wound on that volley.)*
- **Cowardly Numbers** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. While this unit has 20 or more models, it is **Stubborn**. Once reduced to fewer than half its starting strength, it suffers **-1 Res** for the rest of the battle. *(At Cavalry unit sizes, the Stubborn-while-20+ clause is functionally dormant; the -1 Res below-half-strength clause is the active fragility tax.)*
- **No Loves a Scrap** *(Goblin sub-species — see §13)*. Spider Riders do not inherit the Greenskin faction-wide Loves a Scrap rule.
- **Fast Cavalry** *(see §8)* — Vanguard (deploys up to M outside the deployment zone), free pivots at any point during movement, Flee charge reaction (M + D6 directly away from the charger; +4 stress for the act of Fleeing, no Skirmisher discount).
- **Wall-crawler** *(see §8 — Forest Goblin spider-cult biology, sub-tribe-shared with the Arachnarok Spider)*. The unit ignores Movement penalties from Difficult / Dangerous Terrain and may crawl over vertical impassable features (cliffs, walls). Cannot end movement inside horizontal impassable terrain (water, lava, void).
- **Poisoned Attacks** *(see §8 — Forest Goblin spider-cult identity, applies to BOTH rider AND mount)*. All melee and ranged attacks made by this unit gain **+1 to Wound**. Rider's blade and arrowheads are dipped in milked spider-venom; the Giant Spider's bite carries natural arachnid toxin.

## Options

**Worn armour options:** Light armour (+1 pt/model) — rider's worn protection. Stacks with mount NA 6+ as separate armour sources per §8 *Armour Save Stacking* → combined save **5+** (NA 6+ base + Light armour as additional source = +1 step).

**Worn armour additive:** Shield (+1 pt/model) — combined save improves by +1 step per §8 *Armour Save Stacking*. With **no Light armour**: 6+ → 5+. With **Light armour + Shield**: 5+ → 4+ (NA 6+ + worn armour 6+ + shield, three stacking sources).

**Equipment swap options (replace Hand Weapon, pick at most one):**
- **Spear** (+1 pt/model) — 1H, 2" reach, replaces Hand Weapon. Cavalry-charge-spear; on charge round gains +1 S and AP -1.

**Equipment add-on (ranged):**
- **Shortbow** (+1 pt/model — adds 18" ranged profile, keeps melee weapon for fallback). Not available with shield — the bow occupies the off-hand.

**Command options:**
- **Boss (Leader)** (+3 pts) — champion
- **Standard Bearer** (+6 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 25 pts)
- **Musician** (+3 pts)

## Weapon Profiles

All melee and ranged attacks gain **+1 to Wound** from the unit's **Poisoned Attacks** rule (see Special Rules).

### Close Combat

- **Hand Weapon** *(rider, default, 1H — may pair with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — venom-dipped blade or jungle-stone club. `[1H Blade]`
- **Spear** *(rider, option, 1H — may pair with shield, replaces Hand Weapon)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | S 3 baseline (charge: **S 4, AP -1**) | D 1 — venom-tipped jungle spear; the 2" reach lets a second rank stab past the first, and the charge bonus delivers a heavier impact strike on the turn the unit makes contact. `[1H Reach]`
- **Spider Bite** *(mount, natural — always available)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — Giant Spider's two-stage natural attack: venomous fangs + multi-leg grasping strike. The A 2 reflects spider biology (multiple grasping limbs + bite) rather than a single horse-style hoof or wolf-style bite. The Poisoned Attacks rule applies to both strikes — at S 3 AP 0 with +1 to Wound, the mount can threaten T 4-5 targets that S 3 alone could not. `[Natural]`

### Ranged

- **Shortbow** *(rider, option add-on)* — **18" range** | **A 1** | **S 3** | **AP 0** | **D 1** — same base profile as Common Goblin Archers' Shortbow, but every arrow is venom-tipped (Poisoned Attacks rule applies). Fires while moving at the standard -1 to-hit penalty.
