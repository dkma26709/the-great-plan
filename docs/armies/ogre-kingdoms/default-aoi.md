# Default AoI:


> The classic Ogre Kingdoms army — a brutal Maw-worshipping migration of fat-brutes, led by a Tyrant whose authority is established through single-combat dominance and ratified by the Slaughtermaster's reading of entrails. Where Empire wins via combined arms and Bretonnia via mounted charges, Ogres win by *closing the distance and crushing whatever's still standing*. Fewer models, bigger frames, harder hits. The Maw demands meat; the tribe provides.

**Composition Table** *(default constraints per §9.1)*:

| Category | Constraint |
|---|---|
| Lords | Maximum 25% of army points |
| Heroes | Maximum 25% of army points |
| Core | Minimum 25% of army points |
| Special | Maximum 50% of army points |
| Rare | Maximum 25% of army points |

**Category Assignments:**

| Category | Units |
|---|---|
| **Lords** | Tyrant, Slaughtermaster |
| **Heroes** | Bruiser |
| **Core** | Ogre Bulls |
| **Special** | Ironguts |
| **Rare** | *(no units drafted yet — Mournfang Cavalry, Stonehorn, Thundertusk, Ironblaster, Maneaters, Yhetee, Sabretusk pending future drafts)* |

**Army-wide rules:** None. (Themed AoIs may add — e.g., a *Goldtooth's Legion* AoI might add Hatred (Cathay-pact factions) army-wide, a *Beastclaw Raiders* AoI might shift the Stonehorn / Thundertusk to Special when drafted.)

**Mandatory picks:** None.

**Banned picks:** None.

**Magic lore access:** Slaughtermaster channels the **Lore of the Great Maw** (faction-flavoured; pending separate §11 draft). No general-lore access — Ogre magic is exclusively the Maw's. Slaughtermaster is the only caster in the roster.

**Named-hero access:** No named characters drafted yet.

**Faction-specific army-build hooks:**
- **Gut-plate** intrinsic to all Ogre units (one save step; hands-free, preserves with two-handed weapons).
- **No Inspiring Presence aura** from Ogre characters — authority is single-combat dominance, not charisma. Ogre characters operate on combat axes (extra attacks, no-fall-back enforcement, personal kills), never morale axes.
- **No Predatory Fighter** — reserved to Lizardmen per §8 design call.
