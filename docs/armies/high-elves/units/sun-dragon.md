# Sun Dragon

> The youngest of Ulthuan's Dragons — "the youngest Dragons are referred to by the Elves as Sun Dragons, in reference to their hot tempers and the rich, warm hue of their scales." Few young Dragons are hatched now on Ulthuan; each one is precious to the Asur, especially to the Caledorian houses where the ancient Dragon-bond traditions are preserved. A Sun Dragon is a *young* apex — still hot-tempered, less wise than the Moons and Stars, but already a fearsome battlefield presence: an entire regiment of warriors can be savaged by a single Sun Dragon, a Manticore can be torn apart, a Wyvern's head ripped off. Drafted here as the **mid-Monster-Mount profile** for High Elf Hero / mid-Lord-tier characters. The lowest cost-tier in the Asur Dragon ladder, fitting the lore "young, hot-tempered, less powerful than the elders." Sister-pieces: Sun Dragon (Young, mid-Monster), Moon Dragon (Mature, apex), Star Dragon (Ancient, super-apex). The High Elf battlefield read: an accessible Dragon-mount option for Hero / mid-Lord characters that doesn't lock out 539+ pts on a single combined model. Sun Dragon-mounted Princes are the *typical* Dragon-Caledorian combat formation; Moons and Stars are reserved for the highest-tier nobles.

## Special Rules

- **Terror** *(per §8)* — at LiS 4, projects to **(6 + 4)" = 10"**. A young Dragon is no less terrifying than its elders — the Sun Dragon's youth doesn't diminish its physical-presence horror, only its accumulated wisdom.
