# Raptadon Chargers


> Skinks spawned under the sign of Tepok — bold warriors, fanatically devoted to the wisest of the Old Ones, telepathically bonded with their spawn-kin. Where Raptadon Hunters harry with javelins, Chargers tear apart enemies who are foolish enough to chase the lighter cavalry. Their Tepok Lances are temple-vault relics, fully consecrated; on the charge, they discharge an energy blast that strips armour and incinerates lesser foes before the speartips even land. Often seen accompanying Slann across the realms — the Lances are powerful enough to blast apart anything that threatens a Starmaster.

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Skink, Cavalry, Special
- **Unit type:** Cavalry (Fast Cavalry — see §8)
- **Unit size:** 5-10
- **Wounds per model:** **W1** — Raptadon mount W1 + rider conversion (Skink Brave W1 → +0 step), per §1 *Combined Wounds*. Attackers use highest WS-D (3) and highest T (3)
- **Natural Armour:** 6+ (Raptadon hide); 5+ combined with shield
- **Equipment (default):** **Tepok Lance (Charger variant)** + shield
- **Equipment options:** none baseline
- **Blessed Spawnings:** up to 3 per unit, per-model costs from §10 menu (Skink-rider rate). Each spawning beyond the first promotes the unit one FoC tier (Special → Rare; max Rare). Spawning effects apply to the Skink Brave rider only — the Raptadon mount profile is untouched (Itzl exception aside).
- **Command options:**
  - Patrol Leader (champion, +5 pts, no stat buff)
  - Standard bearer (+10 pts — may carry Magic Standard up to 25 pts)
  - Musician (+5 pts)
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - Fast Cavalry (see §8)
  - **Tepok's Blessing** *(unit identity rule)* — the Skink riders are spawned under Tepok's sign; the unit gains **Magic Resistance (1)** (-1 to enemy cast rolls targeting this unit). Lore-direct: "blessed by Tepok, the wisest of the Old Ones." Does not stack with the Tepok Blessed Spawning's MR (1) effect — pick the better of the two; cannot double-apply.
  - **Holy Vanguard of Tepok** *(unit identity rule)* — when this unit charges, the Tepok Lances discharge their energy blast at the target before impact. The target unit suffers **D3 hits at S 4 / AP -2 / D 1 / Magical Attacks / Flaming** *before* combat is resolved (Hits resolve in the Charges sub-phase per the Impact pattern in §8, but this rule does not require the Impact keyword on the lance — it is a charge-trigger ability of the unit). Captures the lore-canonical "blasts of energy powerful enough to blow a clutch of grots to pieces, stripping armour from survivors so the speartips pierce deep."

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Hand weapon** *(rider, implicit melee fallback)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 `[1H Blade]`
- **Tepok Lance (Charger variant)** *(rider, default)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | on charge: +2 S (= S 5), AP -3, D 2 (first round); subsequent rounds: S 3, AP -1, D 1. The fully-consecrated temple-vault relic spear — meaningfully stronger than the Hunter's Tepok Lance, reflecting the Charger's elevated status as a Tepok-blessed warrior.
- **Raptadon's Serrated Fangs and Claws** *(Raptadon mount, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 3 | AP -1 | D 1 — resolved at mount WS 3, I 3 `[Natural]`

> **Lore notes:** Dedicated charge-specialist cavalry, sister unit to the kite-and-skirmish Raptadon Hunters — same beast, different riders, different roles. Tepok-blessed Skink Braves wielding fully-consecrated relic Lances; their identity is the consecrated charge, not the prey-instinct kill. **Holy Vanguard** captures the lore-canonical pre-impact energy blast — a charge-trigger ability rather than a dual-mode shooting/melee weapon, cleaner and more lore-fit. **Tepok's Blessing** (MR 1) reflects the spawning under the wisest of the Old Ones. Predator's Scent deliberately *not* propagated — Chargers are fanatical chargers, not opportunistic hunters.
