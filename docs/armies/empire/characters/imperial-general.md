# Imperial General


> The army's commander — an Elector Count, senior noble, or veteran general of the Imperial military. Drilled at the Imperial War College or risen through the ranks of State Troops; the rally point for an army under pressure. Combat-capable but not the army's killer — his job is Res aura, tactical command, and a shouted instruction at the right moment that turns a lost flank into a counter-charge. Typically fights mounted on a Warhorse; the very richest field a Pegasus or even an Imperial Griffon.

- **Special rules:**
  - **Inspiring Presence (12")** *(intrinsic — see §8)* — friendly Empire units within 12" of the General treat their Res as the General's (10). The army's command anchor; the rule fires by his mere presence on the field
  - **Tactical Master** *(identity rule)* — at the start of each friendly turn, the General nominates one friendly Empire unit within **18"**. Until end of that turn: (a) the nominated unit may **reroll its charge-distance roll** (rerolls the dice once, takes the new result); (b) the nominated unit treats its **Res as 10** (if lower) for stress thresholds. Represents the General's tactical voice cutting through the noise of battle — a single regiment redirected at the right moment

> **Lore notes:** Stats follow heft calibration — WS 6 (matches WFB reference), S 4, T 4, I 6, A 4 unchanged. **W 3 → 4** Lord-tier bump, capped well below Saurus Oldblood W 7 — humans don't grow Lord-durability the way Saurus do. **Res 9 → 10** for the army's central command character (matches our top-Res convention; Inspiring Presence fires at full 12" buff radius). Combined 3+ save via heavy armour + shield baseline; great weapon trades save for damage. **Inspiring Presence (12")** intrinsic — the General is the army's nominated General by default; baseline §8 rule applies. **Tactical Master** is the identity rule: nominate one Empire unit within 18", that unit gets charge-distance reroll AND treats Res as 10 until end of turn. The Res-as-10 effect extends Inspiring Presence's buff to 18" for the chosen unit (vs 12" baseline) — useful when an Empire unit operates outside the General's normal aura. Charge-distance reroll captures "tactical timing" — the General's word turns a marginal charge into a connection. Points **150** base — Lord-tier pricing between Saurus Oldblood (260, Saurus apex with NA 2+ and W 7) and Skink High Chief (170, Skink Lord-tier with army-wide Skink shooting buff). Magic-item budget 100 pts matches Saurus Oldblood; BSB option +25 pts per universal convention.
