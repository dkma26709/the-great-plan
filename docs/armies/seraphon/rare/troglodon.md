# Troglodon


> A cave-dwelling blind saurian predator — eye-less, venom-sacked, and patient. The Troglodon hunts not by sight but by scent, vibration, and chemo-senses, stalking prey through darkness and ambushing from cover. Where a Carnosaur roars and charges, a Troglodon waits. Where a Bastiladon sits armoured, a Troglodon fades into broken terrain until the moment of the strike. A Skink Oracle rides the howdah-saddle on its back, drawing sacred portents from the beast's throat-rumblings and directing the venom-spit at chosen prey.

*Oracle shares the Troglodon's M (rides the howdah-saddle). A Skink priest-figure, support profile per §1 Mixed Unit rules. Has a light melee capability for self-defence and a thrown javelin — see weapon profiles.*

**Points:** **260** per bundle (Troglodon + Oracle)

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurian, Monster, Rare (Troglodon); Lizardmen, Skink, Arcane Vassal, Infantry, Rare (Oracle)
- **Unit type:** Mixed Unit (Monster + Skink Oracle support, per §1)
- **Unit composition:** 1 Troglodon + 1 Oracle (fixed)
- **Natural Armour:** 4+ (thick cave-dwelling hide)
- **Equipment:**
  - Troglodon: Jaws + Talons (natural melee) + Noxious Spittle (ranged venom)
  - Oracle: Moonstone Javelin + hand weapon
- **Command options:** none (single-beast unit)
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Terror** (see §8)
  - **Predatory Fighter** (see §8) — Troglodon natural attacks trigger extra attacks on unmodified 6s to hit
  - **Poisoned Attacks** (see §8) — baked into Troglodon's Jaws, Talons, and Noxious Spittle (not into Oracle weapons; the Oracle's Moonstone Javelin carries its own Poisoned Attacks per Armoury)
  - Mixed Unit (see §1)
  - **Strider (Forest)** (see §8) — cave-dweller moves easily through undergrowth
  - **Ambushers** (see §8) — held in reserve off-table, rolls to arrive from Turn 2 onward. The blind hunter does not begin the battle in formation; it emerges from cover or pool-mouth when the tempo is right
  - **Sightless Hunter** — the Troglodon hunts by scent, vibration, and chemo-senses rather than sight. When this unit makes **ranged attacks**, it **ignores cover-based to-hit penalties** and **ignores the Chameleon special rule** on its target. Smoke, foliage, obscuring terrain, and colour-shifting scales are all irrelevant to a creature that senses prey through heat and ground-tremor. *Applies to Troglodon ranged attacks (Noxious Spittle) only — the Skink Oracle's javelin uses normal sight and is affected by cover and Chameleon normally.*
  - **Arcane Vassal** *(keyword — Skink Oracle)* — the Skink Oracle bears the Arcane Vassal keyword. A friendly Slann may cast spells through the Oracle's model position (see Slann's Arcane Vassal rule for the full mechanic). The Skink Oracle is the cave-mounted variant of the Skink Priest's relay role.
  - **Sotek's Wailing Cry** *(unit identity rule)* — at the start of each friendly turn, while this Troglodon is alive and not Broken, all friendly **Lizardmen** units with any model within **12"** of the Troglodon may **remove 1 stress counter** (down to a minimum of 0). Lore-direct: "all Lizardmen rally at the wailing cry of the Troglodon, for its eerie call stirs their savagery and proves they have the Old Ones' favour." The eerie throat-rumble carries the Old Ones' favour through the Geomantic Web's last echoes.

**Degrading** *(single-tier, per the Monster-scale convention)*:

> **Lore notes:** A blind cave-dwelling saurian — eye-less, venom-sacked, patient. **Sightless Hunter** is the identity: a predator that doesn't need eyes to kill, hunting by scent, vibration, and chemo-senses. Its ranged Noxious Spittle ignores cover and Chameleon; smoke, foliage, and colour-shifting scales are irrelevant to a creature that senses prey through heat and ground-tremor. **Ambushers** replaces a deploy entry: it emerges from caves and pools mid-battle, not on the start line. **Sotek's Wailing Cry** is the morale anchor — friendly Lizardmen within 12" remove a stress at the start of each turn, lore-direct: "all Lizardmen rally at the wailing cry of the Troglodon, for its eerie call stirs their savagery." The Skink Oracle bears the **Arcane Vassal** keyword (Slann can cast through them, the cave-mounted Skink-Priest relay).
