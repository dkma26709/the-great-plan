# Vampire Lord


> Centuries-old, unliving, mind-bending, warp-fast, sword-master, necromancer. The apex of a vampire bloodline — typically Von Carstein in our reference but can carry any bloodline flavour via Bloodline Powers selection. Combines **Lord-tier combat killer** and **Lord-tier wizard** in one model: WS 7 / S 5 / T 5 / W 5 / I 7 swordwork, plus Lvl 2-4 Wizard with Death + Necromancy + Shadow lore access intrinsic. Bloodline Powers customise the vampire's flavour and combat style — Red Fury (cascade-strike Carstein), Quickblood (always-first Strigoi), Forbidden Lore (knowledge-hoarder Lahmian), etc. Vampiric Hunger lets him heal a wound per turn from blood drunk in melee.

*Lvl 2 caster baseline (Lore Access 2 — Basic + Intermediate spell access). May upgrade to Lvl 3 or Lvl 4 at list-build, +35 pts per step. **Lores Known: Death + Necromancy + Shadow** (intrinsic three-lore access; no list-build choice). Each lore contributes Signature spell free + 1 additional spell from any of the three lores per the wizard's normal spell-pick rules (see §11). With **Forbidden Lore** Bloodline Power, lore access expands to all 8 generic lores + Necromancy.*

**Points:** **280** base (Lvl 2, gut-plate-equivalent armour + hand weapon, no Bloodline Powers). **+35** to Lvl 3 / **+70** to Lvl 4. Bloodline Powers add their listed costs (no hard cap on count).

- **Special rules:**
  - Disciplined (see §8)
  - **Undead** (see §8)
  - **Wizard (Lore Access 2 / 3 / 4 by level, intrinsic dual-lore — Death + Necromancy + Shadow)** — see Magic Profile. Lores Known: Death + Necromancy + Shadow (all three intrinsic; no list-build choice). Sig + 1 additional from any of the three lores = 4 spells known total. *(With Forbidden Lore Bloodline Power: lore access expands to all 8 generic lores + Necromancy.)*
  - **Vampiric Hunger** *(intrinsic universal vampire trait)* — see Faction Mechanics. Causing a wound in melee → regen 1 W (capped 1 W/turn)
  - **Necromantic Anchor** *(identity rule)* — at the end of each Combat phase, friendly **Undead** units with any model within **12"** of the Vampire Lord **Crumble for 1 less wound** (the conversion of stress over Res to wounds is reduced by 1, minimum 0). Effect applies **per unit** within range — multiple Undead units in the aura each benefit. Represents the Vampire Lord as a stabilising node in the necromantic web — his presence reinforces the binding magic, slowing dissolution under sustained pressure. Anchor effects from multiple Vampire characters do **not stack** — a unit caught in overlapping Vampire Lord and Vampire Hero auras receives -1 wound reduction, not -2 (cap at -1 from any combination of Anchor sources)

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Hand weapon** *(default, 1H with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 4** | S 5 | AP -1 | D 1 — vampire-grade swordwork; AP -1 reflects centuries of practice and supernatural strength `[1H Blade]`
- **Great weapon** *(option, 2H)* — 1" reach | A 4 | S+2 (= S 7) | AP -2 | D 1 | Two-Handed `[2H]`
- **Lance** *(option, mounted only)* — 2" reach | A 4 | charge: S+2, AP -2, D 2; subsequent: S+0, AP 0, D 1 `[1H Lance]`
- **Bite** *(natural, always available — once per melee round)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 5 | AP -1 | D 1 — vampire's iconic fanged strike; resolved at the Vampire's I (no I bonus or penalty); does *not* trigger Red Fury or other "Vampire's weapon" effects — it's a per-round bonus attack tied to the Vampire's nature, not a standard weapon. **Feeding trigger:** an unsaved wound caused by this Bite triggers **Vampiric Hunger** (regen 1 W, capped 1 W/turn — see Faction Mechanics). The Bite is the Vampire's only feeding mechanism; sword-strikes draw blood, only the fang drinks `[Natural]`

Heavy armour + shield default 3+ save; great weapon swap drops to 4+. **Magic Profile** is the level ladder — Lvl 2 baseline (Lore Access 2) at Cast Base 2 / Dispel Base 1 / Channelling 2 / Cast Bonus +1 / Dispel Bonus +0 / Dispel Range 18", upgradable to Lvl 3 (Lore Access 3) or Lvl 4 (Lore Access 4) for +35 pts each. Lvl 4 Vampire Lord matches Wizard Lord Lvl 4 magic stats nearly exactly — both reach Lore Access 4 Master tier; Vampire Lord's edge is **three intrinsic lores** (Death + Necromancy + Shadow) vs Wizard Lord's single-Wind. With Forbidden Lore the Vampire Lord exceeds even Slann breadth on generic Winds (8 generics + Necromancy), at lower Cast Base / Dispel Base / Channelling / Dispel Range than Slann. **Vampiric Hunger** intrinsic + **Bloodline Powers** menu = the vampire's identity expression: the universal trait + customised power layer. Compares to Saurus Oldblood (260 pts, NA 2+, Predatory Fighter, Primeval Command 18" Res aura, no magic): Vampire Lord 280 pts, no NA but 3+ worn save + magic items + Bloodline Powers, Lvl 2-4 Wizard with multi-lore — different mechanical fingerprint, both Lord-tier slot. **Bite weapon profile** is the iconic vampire flair — once per melee round, S 5 AP -1 D 1, tied to the model's nature rather than equipment (so it applies regardless of weapon swaps). Doesn't trigger Red Fury (avoids cascading-loop concerns).
