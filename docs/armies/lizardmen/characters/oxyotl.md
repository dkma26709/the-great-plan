# Oxyotl


> **He That Hunts Unseen** — last and greatest Chameleon Hunter. Spawned at Pahuax before the Great Catastrophe, alive when Chaos first came to the mortal world. Lured into the Realm of Chaos through Mage-Lord Pocaxalan's collapsing ritual; survived seven thousand mortal years in that hellish unreality, daubing himself in the blood of his Flesh Hound pursuers to mask his scent, witnessing the fate of all the races should Chaos prevail. Returned to Lustria in 2517 IC, his arrival heralding the first new Chameleon Skink spawning in millennia. Now wages a war of revenge against the Dark Gods — Daemons time after time arrive to find Oxyotl already ensconced nearby, a hidden nuisance that thwarts their immortal plans. Killed Tzara'riador the Shimmering, Lord of Change, in the Pit of Sorrows mid-summoning; killed a Black 13 Skaven Assassin sent for Mazdamundi despite all its skills, magical protections and hyper-stimulants. Wields the Golden Blowpipe of P'Toohee — with the lightest exhalation, blasts forth a hail of murderous darts.

**Points:** **235** base (intrinsic regalia included; no upgrades available)

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurian, Skink, Chameleon, Character, Hero, Named
- **Unit type:** Infantry (Character, Skirmishers — coherency 2"); foot only
- **Natural Armour:** 6+ (Chameleon scales)
- **Ward Save:** —
- **Equipment (intrinsic — cannot be removed or swapped):** Golden Blowpipe of P'Toohee, Celestite Dagger
- **Mount/Equipment options:** none (named character)
- **Special rules:**
  - **Skink species package** — Disciplined, Cold-Blooded, Aquatic, Skirmisher, Slippery
  - **Scouts** (deploys outside **12"** of enemy units)
  - **Loner** (does not attach to non-Chameleon-Skink units)
  - **Chameleon** (-1 to hit ranged at >12"; melee chargers also -1 to hit)
  - **Telescopic Eyes** — Oxyotl's ranged attacks have the **Sniper** keyword baseline
  - **He That Hunts Unseen** *(identity rule — perfected camouflage)* — Oxyotl **cannot be targeted by ranged attacks at any range** unless the firing model is within **12"** of him. Stronger than the Stalker's Perfect Stillness — applies regardless of movement, denied only at point-blank. Lore-direct: "all but invisible, unnoticed even by Daemons of the Realm of Chaos."
  - **Daemon-Hunter** *(identity rule — anti-Chaos specialist)* — Oxyotl has **Hatred (Daemon)** for the entire battle. His ranged and melee attacks gain **+1 AP and +1 to wound** against units with the **Daemon** keyword. Lore-direct: "war of revenge against the fell powers; the Daemons arrive to find Oxyotl already nearby."
  - **Daemon-Sense** *(identity rule — pre-positioned awareness)* — when an enemy unit with the **Daemon** keyword arrives onto the battlefield (via summoning, reserves, ambush, deep strike, or any other delayed-deployment effect), Oxyotl may immediately make **one free shooting attack** with his Golden Blowpipe at that unit, regardless of phase or his prior actions. Resolves before the arriving unit may act. Lore-direct: "Daemons arrive to find Oxyotl already ensconced nearby."
  - **Walked the Realm of Chaos** *(mental fortitude)* — Oxyotl is **Immune to Psychology** and **Immune to Fear / Terror**. He has glimpsed the fate of the world should Chaos prevail; nothing on the mortal plane will unsettle him.
  - **The Hidden Hunt** *(character shift — blanket, per §9.3)* — while Oxyotl is in your list, **all Chameleon Skinks units count as Core** instead of Special. The greatest Chameleon Hunter draws every hunt-pack into a shadow vanguard at the army's leading edge — the chameleon-caste, ordinarily a specialist sniper unit, becomes the army's natural rank-and-file vanguard wholesale while Oxyotl directs them.

> **Lore notes:** *He That Hunts Unseen* — last and greatest Chameleon Hunter, the anti-Chaos specialist who survived seven thousand mortal years in the Realm of Chaos and emerged daubed in Flesh Hound blood. **He That Hunts Unseen** is the marquee identity: cannot be targeted by ranged attacks unless the firer is within 12" — invisible-even-to-Daemons, lore-direct. **Daemon-Hunter** is the anti-Chaos specialisation (Hatred plus +1 AP / +1 to wound vs Daemon). **Daemon-Sense** captures "Daemons arrive to find Oxyotl already ensconced nearby" — when a Daemon unit arrives via summoning, reserves, ambush, or any other delayed-deployment effect, Oxyotl shoots it for free before it acts. **Walked the Realm of Chaos** is full Imm Psych + Imm Fear/Terror — he has glimpsed the fate of the world should Chaos prevail; nothing on the mortal plane unsettles him. The **Golden Blowpipe of P'Toohee** ("with the lightest exhalation, blasts forth a hail of murderous darts") is meaningfully stronger than the standard Dartpipe — 18" range, Multiple Shots (3), Magical Attacks, Sniper.
