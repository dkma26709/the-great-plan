# Tehenhauin


> The Prophet of Sotek, Red Crested chosen of the Serpent God. From a humble Skink Priest of Tlaxtlan, Tehenhauin rose to prominence by interpreting omens that foretold the coming of the Skaven plague-ravagers — and by leading the southern temple-cities in their first true war of revenge against ratkind. Sotek's mark blazes upon his brow; serpents coil around his arms and shoulders, untroubled by his presence; jungle swarms slither in his wake at his bidding. Tehenhauin's words can ignite a Skink host into a frenzy of righteous slaughter — his presence on the battlefield is a religious event in its own right. Wields the Blade of the Serpent's Tongue (its edge envenomed with the spittle of Sotek itself) and bears the Plaque of Sotek (a sliver of the original Sotek-glyph from the Ziggurat of Tlaxtlan, which turns enemy strikes aside).
