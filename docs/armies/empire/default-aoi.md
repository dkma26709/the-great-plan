# Default AoI:


> The classic Empire of Man army — disciplined State Troops drawn from the Electoral provinces, professional Knightly Orders providing shock cavalry, Wizards from the Colleges of Magic supporting from the rear, and gunpowder artillery hammering the enemy line at range. The combined-arms doctrine is everything: Halberdiers anchor the centre, Knights flank, Handgunners and Crossbowmen thin the approach, the Cannon shells priority targets, and Wizards channel a Wind to swing the decisive moment. No single unit wins an Imperial battle; the *army* wins through coordinated fire and disciplined positioning.

**Composition Table** *(default constraints per §9.1)*:

| Category | Constraint |
|---|---|
| Lords | Maximum 25% of army points |
| Heroes | Maximum 25% of army points |
| Core | Minimum 25% of army points |
| Special | Maximum 50% of army points |
| Rare | Maximum 25% of army points |

**Category Assignments:**

| Category | Units |
|---|---|
| **Lords** | Imperial General, Wizard Lord |
| **Heroes** | Captain of the Empire, Battle Wizard |
| **Core** | State Troops — Halberdiers, State Troops — Handgunners, State Troops — Swordsmen, State Troops — Spearmen, State Troops — Crossbowmen |
| **Special** | Imperial Knights, Greatswords, Great Cannon |
| **Rare** | Demigryph Knights |

**Army-wide rules:** None. (Themed AoIs may add 0-2 rules — e.g., a *Reiksguard Crusade* AoI might grant Hatred (Chaos) army-wide, a *Hochland Forester* AoI might grant Strider (Forest) on Core units.)

**Mandatory picks:** None.

**Banned picks:** None.

**Magic lore access:** Battle Wizards and Wizard Lords access **all 8 general lores** at list-build (Light, Heavens, Beasts, Life, Fire, Death, Metal, Shadow) — the Empire's Colleges of Magic are deliberately broad. Each individual Wizard channels one Wind only, fixed at list-build per the wizard's College affiliation. The Empire's structural advantage is **breadth of Wind selection across the army**, not per-wizard versatility.

**Named-hero access:** No named characters drafted under §14 yet; templates pending.

**Faction-specific army-build hooks:** None faction-wide. Vow-style or Spawning-style mechanics absent — Empire's army-build identity is the **State Troops Disciplined trait + General/BSB aura economy**, not a layered upgrade menu.
