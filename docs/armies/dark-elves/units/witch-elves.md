# Witch Elves

> The frenzied female fanatics of Khaine, the Dark Elf god of murder. Witch Elves drink from the Cauldron of Blood at their dread temples — a ritual that grants them ecstatic frenzy and the favour of their bloody god. They go to battle bare-fleshed under ritual scarification, dual-bladed and poison-coated, screaming hymns to Khaine as they slaughter. Where their kin the Asur are disciplined phalanx-fighters, the Druchii Witch Elves are the *opposite* — wild, chaos-of-violence, every blow lethally precise but every choice driven by bloodlust rather than tactics. They are Khaine's chosen daughters, blessed with supernatural reflexes (I 6 — the fastest non-character infantry in the cross-faction set), Frenzy that compels them forward, and poison-coated dual blades that wound on rolls others cannot match. Their weakness: T 3 / W 1 / no-armour-baseline glass-cannon profile. Witch Elves break enemy lines or break themselves; there is no middle outcome. **Hatred (High Elves)** is iconic Druchii — the millennia-old grudge between the sundered elven kin. **Daughters of Khaine** captures the bloodlust mechanic: every kill brings ecstatic certainty, washing fear away.

## Special Rules

- **Elven Grace** *(faction-wide for elves — Ward 6+ in melee vs attacks at the elf's I or lower; see §8 Defence)*. At I 6, Witch Elves trigger the Ward against almost everything in the game (only some apex elf-tier characters and a handful of monsters reach I 7+).
- **Murderous Prowess** *(faction-wide for Druchii — reroll to-Wound 1s in melee; see §8 Combat)*. The Druchii lethality variant of the elf-prowess pair, parallel to Asur Martial Prowess.
- **Frenzy** *(see §8)* — Witch Elves are intrinsically Frenzied: +1 Attack on weapon profiles, must charge nearest visible enemy if able, cannot voluntarily Disengage from melee, Immune to Psychology. The Cauldron of Blood ritual; they cannot stop fighting once started.
- **Poisoned Attacks** *(see §8)* — Witch Elf melee attacks gain **+1 to-Wound**. Khainite blades carry the venom of a dozen ritual mixtures, distilled at temple altars; the poison wounds easier regardless of the target's size or toughness.
- **Hatred (High Elves)** *(see §8)* — Witch Elves gain Hatred (reroll failed to-Hit in first round of combat) against units with the **High Elf** keyword. The millennia-old grudge between the sundered elven kin, expressed mechanically as a reroll against Asur specifically.
- **Daughters of Khaine** *(Witch Elves unit identity rule)* — at the **end of each Combat phase** in which this unit killed at least one enemy model, the unit **removes 1 stress**. Khaine's blessing — every kill brings ecstatic certainty, washing fear away. The unit thrives on slaughter; failing to kill in a round means no relief, but a successful round of bloodletting clears any combat-wound stress accumulated. Distinct from Greenskin Loves a Scrap (stress relief on engagement, no kill required) — Daughters of Khaine is conditional on actually drawing blood.
