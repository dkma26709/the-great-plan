# Vampire *(Hero)*


> A junior member of a vampire bloodline — newly turned (a few decades to a century or two old) or a less-prominent lieutenant of a Lord. Combat-skilled but not at apex; Lvl 1 Wizard baseline with Necromancy access. Fights in front of skeleton lines, leads cavalry charges, scouts ahead of the main necromantic core. The Hero-tier vampire carries the universal Vampiric Hunger + can purchase Bloodline Powers (typically 1-2 at list-build budget).

*Lvl 1 caster baseline (Lore Access 1 — Basic spell access only). May upgrade to Lvl 2 at list-build for +30 pts. **Lores Known: Necromancy** intrinsic (single-lore — Vampire Heroes have less arcane breadth than Lord-tier vampires). Signature spell + 1 additional spell from Necromancy = 2 spells known. With **Forbidden Lore** Bloodline Power, lore access expands to all 8 generic lores + Necromancy.*

**Points:** **125** base (Lvl 1, heavy armour + shield + hand weapon, no Bloodline Powers). **+30** to Lvl 2 (= 155 pts). Bloodline Powers add listed costs.
