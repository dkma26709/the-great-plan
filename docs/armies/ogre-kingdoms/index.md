# Ogre Kingdoms — Test Profiles


> **Design principle:** Cross-faction demonstration roster — second non-Lizardmen faction after Empire. Where Empire stress-tested rank-and-flank human infantry, gunpowder, cavalry, war machines, and multi-Wind magic, Ogres stress-test the **Monstrous-Infantry-as-whole-army** archetype: small elite units (5-9 models per block), every model Kroxigor-adjacent in scale and pricing, faction-specific armour (gut-plate), a unique faction lore (Lore of the Great Maw — pending separate §11 draft), and the absence of inspirational-leadership tropes. See `cross-faction-reference-notes.md` at project root for design-prep research.

> **Heft calibration note for Ogres.** Ogres are **fat brutes** — wounds are the Ogre identity, more than Toughness or armour. Per design call (2026-04-25 session), **no artificial wound ceiling applies**; unit identity drives the wound count, not abstract tier caps. **Pass 4 (2026-05-01) species-identity W ladder:**
> - **Bulls W 7** — rank-and-file baseline (Pass 4 bump from W6, capturing Ogre 10ft / ~700lb mass per the §1.5 species reference)
> - **Ironguts W 7** — parity with Bulls; elite distinction expresses through **better save (5+ via gut-plate + light armour intrinsic)**, **better Res (8 vs 7)**, **mandatory great weapons**, and the **Bone Crackers identity rule** — gear and combat skill differentiate elite from rank-and-file, not wound count
> - **Bruiser W 9** — Hero-tier; +2 above Bull baseline
> - **Slaughtermaster W 10** — Lord-caster; spiritual leader, mid-tier between Bruiser and Tyrant
> - **Tyrant W 12** — Lord-combat apex; +5 above Bull baseline (parity with Saurus W3→W8 Oldblood gap), most physically massive Lord-tier infantry character in the ruleset
>
> The **Kroxigor regular is W 8 in §10** (Pass 2 bump per "twenty-three crossbow bolts to fell" lore), with **Kroxigor Ancient W 10** (Hero apex). Ogres now exceed Saurus at every tier on raw W: Bull W7 vs Saurus W3, Bruiser W9 vs Scar-Veteran W6, Tyrant W12 vs Oldblood W8. Lore-coherent — Ogres are physically larger than Saurus.
>
> **Res stays at WFB reference values (no +1 bump)** — Ogres are brutal, not stoic; might-makes-right tribal culture doesn't translate to drilled-regiment morale resilience. WS / S / T / I / A close to reference. **Save ceiling 5+ across the roster** — no heavy armour anywhere; Ogre durability comes from **wounds + Toughness 4-5**, not armour. Compares to Saurus Oldblood's biological NA 2+: Ogres absorb hits with bulk; Saurus deflect them with hide.

> **Resolve note for Ogres.** Ogres do *not* lead like Empire. There is no innate Inspiring Presence on Ogre characters — the Tyrant does not buff allied Res through charisma. Authority in the Mountains of Mourn is established through single-combat dominance ("guts-out" fights in the Maw-pit, where the loser is consumed by the winner). The mechanical translation: Ogre character abilities operate on **combat axes** (extra attacks, no-fall-back enforcement, personal kills) rather than morale axes. **No Ogre character provides Res bumps, rerolls, or Inspiring Presence to other units.**

> **Predatory Fighter is reserved to Lizardmen.** Predatory Fighter (extra attacks on unmodified 6 to hit, see §8) is a **Saurus-caste signature mechanic** per design call (2026-04-25 session). Reserved to Lizardmen units (and very specific other cases where lore strongly justifies). Ogre characters do *not* have Predatory Fighter, even at apex tier — the Tyrant's personal combat dominance expresses through **Mighty Blow (1)** (+1 S in first round, see §8) plus his iconic **Headbutt** weapon profile.

> **Gut-plate convention.** Every Ogre wears a gut-plate — a strapped-on bronze chest-and-belly plate, often etched with tribal sigils. Mechanically, gut-plate functions as a **shield-equivalent** (one save step) but is **hands-free** — two-handed weapons preserve the gut-plate save (unlike Empire shields, which forbid two-handed weapons). Gut-plate is intrinsic to Ogre units and stacks with worn armour per §8 Armour Save Stacking, one step per source. Default Ogre with gut-plate alone = **6+** save; +light armour = **5+**. **No heavy armour anywhere in the Ogre roster.**
