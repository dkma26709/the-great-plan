# Doom Diver Catapult

> A wooden launching contraption manned by two Goblin engineers, with one further Goblin strapped into a crude wood-and-canvas glider — the *Doom Diver*. The Crew hauls back the catapult arm, the Doom Diver Goblin is launched into the air, and the Goblin then **steers mid-flight** using flapping arms, tilting feet, and shrill screams of effort, gliding toward an enemy unit before crashing into a chosen victim at speed. The Doom Diver's identity is *guided indirect fire*: long-range (12-60"), no line of sight required, with the Goblin's mid-flight steering reducing scatter by the Crew's BS — a fully-aimed shot lands more precisely than a Trebuchet stone, though the catapult mechanism's reliability is still Goblin engineering (Misfire on the Artillery Die). On a landed hit, the Doom Diver delivers **S 5 / AP -3 / D D3 (Multiple Wounds)** to the model his arc takes him to — devastating against Monsters, Lord-tier characters, and any single high-value target. The Greenskin battlefield read: deploy a Doom Diver and assign it to whichever enemy character or Monster needs killing, accept the 1/6-per-shot Misfire risk, and trust the Goblin's steering to bring the glider down on something important.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **No Animosity** *(War Machine exception, per §8 Animosity)*. The Doom Diver Catapult does not roll Animosity — the rule's force-charge sub-result requires movement actions the unit cannot perform, and the Crew is occupied with loading/aiming/launching.
- **War Machine** *(unit type — see §14 convention)* — single-profile unit with dual T. Ranged incoming: T 6. Melee incoming: T 3. W is a wound pool — wounds accumulate but do not reduce combat output or fire rate. The Catapult fires at full effectiveness from W=full down to W=1; the engine goes silent only at W=0 (destroyed). Can be charged normally. Cannot itself Charge.
- **Crew attack in melee** — Crew fight with hand weapons at unified MA 2 MD 3, I 3, **A 2** baseline. Melee A is fixed and does not scale with remaining wounds. BS 3 (Goblin baseline) — affects the Doom Diver Projectile's scatter-reduction.
- **Stab in the Back** *(Goblin-typical, applies to Crew melee attacks only)*. +1 to Wound against rear-arc *(melee only)* or Shaken/Wavering targets. Does not apply to the Doom Diver Projectile — the projectile is mid-flight indirect-fire chaos, not Goblin melee opportunism.
- **No Cowardly Numbers / No Loves a Scrap / No Mushroom Madness** *(War Machine convention)*. The Doom Diver Catapult is a single-model unit; species-frame sub-tribe rules do not apply.

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Hand Weapon** *(Crew melee)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — fixed melee attack count, does not decrement with wounds `[1H Blade]`

### Ranged

**Doom Diver Projectile mechanic** *(new ranged weapon rule — Doom Diver Catapult specifically; mid-flight-guided indirect-fire single-target attack)*:

1. Declare a target point within the Doom Diver's range (**12-60"**). The target point may be anywhere within range — **no line of sight required** (the Catapult lobs the Doom Diver up and over; the Goblin glides down). The target point need not be in the unit's forward arc, but conventionally is.
2. Roll the **Scatter Die** + **Artillery Die** for scatter (per §8 Scatter — same convention as Trebuchet Stone Thrower):
   - **HIT** (Scatter Die) — no scatter; the Doom Diver lands exactly on the declared point.
   - **Arrow** (Scatter Die) — the Doom Diver scatters the **Artillery Die distance** in the indicated direction. **Subtract the Crew's BS (3)** from the scatter distance, minimum 0. *(This is the Goblin's mid-flight guidance — the strapped-in Doom Diver actively steers, reducing the off-target distance by his BS.)*
   - **MISFIRE** (Artillery Die) — the launch fails. No Doom Diver projectile delivered. Roll **D6** on the Doom Diver Misfire Table (below).
3. After scatter resolves: identify the **model whose base is closest to the final landing point** (the Doom Diver lands on this model — could be friend or foe, depending on where the scatter pushed him). If no model is within 3" of the final landing point, the Doom Diver crashes into open ground and is wasted (no damage).
4. The identified model takes a **single hit** at:
   - **S 5** (Goblin + glider mass colliding at glide speed)
   - **AP -3** (the dive concentrates impact on a single point, punching through plate)
   - **D D3 (Multiple Wounds D3)** — each successful unsaved wound inflicts D3 wounds (with the standard MW cap — excess wounds beyond the model's W pool are wasted). Lethal vs Monsters, Lord-tier characters, and any high-W target.
5. The Doom Diver Goblin himself is removed from play after the dive — he is the ammunition; he doesn't survive the impact. The catapult Crew strap a fresh Doom Diver into the harness during the next Movement phase (lore-only — mechanically the next shot fires normally).

**Doom Diver Misfire Table** (D6, when the Artillery Die produces MISFIRE):

- **1** — Catastrophic launch failure. The Doom Diver glider catches fire on launch; the Catapult destroyed. The unit is removed (all remaining wounds lost). The strapped-in Goblin also dies (no surprise).
- **2-3** — Winch snapped or rope frayed. Cannot fire next Shooting phase. **D3 wounds** inflicted on the unit (no Ward, no Armour). The Doom Diver Goblin is unharmed but cannot launch.
- **4-5** — Catapult jammed (timber slipped, lever stuck). Cannot fire next Shooting phase. No wounds taken. The Doom Diver Goblin is unharmed.
- **6** — Goblin lost his nerve mid-flight; gliding short. Doom Diver lands at the declared target's HALF distance, scatter resolved normally from the half-distance point. Catapult operable next phase.

**Per-Shooting-phase limit:** One Doom Diver launched per Shooting phase. Crew must be alive (W > 0). The Catapult may not fire on the turn it has moved (any non-zero distance) — repositioning takes the full phase.

- **Doom Diver Projectile** *(ranged, indirect-fire mid-flight-guided single-target; Crew launches once per Shooting phase)* — **12-60" range** | **Indirect Fire** (no line of sight required) | **Scatter (Scatter Die + Artillery Die)** with **BS 3 scatter-reduction** | **S 5** | **AP -3** | **D D3 (Multiple Wounds D3)** | Misfire on Artillery Die Misfire — see Doom Diver Projectile mechanic above for full resolution.
