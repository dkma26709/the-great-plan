# Kroq-Gar


> The Last Defender of Xhotl — an ancient Saurus Oldblood, the last remaining Saurus warrior from the now-ruined temple-city of Xhotl. His spawning produced a handful of Saurus, each marked by Xhotl for greatness, with instinctive battle-cunning unsurpassed by any of their kind and an innate dominion over the native beasts of the jungle. Kroq-Gar's spawning coincided with the rearing of a brood of mighty Carnosaurs; he claimed the largest and most ferocious as his own. **Grimloq** has served him since, an ageless Carnosaur in perfect lockstep with his rider — not a wild beast bound by fury, but a coordinated extension of Kroq-Gar's command. Mazdamundi gifted Kroq-Gar with the Hand of Gods and tasked him with exterminating those creatures not part of the Great Plan. There is no continent on which Kroq-Gar and Grimloq have not fought.

*Standard Carnosaur character mount profile, with one identity-change: Grimloq does **not** have Bloodlust (Kroq-Gar maintains perfect command; the Carnosaur is in lockstep, not berserker fury). In place of Bloodlust, Grimloq has **+1 Attack on Massive Jaws** (A 3 → A 4) — the bite lands more often because rider and mount are perfectly synchronised. Apex Predator, Pinned Prey, Pursuit Predator, Bloodroar, Locked Jaws, Predatory Fighter all preserved. Combined wound pool W14 per §1 *Combined Wounds*.*

**Points:** **700** base (intrinsic equipment + Grimloq mount + 3 baked-in spawnings + Top Commander aura included; no upgrades available)

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurian, Saurus, Character, Lord, Named
- **Unit type:** Monster-mount (Saurus Oldblood rider + Carnosaur mount, single model per §1 *Rider and Mount Profiles*)
- **Combined wound pool:** **W14** — max(rider 8, mount 10) = 10 + rider/2 = 4 → W14, per §1 *Combined Wounds*
- **Natural Armour:** **2+** (NA 3+ Oldblood baseline + Light Armour intrinsic, combined save 2+; lore-direct "Skink-attendant bronze plates")
- **Ward Save:** — (none baseline)
- **Equipment (intrinsic — cannot be removed or swapped):** Revered Spear of Tlanxla, Hand of Gods, Light Armour, Saurus Jaws (natural)
- **Mount/Equipment options:** none (named character)
- **Special rules:**
  - **Saurus Oldblood baseline package** — Disciplined, Cold-Blooded, Stubborn, Predatory Fighter, Fear, Locked Jaws (all inherited from generic Saurus Oldblood, see §10 *Saurus Oldblood*)
  - **Primeval Command — Top Commander variant** *(replaces standard Primeval Command)* — friendly **Lizardmen** units (Saurus, Skinks, Kroxigor, Saurian beasts — broader scope than the generic Oldblood's Saurus-only) with any model within **18"** of Kroq-Gar gain **+1 Res** for stress threshold purposes. Additionally, at the start of any friendly turn, Kroq-Gar nominates one friendly Lizardmen unit within 18"; that unit immediately removes **2 stress counters**. Captures Kroq-Gar's lore-direct role as Mazdamundi's top field commander — leading not just Saurus but the entire Lizardmen host across continents.
  - **Carnosaur baseline package (Grimloq)** — Apex Predator, Pinned Prey, Pursuit Predator, Bloodroar (12" stress aura at start of Combat phase), Locked Jaws, Predatory Fighter. **Bloodlust deliberately omitted** — Kroq-Gar's command keeps Grimloq in perfect lockstep; replaced by **+1 A on Massive Jaws** (see Grimloq's stats and weapon profile).
  - **Mark of Xhotl** *(intrinsic spawning — applies to both Kroq-Gar AND Grimloq, special named-character rider+mount carve-out)* — both rider and mount have **Predatory Fighter (5+)** instead of (6+). Captures the lore-direct "Sacred Spawning of Xhotl protects both him and his Carnosaur."
  - **Tlanxla** *(intrinsic spawning — rider only)* — Kroq-Gar gains **+1 WS-A** (already encoded as WS-A 8 above). The war-god whose spear he wields adds his blessing to every strike.
  - **Tlazcotl** *(intrinsic spawning — unit-level)* — both Kroq-Gar and Grimloq gain **Immune to Psychology** (see §8). The apex predator and his rider don't register fear, terror, or panic; nothing on the battlefield warrants their concern.
  - **Hand of Gods** *(intrinsic, ranged identity rule)* — **once per Shooting phase**, Kroq-Gar may unleash the Hand of Gods at one enemy unit within **18"** line-of-sight: that unit suffers **2D6 hits at S 5 / AP -2 / D 1 / Magical Attacks / Flaming**. Against units with the **Daemon** keyword, hits are at **S 6 / AP -3 / D D3** instead. Captures the lore-direct "sear the flesh from his enemies' bones... purifying" — anti-Daemon scaled significantly higher.
  - **Doom-Laden Visions** *(intrinsic — Revered Spear of Tlanxla effect)* — when Kroq-Gar inflicts one or more unsaved wounds in melee with the Revered Spear in a Combat phase, the targeted enemy unit immediately gains **+D3 stress**. Lore-direct: "those struck by it are overcome by doom-laden visions that sap their will to fight."
  - **Saurian Apex** *(character shift — blanket, per §9.3)* — while Kroq-Gar is in your list, **all Wild Carnosaur units count as Special** instead of Rare. Kroq-Gar's innate dominion over the native beasts of the jungle (the Spawning of Xhotl gift) draws every apex predator into the host's natural order; in his presence the Wild Carnosaurs are coordinated combat assets, not Slann-leashed compulsion cases.

> **Lore notes:** The Last Defender of Xhotl — apex Saurus combat character, Mazdamundi's top field commander, and rider of **Grimloq** (an ageless Carnosaur in perfect lockstep with him, not a wild beast bound by fury). Kroq-Gar carries three intrinsic spawnings: **Mark of Xhotl** (Predatory Fighter at 5+ for *both* rider and mount — special named-character carve-out), **Tlanxla** (the war-god whose spear he wields, +1 WS-A baked in), and **Tlazcotl** (unit-level Immune to Psychology). Grimloq deliberately *skips* Bloodlust — Kroq-Gar keeps the Carnosaur in perfect command, the wild-fury identity replaced by +1 A on Massive Jaws representing rider-mount synchronisation. **Primeval Command — Top Commander variant** broadens scope from Saurus-only to all-Lizardmen, capturing Kroq-Gar's role leading the entire host across continents. Intrinsic regalia: the **Revered Spear of Tlanxla** (Magical + Flaming + Doom-Laden Visions stress-spike on unsaved wounds) and the **Hand of Gods** (once-per-Shooting-phase 2D6 hits, scaled significantly higher against Daemons — Mazdamundi's anti-Chaos extermination mandate made literal).
