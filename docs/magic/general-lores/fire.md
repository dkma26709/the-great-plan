# Lore of Fire


> Pure destruction. Where Heavens is celestial manipulation and Beasts is feral empowerment, Fire is unrelenting burning — the lore of open destruction. Every damage spell is Flaming. Many spells Remain In Play, leaving the battlefield alight with persistent threats an opponent must commit dispelling power to put out.

**Lore Attribute — Kindled Flame:** when this wizard casts a Fire spell targeting a unit that has **already been targeted by a Fire spell this Magic phase** (by any caster, friendly or enemy), the spell's casting value is reduced by **1**. Rewards pouring fire onto the same target — once something's burning, it's easier to burn it more. Minimum casting value 0.

**Signature Spell — Fireball**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 7 | 24" | Magic Missile |

D6 hits at **S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**. Every Fire wizard can call down a Fireball.

#### Basic (Tier 1) — Lore Access 1+

**Flame Breath**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 5 | 12" | Template (flame) |

Uses the **flame template** (see §7.4 for template mechanics). All models under the template take one hit at **S3, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. A gout of fire from the caster's hands or mouth.

**Ignite** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 6 | 24" | Hex, Remains In Play |

Target enemy unit gains the **Flammable** special rule (see §8) while this spell remains in play. Sets up follow-up Fire spells and any other Flaming attack.

**Piercing Bolts of Burning**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 8 | 24" | Magic Missile |

D6 hits at **S5, AP 0, D1, Flaming, Ward Piercing (1)**. Bolts of fire that cut through magical protection as readily as flesh.

**Flame Cage** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 8 | 24" | Hex, Remains In Play |

Target enemy unit is caged in circling fire. While this spell is in play, whenever the target unit performs any movement action (Advance, March, Charge, Wheel, voluntary Flee, Elusive's reactive move), it suffers **D6 hits at S4, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. The unit is not prevented from moving — it simply pays the price in fire each time it tries.

#### Intermediate (Tier 2) — Lore Access 2+

**Burning Head**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 9 | 18" | Template (small round), Travel |

Pick a point within range and a direction. Place the **small round template** at the aimed point. Roll 2D6 — the template travels that many inches in the chosen direction, resolving in a straight line. Every model whose base is touched by the template's path takes one hit at **S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**. A flaming skull launched like a bowling ball.

**Cascading Fire-Cloak** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 8 | 18" | Augment, Remains In Play |

Target friendly unit is wreathed in defensive fire. While this spell is in play, at the start of each Combat phase, each enemy unit with any model within **3"** of the target suffers **D3 hits at S4, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. Punishes anyone engaging or surrounding the protected unit.

**Molten Strike**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 8 | 18" | Magic Missile |

D3 hits at **S7, AP -3, D2, Flaming**. A concentrated lance of liquid fire — bores through heavy armour and elite infantry alike. The anti-plate answer in the lore's toolkit.

#### Advanced (Tier 3) — Lore Access 3+

**Flame Storm** *(RIP, template, drifting)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 10 | 18" | Template (large round), Remains In Play |

**On cast:** aim a point within range. The template **scatters D3"** in a random direction from the aimed point and lands there — the placement is never perfectly reliable. Every model whose base is under the template's final position suffers **1 hit at S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**.

**Each subsequent controlling-player Magic phase while in play:** the template **moves D6"** in a random direction. Every model whose base is touched by the template at **any point along this movement path** suffers **1 hit at S4, AP -1, D1, Flaming**. If the final resting position would overlap a model not already touched by the movement, shift the template the smallest distance needed to clear those bases (this shift does not produce additional hits).

A firestorm that drifts across the battlefield, lethal to anything in its path. Not escalating — just consistently dangerous each turn until dispelled or burned out.

**Sun's Wrath**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 12 | 36" | Magic Missile |

D6+2 hits at **S6, AP -2, D2, Flaming, Ward Piercing (2)**. The capstone offensive spell — concentrated solar heat called down on a single target. Designed to crack characters, monsters, and heavily warded elite units.

**Conflagration** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 10 | 24" | Hex, Remains In Play |

Target enemy unit becomes the seat of a spreading fire. While this spell is in play, each controlling-player Magic phase the target suffers **D3 hits at S4, AP -1, D2, Flaming**, and every other enemy unit with any model within **3"** of the target at that moment suffers **D3 hits at S3, AP 0, D1, Flaming**. The fire consumes the target and jumps to anything near it.
