# Goblin Wolf Riders

> Common Goblins mounted on Giant Wolves — the open-plain Goblin tribes' answer to the Bretonnian Mounted Yeoman: a fast, cowardly, opportunistic harasser unit that screens the army's flanks, picks off stragglers, looses arrows from a safe distance, and runs the moment something serious comes for them. The Giant Wolves themselves are gaunt, scarred, half-starved animals — bred for speed rather than weight, and held together by the Goblins' habit of beating any wolf that tries to bolt. Where Orc Boar Boyz are heavy shock cavalry (Tusker Charge, S 4 mount with NA 6+ tough hide), Wolf Riders are *light* cavalry — M 9 (one short of the Bretonnian common warhorse), no mount Natural Armour, S 3 wolf bite, and an unbreakable habit of fleeing whenever the player chooses to declare it. Fast Cavalry per §8 — Vanguard, free pivots, Flee reaction. Used well, a Wolf Rider unit harasses an enemy flank for half the game, charges only when the opponent is already breaking (Stab in the Back +1 to Wound vs Shaken/Wavering), and ends the battle alive at half-strength.

## Equipment

- **Equipment (default):** Hand Weapon (rider) + Wolf Bite (mount, natural). No worn armour, no shield, no shortbow — the default Wolf Rider is a bare-Goblin-on-a-Wolf.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines charge / self-hits at the rider's Strength.
- **Stab in the Back** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. **Applies to both melee and ranged attacks.** +1 to Wound against any target being attacked in its rear arc *(melee only)* or currently Shaken or Wavering *(both melee and ranged)*. Does not stack with itself.
- **Cowardly Numbers** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. While this unit has 20 or more models, it is **Stubborn**. Once reduced to fewer than half its starting strength, it suffers **-1 Res** for the rest of the battle. *(Note: at Cavalry unit sizes — typically 5-12 models — the Stubborn-while-20+ clause is functionally dormant. The -1 Res below-half-strength clause still applies and is the relevant Goblin fragility tax.)*
- **No Loves a Scrap** *(Goblin sub-species — see §13)*. Wolf Riders do not inherit the Greenskin faction-wide Loves a Scrap rule.
- **Fast Cavalry** *(see §8)* — Vanguard (deploys up to M outside the deployment zone), free pivots at any point during movement, Flee charge reaction (M + D6 directly away from the charger; +4 stress for the act of Fleeing, no Skirmisher discount). Fast Cavalry does **not** waive the -1 to-hit for moving-and-shooting (per §7.4).

## Options

**Worn armour options:** Light armour (+1 pt/model) → rider 6+ save (no mount NA to stack with — combined save is the rider's worn save alone)

**Worn armour additive:** Shield (+1 pt/model) — combines with armour for one step per source (per §8 Armour Save Stacking)

**Equipment swap options (replace Hand Weapon, pick at most one):**
- **Spear** (+1 pt/model) — 1H, 2" reach, replaces Hand Weapon. Cavalry-charge-spear; on charge round gains +1 S and AP -1 (captures the WAP-canonical "Wolf Rider spear" charge bonus).

**Equipment add-on (ranged):**
- **Shortbow** (+1 pt/model — adds 18" ranged profile, keeps Hand Weapon or Spear for melee). Not available with shield — the bow occupies the off-hand.

**Command options:**
- **Boss (Leader)** (+3 pts) — champion
- **Standard Bearer** (+6 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 25 pts)
- **Musician** (+3 pts)

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Hand Weapon** *(rider, default, 1H — may pair with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — short blade or club. `[1H Blade]`
- **Spear** *(rider, option, 1H — may pair with shield, replaces Hand Weapon)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | S 3 baseline (charge: **S 4, AP -1**) | D 1 — cavalry spear; the 2" reach lets the second rank participate, and the charge bonus delivers a one-time impact strike. `[1H Reach]`
- **Wolf Bite** *(mount, natural — always available)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — Giant Wolf's bite. No AP — the wolf's teeth tear flesh but don't bite through metal. `[Natural]`

### Ranged

- **Shortbow** *(rider, option add-on)* — **18" range** | **A 1** | **S 3** | **AP 0** | **D 1** — same profile as Common Goblin Archers' Shortbow. Fires while moving at the standard -1 to-hit penalty.
