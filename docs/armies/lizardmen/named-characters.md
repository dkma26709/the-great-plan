# Named Characters


> Named characters are unique individuals with one-of-a-kind identity rules and intrinsic equipment. Each is **0-1 per army** (no army may field more than one of a given named character). They cannot take Blessed Spawnings as list-build options — many of them are *pre-loaded* with their canonical spawning baked into their identity rules (Gor-Rok = Mark of the Old Ones, Kroq-Gar = Xhotl, etc.). Magic items, where applicable, are intrinsic to the named character and cannot be swapped.

#### Lord Kroak

> The Venerable Lord Kroak — Defender of Itza, eldest of the First Spawning, the most ancient and powerful of all the Slann. Mortally torn apart by Bloodthirsters at the Defence of Itza in -4450 IC, his spirit refused to concede defeat: it scoured the invaders with a divine light "like unto a second sun" and saved the First City. His Skink attendants gathered every scrap of his ruined body and wrapped it in resin-soaked bandages. Thus was created the first Relic Priest. His husk lies in hidden crypt at Itza, brought forth only in moments of greatest need. He was, without doubt, the strongest caster ever to live; in his half-state between life and death, that power is only a shadow of its former glory — yet still strong enough that Kroak could simply choose to be alive again, were he so inclined. He is, however, more often in a slumbering state.

**Lord Kroak (Slann variant — Relic Priest):**

|---|----|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|-----|----|
| 4 | 0 | 0 | 0 | 3 | 5 | 10 | 1 | 12 | 3 | 4 |

*WS-A 0 / WS-D 0 — Kroak is a mummified husk on a palanquin, surrounded by spirits. He doesn't dodge; he doesn't dueling-strike; he doesn't even register the battle physically. Combat is something done **at** him. The spirits and intrinsic relics fight on his behalf via fixed-2+ to-hit weapon profiles, the same pattern as the generic Slann's Palanquin Lightning.*

**Magic Profile:**

|-----|-----|----|----|----|----|----|-----|
| 6 | 6 | 6 | +5 | +5 | 48" | **4 (all 10 lores)** | **10** |

*Lore Access 4 (Master) across **all 10 lores** — every general lore (Heavens, Beasts, Fire, Light, Life, Death, Shadow, Metal) plus both faction lores (Cosmic Architecture, Geomancy). **SK 10** = the player picks **10 spells at list-build** from any combination of lores and tiers (within Lore Access 4 Master limits). Captures the lore-canonical "strongest caster ever" — Kroak's spell list is fully player-tailored each game, reflecting his omni-arcane mastery. Dispel Range 48" (vs Slann 36") for the lore-direct extended psychic reach.*

**Points:** **675** base (intrinsic regalia and miscast immunity included; no upgrades available)

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Slann, Relic Priest, Wizard, Character, Lord, Named
- **Unit type:** Infantry (Character — Slann on floating palanquin)
- **Natural Armour:** — (mummified husk)
- **Ward Save:** **3+** (Amulet of Itza + spirit-bound resilience; better than living Slann's 4+)
- **Equipment (intrinsic — cannot be removed or swapped):** Ceremonial Mace of Malachite, Standard of the Sacred Serpent, Golden Death Mask, Amulet of Itza, Glyph of Potec, Palanquin
- **Mount/Equipment options:** none (named character)
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Unbreakable** (see §8) — does not accumulate stress, cannot become Shaken / Wavering / Broken; includes Imm Psych. The spirit-bound husk is functionally undead-adjacent: stress, fear, panic, terror — all irrelevant to a being that already died. Lore-direct: Kroak's spirit "refused to let even death hinder him."
  - **Inspiring Presence (30")** — extended even beyond the Slann's 24"; Kroak's psychic projection covers an entire battle line
  - **Miscast Immunity** *(identity rule)* — Lord Kroak ignores all Miscasts. When he would suffer a miscast result on a casting roll (per §7.3 Miscasts), no miscast effect occurs — he simply cannot be backlashed by his own magic. Captures the lore-canonical apex-caster framing: "the strongest caster ever, when alive, and he could just choose to be alive again, such strong is he." Reality has stopped offering Kroak miscast consequences.
  - **Hypnotic Death Mask** *(intrinsic — Golden Death Mask)* — all attacks targeting Kroak in melee suffer **-1 to hit**. The mask's gem-eyes hold the gaze of any who look upon it.
  - **Mace of Malachite** *(intrinsic)* — magical weapons used in melee against Kroak have their AP reduced to 0 (capped). The Mace's residual enchantment negates the magic of weapons that strike at him.
  - **Glyph of Potec** *(intrinsic)* — Kroak's intrinsic weapon attacks (Mace, Serpent Standard, Guardian Spirits) deal **+1 Damage** against any unit with the **Undead** keyword.
  - **Amulet of Itza** *(intrinsic)* — Kroak gains **+1 to all Dispel rolls**.

**Weapon profiles** — all intrinsic, all auto-resolved at fixed **2+ to-hit** (Slann Palanquin Lightning pattern; Kroak does not roll WS-based to-hit because his WS-A is 0):

| Weapon | Reach | A | Hit | S | AP | D | Notes |

| **Standard of the Sacred Serpent** | 1" | D3 | 2+ fixed | 4 | -1 | 1 | Magical Attacks — the golden serpent animates and bites; lore-direct "writhes, hisses and spits like a living creature, animated by Kroak's residual power" |
| **Guardian Spirits** | 2" | D3+1 | 2+ fixed | 4 | -2 | 1 | Magical Attacks — ancestral spirits gravitate around Kroak's husk and lash at any approaching enemy. Range 2" reflects spirit-pursuit (slightly longer than the Mace/Serpent which strike from base contact) |

> **Lore notes:** The Venerable Lord Kroak — Defender of Itza, the most ancient and powerful Slann ever to live, mortally torn apart by Bloodthirsters at the Defence of Itza in -4450 IC and refusing to concede defeat. His husk lies in resin-soaked bandages and is brought forth only in moments of greatest need. **WS-A 0 / WS-D 0** is lore-direct: the mummified husk doesn't fight, doesn't dodge — combat is something done *at* him, and the spirits and intrinsic relics fight back via fixed-2+ profiles. The strongest caster ever to live: **Lore Access 4 Master across all 10 lores** with **SK 10** spells picked freely at list-build, Dispel Range 48". **Miscast Immunity** is the apex-caster identity — reality has stopped offering Kroak miscast consequences; lore-direct "could simply choose to be alive again, were he so inclined." **Unbreakable** replaces Stubborn — Kroak functions like undead in every meaningful way (mummified, spirit-bound, stress-immune, fear-immune); he just isn't *technically* Undead by keyword.

#### Mazdamundi

> Lord Mazdamundi of the Enlightened Sphere — the oldest and most powerful living Slann Mage-Priest, ruler of Hexoatl the Solar-City. Pupil of Lord Kroak at the Dawn of Creation, now his successor in the living world. Where the entranced Slann slumber, Mazdamundi has shaken off lethargy: in recent centuries he has become "the most proactive of all the Slann." A flick of his wrist has shifted continents, smashed cities, doomed armies. He once buried thousands of Skaven beneath a tidal wave of stone with a single word and gesture taught to him by Lord Kroak. He levitates his palanquin-throne atop **Zlaaq**, a colossal Ancient Stegadon that has served him for nearly five hundred years.

| — | 0 | 1 | 0 | 3 | 4 | 8 | 1 | 12 | (mount) | (mount) |

*Slann rider profile is identical to the generic Slann Mage-Priest baseline. Movement, LiS, US derive from Zlaaq the mount.*

**Zlaaq the Ancient Stegadon (mount):**

|---|----|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|-----|----|

*Zlaaq matches the Engine of the Gods chassis (Ancient Stegadon: T 7 / W 12 / NA 3+) — colossal even by Stegadon standards. No howdah weapon (Mazdamundi doesn't need ranged howdah-fire — he is the engine). Combined Mixed Unit per §1.*

**Magic Profile:**

| Cast Base | Dispel Base | Channelling | Cast Bonus | Dispel Bonus | Dispel Range | Lore Access | SK |
|-----|-----|----|----|----|----|----|-----|
| **7** | 6 | 6 | +5 | +5 | 42" | **4 (4 chosen + Loremaster Geomancy)** | **8 + Loremaster Geomancy** |

*Lore Access 4 (Master) across **4 chosen lores** at list-build (any 4 of the 9 non-Geomancy lores) via the **Greater Mastery** Discipline, PLUS **Loremaster (Geomancy)** intrinsic — Mazdamundi knows every Geomancy spell at every tier as a separate, automatic lore. **SK 8** = total spells known across the 4 chosen lores: 4 Sigs (one from each chosen lore, free) + 4 additional spells picked at list-build from the combined pool (any tier within Lore Access limits). Plus all Geomancy spells via Loremaster — total spells known: ~18 (8 picked + ~10 Geomancy). Dispel Range 42" sits between the generic Slann (36") and Lord Kroak (48"). **Cast Base 7** is one above the generic Slann, the lore-direct "powers far outstrip even those of other Slann."*

**Points:** **800** base (intrinsic regalia + Zlaaq mount + 4 baked-in Disciplines included; no upgrades available)

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Slann, Saurian, Wizard, Character, Lord, Named
- **Unit type:** Monster-mount (Slann rider + Ancient Stegadon mount, single model per §1 *Rider and Mount Profiles*)
- **Combined wound pool:** **W16** — max(rider 8, mount 12) = 12 + rider/2 = 4 → W16, per §1 *Combined Wounds*
- **Natural Armour:** **3+** (Zlaaq's plates; Mazdamundi takes wounds via the Mixed Unit shared pool)
- **Ward Save:** **4+** (Shield of the Old Ones — same as generic Slann; deliberately *not* upgraded to 3+ to avoid stacking with the W 20 combined pool into a too-tanky chassis)
- **Equipment (intrinsic — cannot be removed or swapped):** Cobra Mace of Mazdamundi, Sunburst Standard of Hexoatl, Plaque of Tepec, Plaque of Xoloc, Palanquin (intrinsic over Zlaaq's back)
- **Mount/Equipment options:** none (named character)
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) — Slann baseline
  - **Stubborn** (see §8) — Slann baseline
  - **Inspiring Presence (30")** — extended even beyond the generic Slann's 24"; "the most proactive of all the Slann" extends his presence further than slumbering peers
  - **Disciplines of the Slann (4 baked-in)** — Mazdamundi has the following Disciplines pre-applied (cannot be swapped or removed):
    - **Focus of Mystery (Geomancy)** — Loremaster (Geomancy); knows every Geomancy spell at all tiers
    - **Greater Mastery** — 4 chosen lores at list-build instead of 3 (above)
    - **Soul of Stone** — for miscast checks, the Slann's lowest-rolled cast die is not counted toward the "2+ natural 1s" trigger
    - **Focused Rumination** — +1 Cast Base (already encoded as Cast Base 7 above)
  - **Cobra Mace of Mazdamundi** *(intrinsic, sentient weapon)* — the Mace has a sentience of its own and strikes with lightning speed to defy Mazdamundi's foes. The Mace has its own effective **WS-A 5** for to-hit purposes (rolled normally vs the target's WS-D). **Sentient Strike**: when an enemy model in base contact with Mazdamundi or Zlaaq successfully lands a melee hit against this unit, the Cobra Mace immediately makes 1 retaliatory attack against that attacker, resolved *before* the original hit's wound roll. Profile: **1" reach | A 1 | WS-A 5 | S 5 | AP -2 | D 1 | Magical Attacks**. Limit: once per attacking enemy model per Combat phase. Distinct from Lord Kroak's Mace of Malachite — the Cobra Mace is *reactive retaliation* rather than passive AP negation.
  - **Sunburst Standard of Hexoatl** *(intrinsic — Chotec-themed)* — friendly Lizardmen units within **12"** of Mazdamundi gain **+1 Resolve** (separate from Inspiring Presence; the Standard is a totem, the Presence is psychic). Additionally, enemy ranged attacks targeting Mazdamundi or Zlaaq suffer **-1 to hit when fired from more than 12" away, and -2 to hit when fired from 12" or less**. The closer the foe, the brighter the sun-standard burns.
  - **Plaque of Tepec** *(intrinsic, once per battle)* — Mazdamundi may declare the use of the Plaque after an enemy spell is successfully cast but before its effects resolve. The spell **automatically fails** (no dispel roll required); the Plaque shatters into a thousand shards.
  - **Plaque of Xoloc** *(intrinsic, once per battle)* — Mazdamundi may declare the use of the Plaque before rolling a casting roll. He gains **+D6 to that casting roll** (rolled and added). The Plaque crumbles to dust.
  - **Geomancer's Awakening** *(identity rule, once per battle)* — at the start of any friendly Magic phase, Mazdamundi may cast **one Geomancy spell with no Cast Dice required** — the spell auto-succeeds at its base CV (no Miscast roll, no Dispel attempt allowed). Captures the lore-direct "raised one hand and using a word and gesture taught by Lord Kroak, used Geomancy to bury thousands beneath a wave of stone." A once-per-game guaranteed cataclysm.

**Weapon profiles (on Zlaaq):**

- **Goring Horns** *(Zlaaq, charge-only first round)* — 2" reach | **A 2** | S+2 (= S 8 on charge) | AP -3 | D3 | Unstoppable Momentum (D3 hits per enemy in reach) `[Natural]` — same as Stegadon
- **Crushing Bulk** *(Zlaaq, always)* — 1" reach | **A D6** | S 6 | AP -1 | D 1 `[Natural]`
- **Armoured Tail** *(Zlaaq, rear arc only)* — 3" reach | **A 1** | S 6 | AP -1 | D 2 `[Natural]`

> **Lore notes:** The strongest *living* Slann — Lord Kroak's pupil at the Dawn of Creation, now his successor. While most Slann slumber in entranced contemplation, Mazdamundi has shaken off lethargy: "the most proactive of all the Slann," moving continents and dooming armies with a flick of the wrist. He levitates his palanquin atop **Zlaaq**, an Ancient Stegadon that has served him for nearly five hundred years. **Geomancer's Awakening** is the marquee identity: once per game, cast a Geomancy spell with no Cast Dice required (auto-succeeds at base CV, no Miscast, no Dispel allowed) — captures the lore-direct "raised one hand and using a word and gesture taught by Lord Kroak, used Geomancy to bury thousands beneath a wave of stone." Four Disciplines baked in (Focus of Mystery for Loremaster Geomancy, Greater Mastery for 4 lores, Soul of Stone, Focused Rumination), four intrinsic relics (Cobra Mace as sentient retaliatory weapon, Sunburst Standard's dual-range deflection, Plaque of Tepec auto-dispel, Plaque of Xoloc +D6 cast bonus). Magic-power-wise, Kroak still leads on flexibility / reach / miscast safety; Mazdamundi has more *raw* spells known via Loremaster Geomancy. Lore-consistent: Kroak was the strongest caster ever, Mazdamundi is the strongest living one.

#### Kroq-Gar

> The Last Defender of Xhotl — an ancient Saurus Oldblood, the last remaining Saurus warrior from the now-ruined temple-city of Xhotl. His spawning produced a handful of Saurus, each marked by Xhotl for greatness, with instinctive battle-cunning unsurpassed by any of their kind and an innate dominion over the native beasts of the jungle. Kroq-Gar's spawning coincided with the rearing of a brood of mighty Carnosaurs; he claimed the largest and most ferocious as his own. **Grimloq** has served him since, an ageless Carnosaur in perfect lockstep with his rider — not a wild beast bound by fury, but a coordinated extension of Kroq-Gar's command. Mazdamundi gifted Kroq-Gar with the Hand of Gods and tasked him with exterminating those creatures not part of the Great Plan. There is no continent on which Kroq-Gar and Grimloq have not fought.
