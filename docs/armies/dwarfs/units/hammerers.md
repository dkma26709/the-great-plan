# Hammerers

> *"An individual bound by oath to his Lord will fight all the harder, and for the leader of the throng, a Hammerer will gladly give his life rather than face the dishonour of failure."* — Hammerers are the **King's Guard** of the Karaz Ankor: royal bodyguards hand-picked by the Dwarf King from across clans and holds for their martial prowess, bold and courageous nature, and steadfast loyalty. The cult begins in -1245 IC at the gold mines of Gunbad, when Night Goblins overran the entourage of High King **Morgrim Blackbeard** and a master craftsman named **Kadrin Redmane** seized his forge hammer and charged the goblins, saving the King and earning a magnificent Rune Hammer as reward. Since that day, Hammerers have guarded Dwarf kings. Each Hammerer carries a rune-engraved great warhammer — the cult's signature weapon, master-forged to crush armour and split helms — and wears full heavy armour. The apex sub-tier, the **Everguard**, serves the High King at Karaz-a-Karak; their runic hammers are crafted from the deepest hoard of the royal armoury and can *"turn rock to powder with a blow."* The fingerprint: apex Dwarf melee veteran (MA 5, A 2 Great Hammer at S 6 AP -2), Stubborn-amplified by **Personal Oath** (Unbreakable when joined by or near a friendly Dwarf Character), and slow on the swing (-1 I on the Great Hammer) — Hammerers strike *last* but they strike *hard*.

## Special Rules

- **Disciplined** *(see §8 Psychology)* — +1 to Recovery rolls. Hammerers are drilled veteran bodyguards.
- **Stubborn** *(Dwarf faction-wide identity rule — see §8)*. Ignores the state effects of Shaken and Wavering; baseline Dwarf stoicism. The Hammerer's iron-stoic floor *(further amplified by Personal Oath when a Dwarf Character is present)*.
- **Ancestral Grudge** *(Dwarf faction-wide species trait — see §8 Combat: Hatred)*. Hatred (Greenskin / Skaven / Troll / Beastmen). Reroll failed to-Hit in the first round of combat against the four traditional Dwarf enemies. *(The Goblin raid at Gunbad is the cult's founding moment; Greenskins are the most natural Hammerer prey.)*
- **Resolute** *(Dwarf faction-wide species trait — see §8 Psychology)*. -1 to combat-resolution-loss-to-stress conversion (minimum 0).
- **Magic Resistance (1)** *(Dwarf faction-wide species trait — see §8 Defence)*. -1 to enemy cast rolls targeting this unit.
- **Personal Oath** *(Hammerer unit identity rule)* — the Hammerer is sworn to his liege; under the lord's eye, the Oath cannot fail. While this unit is **joined by** any friendly **Dwarf Character** (a Dwarf model with the **Character** keyword — King, Thane, Runelord, Runesmith, etc.), or while any friendly Dwarf Character is **within 6"** of any model in this unit, the unit gains the **Unbreakable** special rule (per §8). The Hammerers do not accumulate stress, cannot become Shaken / Wavering / Broken, are Immune to Psychology, and fight at full effectiveness until physically destroyed. **When no friendly Dwarf Character is within range, the unit reverts to baseline Stubborn** — still Dwarf-stoic (state-effect immunity for Shaken / Wavering), but stress accumulates and the Broken threshold applies. The Oath needs an anchor; without his lord to fight for, the Hammerer is *merely* a stoic veteran. *(Mechanically: the unit toggles between Stubborn (default) and Stubborn + Unbreakable (when within 6" of a friendly Dwarf Character). Forward-compatible with the Dwarf Lord / Thane / Runelord character drafts; until those exist, the unit operates at the Stubborn-baseline tier.)*

## Options

**No equipment options.** The Great Hammer is the cult's signature weapon — no swap to single hammer, no shield, no ranged loadout, no armour downgrade. The full-kit King's-Guard loadout is the cult's identity.

**Command options:**
- **Keeper of the Gate (Leader)** (+5 pts) — Hammerer champion, veteran of the King's-Guard cult; the rank-title carried by senior Hammerers entrusted with the King's hall.
- **Standard Bearer** (+8 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 25 pts). *Runic Standard option held pending §11 Runic Magic system draft — Hammerers traditionally carry rune-engraved banners from the King's forges.*
- **Musician** (+4 pts) — Hammerer drum or horn-player; sets the King's-Guard cadence.

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Great Hammer** *(default, 2H — runic Dwarf-craft great warhammer)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S+2 (= S 6) | AP -2 | D 1 | Two-Handed | **-1 I when used** — the master-forged great warhammer of the King's Guard, often runically engraved at the King's forges. **S 6** wounds T 4 on 2+ and T 5 on 3+, splitting plate with the **AP -2** Dwarf-craft cut. **A 2** reflects the cult's drilled-veteran two-swing combination — one of the rare units that gets multiple swings from a single great weapon, a function of cult training and the carefully-weighted hammer head. The **-1 I** penalty drops the Hammerer to **I 1 at base** (I 2 on the charge with the +1 I charge bonus) — the Hammerer is *built* to strike last and hard, taking the enemy's swing on the heavy plate before delivering the killing blow. `[2H]`
