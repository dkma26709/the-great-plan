# Faction Mechanics


#### Vampiric Hunger *(intrinsic universal trait)*

Applies to all **Vampire** models (Vampire Lord, Vampire Hero, and any future named Vampires). Not a Bloodline Power — it's the foundational trait of vampire-ness.

When a Vampire model **causes a wound** with his own melee attacks (a successful unsaved wound landing on an enemy model — does *not* require killing the target), he immediately regenerates **1 W** (capped at his starting maximum). **Maximum 1 W regenerated per turn** per Vampire — even if the Vampire causes multiple wounds across multiple attacks in a single turn, only one wound is recovered per turn. Captures the lore: vampires drink the blood of those they wound, regenerating their own bulk and unliving vitality.

Pattern note: parallels the Slaughtermaster's **Bloodgreed** (Ogre Lord, regenerates on personal melee kills). Vampiric Hunger triggers on *wounds* (easier to proc, more thematic — vampires drink, they don't only feed on kills) but is capped at *1 W/turn* (preventing the vampire from being functionally unkillable while wounding multiple targets). Bloodgreed and Vampiric Hunger represent two different identity-rule textures for Lord-tier characters with personal-blood-cost regeneration: kill-gated unlimited (Slaughtermaster) vs wound-gated capped (Vampires).

#### Bloodline Powers *(list-build customisation menu — Vampire Lord & Vampire Hero)*

Each Vampire model may purchase any number of Bloodline Powers at list-build (no hard cap — pricing is the only constraint). Powers stack with each other and with Vampiric Hunger. Pattern: faction-flavoured customisation analogous to Lizardmen Blessed Spawnings; each power is a list-build *upgrade slot*, not a per-turn ability. Necromancers do **not** take Bloodline Powers (mortal mages, not vampires).

| Power | Cost | Effect |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Red Fury** | +30 pts | For every **wound** the Vampire causes in his own melee attacks, he may immediately make one additional attack with **the same weapon profile**. Cascading attacks **do not themselves trigger Red Fury** — single layer of cascade only |
| **Quickblood** | +30 pts | Vampire's melee weapons gain **Always Strikes First** (see §8) — strikes resolve before Initiative-ordered strikes regardless of opponent's I |
| **Aura of Dark Majesty** | +25 pts | Enemy units with any model within **6"** of the Vampire suffer **-1 Ld** (no LoS or facing required; the vampire's presence is felt regardless) |
| **Ethereal** | +30 pts | Vampire gains a **5+ Ward Save** against all attacks. Reframed from "only Magical Attacks wound" — the vampire phases partially out of corporeality at irregular intervals, not fully intangible |
| **Master of the Black Arts** | +25 pts | Vampire gains **+1 to all Cast Bonus rolls** (CB on the Vampire's Wizard profile) |
| **Forbidden Lore** | +50 pts | At list-build, the Vampire gains access to **all 8 general lores** (Heavens, Beasts, Fire, Light, Life, Death, Shadow, Metal) on top of intrinsic Necromancy. Effectively expands intrinsic 3-lore access (Death + Necromancy + Shadow) to Mastery-style breadth across the generic Winds. Each newly-accessed lore contributes its Signature spell free; the Vampire's "+1 additional spell" pick may now be drawn from any of the 8 generic lores or Necromancy |
| **Beguile** | +20 pts | Enemy models in **base contact** with the Vampire suffer **-1 WS and -1 I** (passive aura, always-on). The vampire's hypnotic charm rattles direct opponents — sluggish strikes, fumbled timing |
