# River Trolls

> The marsh-and-river sub-variant of the Troll species. River Trolls live in slime, mire, and brackish water — semi-aquatic by biology, with permanently moist skin, webbed feet, and an extra digestive organ that produces caustic stomach acid. Same Common Troll baseline (M 6 / S 5 / T 4 / W 7 / Regen 4+ / Stupid / Fear / Animosity / Promised Feeding) with three sub-variant overlays: **Aquatic** (ignores water-terrain movement penalties), **Strider (Water)** (ignores Dangerous Terrain tests from water), and a signature ranged attack — **Acidic Vomit** (6" single-target, S 5 AP -2 D 1, auto-hits despite Troll BS 0, once per Shooting Phase per Troll). The army-list role: a Troll variant that holds river / marsh terrain as if it were open ground, harasses with short-range corrosive vomit before engaging in melee, and otherwise contributes the same Common Troll brutality. A pack of three River Trolls vomiting in unison on charge-prep produces three auto-hitting S 5 AP -2 strikes — meaningful softening of a heavy-armoured target before the W 7 / S 5 / Regen 4+ melee bodies arrive.

## Special Rules

- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines charge / self-hits at S 5. *(River Troll manifestation: distracted-driven — the brutes pause to scoop and gulp mud-water when bored.)*
- **Stupidity** *(Psychology — see §8)*. Same as Common Trolls; mitigated by Promised Feeding.
- **Regeneration (4+)** *(Defence — see §8)*. 4+ Ward Save against unsaved wounds. **Negated by Flaming attacks.**
- **Fear** *(Psychology — see §8)*. At LiS 2, Fear aura projects to (6 + 2)" = 8".
- **Promised Feeding** *(Troll-typical, Stupidity mitigation)* — friendly Greenskin Character within 6" at start of Movement phase suppresses Stupidity for that turn.
- **Aquatic** *(see §8)* — this unit ignores Movement penalties from water terrain (shallows, streams, marsh) for all movement including charges. Combined with Strider (Water) below, River Trolls treat water terrain as open ground entirely.
- **Strider (Water)** *(see §8)* — this unit ignores Dangerous Terrain tests arising from water-tagged terrain's natural features. Combined with Aquatic above, the unit takes neither movement penalty nor wound risk from water.
- **Acidic Vomit** *(River Troll unit identity rule — natural ranged attack)*. Each River Troll model produces a single short-range corrosive vomit attack during the **Shooting Phase**. **The attack auto-hits — no to-hit roll, BS 0 is irrelevant.** See weapon profile below. Once per Shooting Phase per Troll; cannot be used while engaged in melee (the Troll's stomach is occupied surviving the brawl).

### Ranged

- **Acidic Vomit** *(natural ranged — always available)* — **6" range** | **A 1** | **S 5** | **AP -2** | **D 1** | **Auto-hits** (no to-hit roll; BS 0 is irrelevant) — corrosive stomach acid ejected at close range. Treats the Troll's BS 0 as a non-blocker — the projectile is so close-range and high-volume that aim is not the determining factor.
