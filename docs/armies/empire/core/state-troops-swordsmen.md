# State Troops — Swordsmen


> State Troops armed with sword and Medium Shield, drilled to be fast and excellent duellists. Where Halberdiers march in lockstep with reach-weapons and Spearmen brace into a thicket of points, Swordsmen are the line's *fencers* — close-range parries and ripostes, blade-and-buckler footwork honed in the drill yards of Altdorf and Nuln. The standard pairing in the Detachment system is a Halberdier main block flanked by a Swordsmen detachment that rounds the enemy's exposed side. Recruited from the same provincial levies as their polearm-wielding brethren but selected for hand-eye precision — the regiment's WS bump over the standard reflects the duellist's training.

- **Keywords:** Empire, Human, Infantry, Core, State Troops
- **Unit type:** Infantry
- **Unit size:** 10-30
- **Points:** 8 per model
- **Natural Armour:** —
- **Equipment (default):** Sword (1H Blade) + Medium Shield
- **Worn armour options (pick one, additive):**
  - Light armour (+0.5 pt/model) → 6+ save
  - Medium armour (+1.5 pt/model) → 5+ save
- **Worn armour additive:** Medium Shield is part of the default kit (no additional cost — already included in the 8-pt base)
- **Command options:**
  - **Leader** (+5 pts) — champion. May take a pistol (+3 pts).
  - **Standard bearer** (+10 pts) — may carry a Magic Standard up to 25 pts
  - **Musician** (+5 pts)
- **Special rules:**
  - Disciplined (see §8)
  - **State Troops** — reroll recovery / rally rolls within 6" of friendly Imperial General or BSB

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Sword** *(default, 1H Blade — pairs with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — duellist's blade, parries with the shield arm and ripostes with the sword. Parry baseline 1H Blade(1) + Medium Shield(1) = **Parry(2)**, +2 WS-D in melee per §8 — the Swordsman's defensive identity is *evade and counter*, not block-with-armour `[1H Blade]`

> **Lore notes:** WS-A 4 / WS-D 4 (one above the State Troops baseline) is the WFB-canonical Swordsmen distinction — better hit rate, better defense from training. **Parry(2)** from 1H Blade + Medium Shield (per §8 Parry computation) gives effective WS-D 6 in melee — Swordsmen are genuinely hard to land hits on by mid-skill attackers. Captures the lore-canonical "fast and excellent duellists" + "close range parries and ripostes." No reach (1" Sword) means no second-rank attacks — the Swordsman is a single-rank fencer, not a phalanx fighter. **Pricing 8** — Halberdier 7 + 1 (WS bump). Default kit includes the Medium Shield (Swordsmen lore is *sword-and-shield*; not a shield-optional kit). State Troops baseline rule (reroll near General/BSB) preserved.
