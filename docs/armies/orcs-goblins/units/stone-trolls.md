# Stone Trolls

> The mountain-dwelling sub-variant of the Troll species. Where Common Trolls are forest-and-cave biology, Stone Trolls have spent generations in stony highlands, slowly absorbing mineral matter into their hide. Their skin is petrified — literally — into a thick crust of rock-and-scar that turns blades and resists magic alike. Mechanically: same Common Troll baseline (M 6 / S 5 / T 4 / W 7 / Regen 4+ / Stupid / Fear / Animosity / Promised Feeding), with two sub-variant overlays — **Natural Armour upgraded to 4+** (petrified hide is genuinely armour-grade now), and **Magic Resistance (2)** (the mineralised skin partially blocks magical energy from reaching the Troll inside). They are slightly slower-witted than Common Trolls — even by Troll standards — but that's flavour, not stat. The army-list role: a more durable Troll anchor against magic-heavy opponents, with the same Stupidity tax and the same Promised Feeding mitigation. A pack of three Stone Trolls within 6" of an Orc Big Boss is a brick wall vs Empire Wizard / Forest Spirit / Slann / Tomb Kings — exactly the matchups where magic damage breaks the Common Troll's Regeneration tempo.

## Equipment

- **Equipment (default):** Hands and Maw (natural melee). No worn equipment; the petrified hide replaces any need for worn armour.

## Special Rules

- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines charge / self-hits at S 5. *(Stone Troll manifestation: distracted-driven — the brutes pause to chew on their own hide-rocks if there's no immediate enemy to fight.)*
- **Stupidity** *(Psychology — see §8)*. Same as Common Trolls; mitigated by Promised Feeding.
- **Regeneration (4+)** *(Defence — see §8)*. 4+ Ward Save against unsaved wounds. **Negated by Flaming attacks.**
- **Fear** *(Psychology — see §8)*. At LiS 2, Fear aura projects to (6 + 2)" = 8".
- **Promised Feeding** *(Troll-typical, Stupidity mitigation)* — friendly Greenskin Character within 6" at start of Movement phase suppresses Stupidity for that turn.
- **Magic Resistance (2)** *(see §8)* — enemy spells that target or affect this unit suffer **-2 to the casting roll**. The reduction applies to both the casting-value check and any subsequent dispel attempts. Lore: the mineral-encrusted hide partially blocks magical energy from reaching the Troll inside. Stone Troll sub-variant signature.

## Options

**Equipment swap option (replaces Hands and Maw, pick at most one):**
- **Great Weapon** (+2 pts/model) — replaces the default Hands and Maw profile. Same profile as Common Troll Great Weapon: S+2 (= S 7), AP -2, D 1, Two-Handed, **-2 Initiative when used**.

**No worn armour options.** The petrified hide IS the armour. NA 4+ is the entire defence (alongside Regeneration).

**No command options.** Stone Trolls follow the same pack-of-brutes convention as Common Trolls.
