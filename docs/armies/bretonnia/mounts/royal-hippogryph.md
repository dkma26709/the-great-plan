# Royal Hippogryph *(Lord only)*


The apex flying mount of the Bretonnian noble — a hippogryph, the great fae-touched predator of the Old World's high mountains. Half eagle, half lion, half horse, with razor talons, a serrated maw, and a bestial fury that even the Lady's blessing cannot fully tame. A duke who rides a Royal Hippogryph is making a statement: he is the realm's apex warrior, his personal hunt is for kings and monsters, and his charge is not to be intercepted by lesser cavalry. Reserved exclusively to the Bretonnian Lord — Heroes (Paladin) and Lady's Chosen (Damsel, Prophetess) do not bear hippogryphs.

**Royal Hippogryph (barded):**

*(Royal Hippogryph baseline ground M 8, reduced to M 6 by barding per §8.* **Fly 12"** *— see special rules.)*

- **Available to:** Bretonnian Lord (+130 pts) only *(Pass 3, 2026-05-01: 150 → 130 reflecting W collapse)*
- **Combined wounds:** per §1 *Combined Wounds* (higher of mount W5 or rider W, plus the other ÷ 2 round down). With Lord (W5): max(5,5) + 5÷2 = **W7**. Attackers use highest WS-D (5) and highest T (5) per §1
- **Special rules (intrinsic to Royal Hippogryph):**
  - **Fly (12")** (see §8) — same Fly value as a regular Pegasus. The Royal Hippogryph is heavier than a Royal Pegasus and trades raw flight speed for combat power
  - **Fear** (per §8 — enemy units within charge range gain +1 stress from confronting a Fear-causing model). The hippogryph's bestial scent and predatory motion unsettle lesser units before contact, but it does not radiate the supernatural dread of a Terror-class threat
  - **Apex Hunter** *(identity rule)* — the Royal Hippogryph's natural attacks (Talons and Maw) gain **+1 to-Wound** when targeting enemy models with the **Character**, **Monster**, or **Monstrous Cavalry** keyword. Captures "the hippogryph hunts champions and beasts; lesser prey is not its quarry." Pairs with the Lord's role as the army's hammer against enemy heroes
  - **NOT Purebred Warhorse** — barding's -2 ground M penalty applies; Fly 12" unaffected

**Mount weapon profiles:**

- **Hippogryph's Talons** *(mount natural, primary)* — 1" reach | **A 3** | S 5 | AP -2 | D 1 — rending claws that strip armour and shred flesh. The hippogryph's primary weapon, three slashing strikes per round `[Natural]`
- **Hippogryph's Maw** *(mount natural, secondary)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 5 | AP -1 | D D3 — a single crushing bite, capable of biting through plate. D D3 (D6 / 3, rounded up) means the bite can do 1-3 wounds per unsaved hit — appropriate against Heroes and Monsters `[Natural]`

> **Stat profile summary.** The Royal Hippogryph is a Monster-tier flying mount disguised as cavalry: mount W 5 / T 5 / A 4 (3 Talons + 1 Maw) / Fly 12 / Fear keyword. **Pass 3 (2026-05-01)** — under §1 *Combined Wounds* the Lord-on-Hippogryph model is W7 (max(5,5) + 5÷2 = 5 + 2 = **W7**), comparable to Saurus Oldblood + Carnosaur at W14 on a smaller scale but with Fly mobility. **+150 → +130 pts** for Pass 3 reflecting the W collapse from old pool W10 → conversion W7 (factor 6.48 → 4.36, ratio 0.67 applied to mount-W-scaling portion). With Grail Vow + Royal Hippogryph + 100 pt magic items + BSB, the Lord lands at **~455 pts** — the duchy's grand champion, fully kitted.
