#### Gor-Rok

> The Great White Lizard, the Scarred One — among the greatest Saurus in living memory. Spawned at Itza with pure albino colouring and lone arrival, marked from his first breath as a future champion. Now an apex Sunblood-tier combatant: the **solid centre** around which Saurus battle lines advance, the rock on which Itza's enemies are broken. Heavily scarred body, oversized frame thick-corded with muscle, scales tough enough to turn most blade-strikes. He has survived horrendous wounds — pulled an Elven assassin's lance through his own chest to bite the wielder's throat — and never has injury hindered him from duty. Gifted by the Skink-priesthood with the Mace of Ulumak (whose hand the Old Ones themselves guide) and the Shield of Aeons (a massive volcanic-stone slab a dozen Kroxigor were needed to haul). Gor-Rok knows neither pain nor fear; in combat he is unrelenting, always seeking the next opening, never himself stepping backwards.
