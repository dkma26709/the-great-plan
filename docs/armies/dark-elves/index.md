# Dark Elves


> The Druchii of Naggaroth. Three millennia of exiled bitterness, slave-society militarism, and Khainite cult-violence have shaped a people for whom war is both vocation and worship. Every Druchii unit is an expression of *lethality* — the kin-shared elven skill stack channelled through cult ritual, raider tradition, or executioner discipline. Where the Asur defend Ulthuan with cult-disciplined precision, the Druchii *attack* the world with cult-ritualised brutality.

## Status

**Currently classed as §18 Cross-Faction Reference** — a single representative unit (Witch Elves) at present, with full-faction promotion deferred until the roster is broader. This document is the design reference for future Druchii unit drafts: it captures the philosophy, axes, and constraints that should hold across the faction so each unit's design loop starts from a shared baseline.

**Drafted units:** [Witch Elves](units/witch-elves.md), [Executioners of Har Ganeth](units/executioners-of-har-ganeth.md), [War Hydra](units/war-hydra.md), [Kharibdyss](units/kharibdyss.md).

## Cultural axis

Within the elf species, three sub-factions share biology but warp the cultural axis differently:

| Sub-faction | Cultural identity | MA / MD axis | Recovery trait |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Asur** (High Elves) | Disciplined citizen-soldiers, ten-millennia drill, warrior-cults at elite tier | **MA ≈ MD** (disciplined-balanced) | **Disciplined** baseline |
| **Druchii** (Dark Elves) | Khainite raiders, slave-society militarism, exile-bitterness | **MA > MD** (attacker-lean — degree varies by cult) | **Cult-dependent** — Frenzy / Disciplined / hatred-driven |
| **Asrai** (Wood Elves, pending) | Forest-rangers, ambush-hunters, kite-and-fade | **MA < MD** (skirmisher-evader) | Likely neutral or skirmisher-specific |

Druchii sit at the **attacker-lean pole**. Their cultural identity is *killing* — the elf skill stack channelled into lethality rather than discipline. But the *intensity* of attacker-lean varies significantly across the Druchii roster — three sub-cults express the killing-ethos differently:

| Cult / role | MA / MD pattern | Recovery / morale | Example unit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Khainite frenzied** | MA > MD by 2+ (e.g., MA 5 / MD 3) | **Frenzy** + cult bloodlust | Witch Elves *(drafted, 13 pts)*, Sisters of Slaughter |
| **Khainite disciplined** | MA > MD by 1-2 (e.g., MA 7 / MD 5) | **Disciplined** + cult ritual | Executioners *(drafted, 26 pts)*, Black Guard |
| **Slaver-raider militarism** | MA = MD or MA > MD by 1 | Hatred-extension or baseline | Bleakswords, Dreadspears, Corsairs, Dark Riders |

The **Asur/Druchii axis** is therefore not "Asur disciplined / Druchii frenzied" full-stop — both factions have disciplined and frenzied units. The cleaner axis is **defensive-skill (Asur) vs lethal-attack (Druchii)**: Asur cults specialise in *parrying and counter-striking*, Druchii cults specialise in *first-strike and kill-or-die*. The MA/MD pattern reflects this — Asur push MD up, Druchii push MD down.

## Biological constraints

The shared elf-species ceiling (per §13) is hard — same as for Asur:

- **T 3 / W 1 at rank-and-file** — never raised by elf-cultural rules. Druchii elites get more attacks, higher MA, sometimes Killing Blow or other one-strike mechanics; they **do not** get T 4 or W 2.
- **No Natural Armour** — Druchii protection comes from worn armour (often heavy mail and shield), Elven Grace, and Murderous Prowess; never from intrinsic hide.
- **Hero-tier W cap 3, Lord-tier W cap 4-5** — Druchii Dreadlords are individually formidable but cannot match a Vampire Lord or Saurus Oldblood's biological bulk.
- **No Monstrous Infantry tier** — elf-bodied units stay slim. Druchii reach into Monster-class via *mounts* (Cold Ones, Hydras, Dragons, Manticores), never via the elf body itself.

## Faction-wide rules

All rank-and-file and most elite Druchii **elven** units carry the following at minimum (the rules are *biological* to the elf — non-elven Druchii units like Cold Ones, War Hydras, and Dark Pegasus mounts do **not** inherit them):

- **Elven Grace** (per §8 *Defence*) — Ward 6+ in melee vs attacks at the elf's I or lower. Cross-elf rule — shared with Asur, and expected for Asrai pending the Wood Elf draft.
- **Murderous Prowess** (per §8 *Combat*) — reroll to-Wound 1s in melee. **Druchii-specific.** The lethality axis of the elf-prowess pair.

**Non-faction-wide rules** (cult-dependent — apply only to specific Druchii units):

- **Disciplined** — applies to Khainite-disciplined cults (Executioners, Black Guard) and some elite raider units. Does **not** apply to Khainite-frenzied cults (Witch Elves, Sisters of Slaughter — they carry Frenzy instead) or to rank-and-file raider militia.
- **Frenzy** — Khainite-frenzied cults only. Mutually exclusive with Disciplined.
- **Khainite** *(keyword)* — gates Hag Queen / Death Hag character attachment, sometimes confers cult-specific buffs. Carried by Witch Elves, Sisters of Slaughter, Executioners, Death Hags.

**Asur prowess axis (for contrast):** the Asur carry **Martial Prowess** (reroll to-Hit 1s) — the accuracy axis. Druchii and Asur prowess are mutually exclusive: a Druchii unit carries Murderous, never Martial; an Asur unit carries Martial, never Murderous.

## Cult-elite scaling rules

Druchii cult-elites scale above the rank-and-file baseline along several axes, depending on the cult identity:

**Khainite-frenzied cults (Witch Elves, Sisters of Slaughter):**
1. **Push MA up, MD down** to extreme asymmetric attacker (MA 5-6, MD 3).
2. **Frenzy intrinsic** — Imm Psych + +1 A + must-charge + must-pursue.
3. **Add unit-specific Khainite identity rule** — Daughters of Khaine for Witch Elves (kill-conditioned stress relief); Sisters of Slaughter likely a parry-and-counterstrike rule.
4. **Add Poisoned Attacks** — Khainite blade-venom.
5. **Add Hatred (High Elves)** — kin-grudge.
6. **No armour, paired weapons** — bare-flesh ritual identity; trade defence for offence.

**Khainite-disciplined cults (Executioners, Black Guard):**
1. **Push MA up, MD up modestly** (MA 7, MD 5-6) — disciplined-attacker rather than frenzied-glass-cannon.
2. **Disciplined** intrinsic — cold cult ritual rather than blood-frenzy.
3. **Add Killing Blow variant** — Executioner-cult signature is one-perfect-strike (extended-trigger KB, variable damage).
4. **Heavy armour standard** — cult-ceremonial plate.
5. **Khainite keyword** — character-attachment gating + cult-buff eligibility.

**Slaver-raider militarism (Corsairs, Dark Shards, Dreadspears):**
1. **MA = MD or MA > MD by 1** — competent attacker but not cult-elite.
2. **Hatred-extension** — Eternal Hatred (High Elves) or extended-Hatred-target.
3. **Repeater Crossbow** for ranged specialists — the Druchii-specific shooting baseline.
4. **Sea Dragon Cloak** for raider units — defensive layer with Ward vs ranged equivalent (Corsairs).

## Equipment patterns

- **Paired weapons** — Witch Elves' Pair of Khainite Blades; Corsairs' Sea-Drake blades; Dark Shards' melee fallback. The Druchii multi-strike baseline.
- **Repeater Crossbows** — the Druchii-specific shooting weapon. Multiple Shots (2-3), short-range volley. Dark Shards' hallmark; mounted variants on Dark Riders.
- **Draich** *(Khainite ceremonial executioner blade)* — 2H greataxe variant, ritual-forged by the Executioner wielder. The Druchii-specific great weapon class.
- **Heavy armour standard for cult elites** — Black Guard, Cold One Knights, Executioners wear heavy mail and full plate.
- **Sea Dragon Cloak** — defensive equipment for Corsairs and Dark Shards, providing a Ward-equivalent vs ranged attacks. Sea-coast raider tradition.
- **No gunpowder** — the Druchii reject industrial firearms in favour of repeater crossbow tradition.

## Magic

**Dark Magic** is the Druchii faction lore — the corrupted, dangerous version of the disciplined Asur High Magic. Pending dedicated drafting, but the design hooks are:

- Druchii **Sorceresses** are common at Hero tier; **Supreme Sorceresses** and **Witch Kings** at Lord tier.
- Dark Magic should be drafted with an *aggressive-corruption* emphasis, distinct from Asur High Magic (buff/dispel) and from Asrai Wild magic (forest-tradition).
- A Dark Magic caster pays *self-harm* costs for casting — captures the "fast and powerful but unstable and self-damaging" framing.
- **Witch King Malekith** — apex Druchii Lord (Sorcerer-King); pending design.

## Mounts and monsters

- **Cold Ones** — Druchii-specific cavalry mount. Cold-blooded saurians ridden by Cold One Knights; parallel to Lizardmen Aggradon mounts. Mount carries Stupidity-equivalent or Stupid-suppression mechanic per WAP.
- **War Hydra** *(drafted, 294 pts)* — Druchii-iconic apex non-character Monster. W 10 multi-headed serpent with model-based snake-body stat read (M 5, MD 3, NA 5+, Res 6 — animal-tier morale, snake-bodied rather than the lore-implied heavily-armoured-stomper). **Multi-Headed Regrowth** custom Regeneration variant (per-wound D6 4+ heal, no Flame-negation — homebrew bump against the WAP canonical Hydra-fire weakness). **Many Heads** dynamic A=W mechanic (Heads-and-Jaws Attacks equals current Wounds remaining; A 10 at full health, dropping one-per-wound). **Heads-and-Jaws** 3" reach, S 5 AP -1 D 1 with **Poisoned Attacks** (venomous-bite from Druchii breeding programmes; lore-myth inheritance from classical Hydra blood-poison). **Fiery Breath** template (S 5 / AP -1 / Flaming). Pure Monster profile (no on-table Beastmaster Handlers — handler bond is implicit, deferred to a future Druchii Hero draft).
- **Kharibdyss** *(drafted, 308 pts)* — Druchii second apex non-character Monster, deep-sea counterpart to the War Hydra. W 10 chitin-bodied abyssal beast (M 5, MA 5, MD 4, S 7, T 6, NA 4+, Res 6 — same M-band as the Hydra but better across MA / MD / S / NA, paying for it with no Regen and no Breath). **Abyssal Howl** *(Psychology-tagged)* custom stress-aura amplifier (+1 stress per turn to enemies within Terror radius, on top of Terror's +2 — net +3 stress per turn within 11"). Imm Psych units (Frenzied, Will of Chaos, Undead) ignore both the Howl and the Terror baseline. **Cavernous Maw** anti-elite concentrated bite (1" reach, A 3, S 7, AP -3, D D3 — one of the highest per-hit damage natural weapons in the codebase). **Lashing Tentacles** long-reach grab (3" reach, A 5, S 4, AP 0, D 1) with the **Pluck** unit identity rule: against a model with US-1 base contribution (typical Infantry / on-foot character / champion), each Lashing Tentacle wound deals **6 Damage** instead of D 1 (normal saves apply, counterplay through Toughness / armour / Ward). Pure Monster profile (no on-table Beastmaster Handlers — handler bond implicit, deferred). **Roster-pair with the War Hydra:** both Rare slot, both W 10, both M 5, but opposite mechanical valence. Hydra is the *anti-mass line-breaker* (volume-bites + breath template + Regrowth); Kharibdyss is the *anti-character / character-grabber* (Maw concentrated crush + Tentacle 3" reach over front-rank + Pluck spike vs single-base targets + Howl morale dominance, mind-vulnerable targets only). List-builder picks one or pays 600+ pts for both.
- **Dark Pegasus** — Druchii Hero mount, mounted Sorceress / Dreadlord; corrupted-Pegasus variant.
- **Manticores** — shared with Asur for apex Hero / Lord mounts.
- **Dragons** — apex Lord mount. Black Dragons (Druchii-trained corrupt Dragons) are the Druchii apex battlefield commanders, equivalent to Asur Star/Moon/Sun Dragons.
- **Cauldron of Blood** — Druchii-iconic Khainite altar. Not strictly a monster, but a faction-anchor mobile shrine that boosts nearby Witch Elves and other Khainite units. Carried by Hag Queens.
- **Reaper Bolt Thrower** — Druchii war machine, twin-bolt repeater-style. Parallel to Asur Eagle Claw.

## Pricing notes

**Pass 9 framework (2026-05-07)** — all Druchii units re-derive under §13 with the **0.8× global scaling factor**. Pure 0.8× of WAP printed prices under-prices Druchii similar to Asur, because the +1 Res convention, Elven Grace, Murderous Prowess, and high-I baseline are not in the WAP baseline. The framework correction produces:

- Witch Elf 13 pts/model (T 3 W 1 glass-cannon Frenzy with paired blades; sits same tier as Dwarf Warrior 13 pts — different identity, same cost).
- Executioner 26 pts/model (drafted — heavy armour, MA 7 / MD 5, I 7, Res 10, Draich brute-force greataxe S+2 AP -2 **D 1 baseline**, Khaine's Stroke KB(Any, D3) on natural 5+, Disciplined + Hatred (HE) + Fear). Sits 1 pt below Swordmasters of Hoeth (27 pts) — the lower baseline D 1 trades absolute-damage for variable-spike texture; expected damage per attack is roughly matched.

**Expected Druchii ladder for undrafted units:** Bleakswords ~10 / Dreadspears ~10 / Dark Shards ~11-13 / Corsairs ~14-16 / Black Guard ~26-32 / Cold One Knights ~28-35 / Sisters of Slaughter ~14-16 / Shades ~16-18 / War Hydra and Kharibdyss drafted (both apex-Monster Rare).

**Apply at draft:** trust the framework re-derivation over the WAP printed price when they disagree (per Witch Elves precedent — uplifted from WAP 10 to framework 13 reflecting rule-density). Document framework-versus-legacy disagreements in the unit's design diary.

## Cross-faction comparators

Useful anchors when drafting a Druchii unit:

| Comparator | Why it's useful |
|---|---|
| **Witch Elves (drafted, Druchii Frenzy)** | The canonical *frenzied-Khainite* anchor. MA 5 / MD 3 + Frenzy + Poisoned + Hatred (HE) + Daughters of Khaine. Use as the contra-axis comparator when drafting *disciplined-Khainite* units like Executioners or Black Guard — the same cult-god, the same kin-grudge, expressed through opposite mechanical channels. |
| **Swordmasters of Hoeth (Asur Special)** | The kin-counterpart for any disciplined-Khainite Druchii draft. Same I 7 / Res 10 / heavy armour / 2H weapon / Killing Blow signature, but the Asur version pushes defence (MA/MD 7/7 + Ward vs ranged) while the Druchii version pushes offence (MA 7 / MD 5 + brute-force weapon profile). |
| **Saurus Temple Guard (Lizardmen Special)** | The cross-species cult-elite bodyguard comparator. Temple Guard tank via T 4 / W 2 / 4+ save / Sworn Protectors; Druchii cult-elites kill via I 7 / Murderous Prowess / Killing Blow. Same role-tier in respective factions, opposite design valence. |
| **Chaos Warriors (Mortal Chaos baseline)** | The non-elf elite ceiling. Chaos wins on T 4 / W 2 / Will of Chaos; Druchii cult-elites win on I 7 / MA 7 / KB. Druchii pays for higher Initiative and skill density; Chaos pays for biological bulk and morale resilience. |
| **Bretonnian Foot Knights / Empire Greatswords (human elite-Special)** | The "what Druchii cult-elites slaughter" comparator. Druchii kill heavy human infantry handily — MA 7 / KB / S 4-5 greatweapons strip the heavy-armour / W 1 human profile efficiently. |

## Drafting hooks

Open Druchii design questions, captured here for future unit passes:

- **Dark Magic mechanic** — pending. Likely an aggressive-burst-with-self-harm lore distinct from the eight Winds, Asur High Magic, and Lore of Necromancy. Drafted Druchii caster units (Sorceress, Supreme Sorceress) will surface specifics.
- **Sea Dragon Cloak** — pending. A Druchii equipment layer (Corsairs, Dark Shards) providing Ward-equivalent defence vs ranged. Mechanically parallel to Bretonnian Blessing of the Lady's ranged-Ward component, but equipment-tier rather than faction-mechanic-tier.
- **Cold One Stupidity / Stupid-suppression** — pending. WAP-canonical Cold Ones cause Stupidity on the rider. Need a mechanical translation: either a baseline Stupid penalty or a "Cold One Knight discipline suppresses Stupid" rule.
- **War Hydra breath weapon and regeneration** — drafted. Apex Druchii Monster; see [War Hydra/profile.md](units/war-hydra.md).
- **Kharibdyss Abyssal Howl and Pluck** — drafted. Second apex Druchii Monster; see [Kharibdyss/profile.md](units/kharibdyss.md).
- **Witch King Malekith** — Lord-tier apex character. Sorcerer-King with Black Dragon mount, Soul-Bound items, full magical and martial profile.
- **Cauldron of Blood mechanics** — Khainite mobile shrine; faction-anchor aura unit. Parallel to Bretonnian Sacrosanctum but Druchii-themed.
- **Eternal Hatred (High Elves)** — most Druchii units carry Hatred against Asur. Should this be a faction-wide rule (auto-on all Druchii units in matchups vs Asur), or a per-unit declaration (Witch Elves carry it, others optional)? Current draft has Witch Elves with Hatred(HE); future passes need to standardise.
