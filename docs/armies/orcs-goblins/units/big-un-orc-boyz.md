# Big 'Un Orc Boyz

> The Boyz who survived. Every Common Orc Boy who lives through enough fights grows physically larger — bigger frame, thicker arms, harder fists, a noticeably louder voice when shouting "WAAAGH!". The Big 'Uns are these veteran survivors: not a cult-elite (that's the Black Orc), not a frenzied glass-cannon (that's the Savage Orc), just *bigger Boyz*. Same Choppa, same dim wits, same Animosity tax, same Loves a Scrap brawl-momentum — but more wounds in the bigger frame and an extra attack on every swing (**+1 A always-on**). A unit of Big 'Uns is a unit of Boyz that hit harder for longer, with a slightly better armour kit (medium hide-and-plate scrap rather than light scavenged scraps). They are not more *skilled* — MA 4 / MD 3 unchanged — and they are not more *disciplined* — still Undisciplined, still rolling Animosity. They just have more *meat* and more *swings* than a Common Boy. From round 2 of any sustained brawl, Big 'Uns hit harder still — the +1 A from Big 'Un identity stacks with the +1 A from Loves a Scrap sustained-combat momentum, giving 3 attacks per Choppa or 4 per Pair of Choppas while the brawl holds.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines whether the unit charges nearest enemy or takes D3 self-hits at the unit's own Strength.
- **Loves a Scrap** *(Orc-specific faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. Removes 1 stress per Combat phase engaged in melee; +1 A on a single weapon profile if engaged at end of previous turn.
- **Big 'Un** *(unit identity rule)*. **+1 Attack on a single weapon profile, always-on** (per the +1-attack convention — single profile, not all profiles, and applies regardless of engagement history). Stacks additively with Loves a Scrap's sustained-combat +1 A: a Big 'Un already in melee at the end of the previous turn gains **+2 A on a single weapon profile** for the current turn (Big 'Un baseline +1 *plus* Loves a Scrap momentum +1).

## Options

**Worn armour options:**
- **Light armour** (+1 pt/model) → 6+ save
- **Medium armour** (+2 pt/model) → 5+ save

**Worn armour additive:** Shield (+1 pt/model) — combines with armour for one step per source (per §8 Armour Save Stacking)

**Equipment options (pick one):**
- **Additional Choppa** (+2 pt/model) — second hand weapon; cannot pair with shield
- **Spear** (+1 pt/model) — 2" reach; may pair with shield
- **Great Choppa** (+2 pt/model) — replaces hand weapon, no shield, two-handed

**Command options:**
- **Boss (Leader)** (+5 pts) — champion
- **Standard Bearer** (+10 pts, may take Magic Standard up to 25 pts)
- **Musician** (+5 pts)

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Choppa** *(default, 1H — may pair with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 4 | **AP -1** | D 1 — any heavy edged thing the Big 'Un can swing; cleaver-weight bites through light armour. Big 'Un identity adds +1 A always-on → effective A 2 on this profile; Loves a Scrap momentum from round 2 stacks → effective A 3 `[1H Blade]`
- **Pair of Choppas** *(Additional Choppa — 2× 1H — cannot pair with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 4 | **AP -1** | D 1 — second hand weapon doubles the swings; loses the shield slot. Big 'Un +1 A → effective A 3; Loves a Scrap stack → effective A 4 from round 2. The signature brawl-volume Big 'Un load `[Paired]`
- **Spear** *(option, 1H — may pair with shield)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | S 4 | AP 0 | D 1 — improvised spear; the 2" reach lets the second rank stab past the first. No charge-defence bonus. Big 'Un +1 A → effective A 2; Loves a Scrap stack → effective A 3 from round 2. Trade-off vs Choppa: gain reach, lose AP `[1H Reach]`
- **Great Choppa** *(option, 2H — no shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S+2 (= S 6) | **AP -1** | D 1 | **Two-Handed** | **-1 Initiative when used** — heavy two-handed cleaver; pure brute damage. Big 'Un +1 A → effective A 2 at S 6; Loves a Scrap stack → effective A 3 from round 2 (still at the -1 I penalty — effective I 1 baseline, I 2 on the charge turn) `[2H]`
