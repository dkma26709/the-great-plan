# Chakax


> The Eternity Warden, Prime Guardian of Xlanhuapec, City of Mists. Last surviving member of his spawning, eldest of the city's Temple Guard. For millennia he has stood watch over the Slann at their most vulnerable moments — when they meditate in the Eternity Chamber. **Not a single Slann under his protection has fallen to an enemy's blade.** His vigil is silent and absolute: he stands motionless for centuries, dust settling upon his form — until a threat materialises, at which point he explodes into violent motion. He pulped the Daemon Slaa'Ulaan with a single combat sequence; he caught a Skaven Assassin's Weeping Blade strike, smashed the assassin against a wall, and resumed his vigil without recording a word. Star-Stone Mace, Helm of the Prime Guardian, Key to the Eternity Chamber — sacred regalia that have served the line of Eternity Wardens since the founding of the first temple-city.

- **Special rules:**
  - **Saurus species package** — Disciplined, Cold-Blooded, Stubborn, Predatory Fighter, Fear, Locked Jaws (all from Saurus species)
  - **Tail Sweep weapon** *(rear-arc, foot-only — see weapon profiles)*
  - **Eternity Warden** *(bodyguard identity rule — the marquee role)* — when a friendly **Slann** model (named or generic) within **6"** of Chakax would suffer an unsaved wound, the controlling player may transfer that wound to Chakax instead. Chakax takes the wound; the Slann is saved. Captures lore-direct "stepped into the bolt of energy, saving his lord from death" + "not a single Slann under his protection has died to an enemy blow." Limit: while Chakax has Wounds remaining; once at 0 W, this rule no longer functions.
  - **Sentinel-Until-Triggered** *(identity rule — explodes into motion when threatened)* — at the start of any friendly turn, if Chakax did not move in his prior Movement phase, he gains **+1 A on all weapon profiles** for the upcoming Combat phase. Captures lore-direct "stood guard until a patrol found them" + "exploded into violent motion when Slaa'Ulaan stepped to claim the Slann" — Chakax fights *harder* when he's been holding position. The reward for his stoic vigil.
  - **Star-Stone Mace — Magic-Inerting Aura** *(intrinsic, 3" aura)* — enemy magical items, magic weapons' enchantments, Wards from magical sources, and other magical-item-derived effects are **rendered inert** while their bearer is within **3" of Chakax**. Magical items cannot be activated; magic-weapon AP/keyword modifiers don't apply; magical Wards fail. (The **Magical Attacks** keyword as a hit-detection mechanic — Ethereal-bypass etc. — is biological-resonance and still functions normally.) Lore-direct "Star-Stone has the rare property of rendering magical enchantments inert."
  - **Helm of the Prime Guardian** *(intrinsic — heightened awareness)* — **Scouts** deploying onto the battlefield (per §8) cannot be placed within **20"** of Chakax (instead of the normal 12" from enemy units). The Helm's lingering spirit detects scouts at extended range and pre-empts their forward deployment.
  - **Key to the Eternity Chamber** *(intrinsic — time-slow aura)* — enemy units with at least one model within **6" of Chakax** have their melee attacks resolve at **Always Strikes Last** for the combat phase, regardless of Initiative. Lore-direct "the Key slows the passage of time" — enemies caught in temporal lag near Chakax always strike last. Single, strong effect; the temporal-lag aura is the entire mechanic.
