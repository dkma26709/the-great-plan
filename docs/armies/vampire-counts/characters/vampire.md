# Vampire *(Hero)*


> A junior member of a vampire bloodline — newly turned (a few decades to a century or two old) or a less-prominent lieutenant of a Lord. Combat-skilled but not at apex; Lvl 1 Wizard baseline with Necromancy access. Fights in front of skeleton lines, leads cavalry charges, scouts ahead of the main necromantic core. The Hero-tier vampire carries the universal Vampiric Hunger + can purchase Bloodline Powers (typically 1-2 at list-build budget).

*Lvl 1 caster baseline (LA 1 — Basic spell access only). May upgrade to Lvl 2 at list-build for +30 pts. **Lores Known: Necromancy** intrinsic (single-lore — Vampire Heroes have less arcane breadth than Lord-tier vampires). **SK 2** = total spells known: Signature spell (free, from Necromancy) + 1 additional spell from Necromancy. With **Forbidden Lore** Bloodline Power, lore access expands to all 8 generic lores + Necromancy (does not change SK; just widens the pool).*

**Points:** **125** base (Lvl 1, heavy armour + shield + hand weapon, no Bloodline Powers). **+30** to Lvl 2 (= 155 pts). Bloodline Powers add listed costs.

  - **Necromantic Anchor** *(identity rule, Hero version)* — at the end of each Combat phase, friendly **Undead** units with any model within **6"** of the Vampire Hero **Crumble for 1 less wound** (same mechanic as the Vampire Lord's Necromantic Anchor, half range). Anchor effects from multiple Vampire characters do **not stack** — a unit caught in overlapping Vampire Lord and Vampire Hero auras receives -1 wound reduction, not -2

> **Lore notes:** A junior member of a vampire bloodline — newly turned (a few decades to a century or two old) or a less-prominent lieutenant. Combat-skilled but not at apex; Lvl 1 Wizard baseline with Necromancy access only (single-lore — significantly less arcane breadth than the Lord's three-lore access; the Hero/Lord magic-breadth differentiator). Same identity-expression structure as the Vampire Lord, scaled to Hero budget: **Vampiric Hunger** intrinsic + **Bloodline Powers** purchasable menu, plus **Necromantic Anchor** at half the Lord's range (6"). Anchor effects do not stack across multiple Vampire characters — a unit caught in overlapping Lord and Hero auras receives -1 wound reduction, not -2.
