# Default AoI:


> The classic Lizardmen army — a balanced host of Saurus rank-and-file backed by Skink scouts and skirmishers, anchored by Old One-blessed temple guard, and supported by jungle beasts ranging from Salamanders to apex monsters. Slann command from levitating palanquins; Saurus and Skink characters direct individual cohorts. The Lustrian Host represents the canonical Lizardmen way of war: combined arms, combined castes, the Old Ones' Plan executed across the field.

**Army-wide rules:** None. (Themed AoIs may add 0-2 rules.)

**Mandatory picks:** None.

**Banned picks:** None.

**Magic lore access:** Per character profiles. Slann Mage-Priests access all 8 general lores (Light, Heavens, Beasts, Life, Fire, Death, Metal, Shadow) plus the Lizardmen-flavoured **Cosmic Architecture**. Skink Priests / High Priests / Starseers / Tetto'eko access narrower per their stat blocks (typically Heavens + Beasts + Life subsets per character).

**Named-hero access:** All named characters available under the Default AoI. Themed AoIs may carve some out as locked-only-under-this-AoI unlocks.

**Faction-specific army-build hooks:**
- **Blessed Spawnings** (see Blessed Spawnings section below) — eligible units and characters may take up to 3 Spawnings each, drawn from the 14-Spawning menu. Each Spawning beyond the first promotes a *unit* one FoC tier (Core → Special → Rare; max Rare). Character Spawnings are flat-cost with no tier shift. The Spawnings system is the primary list-build identity layer for Lizardmen.
