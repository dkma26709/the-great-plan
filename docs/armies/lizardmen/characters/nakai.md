# Nakai


> **The Wanderer, Sacred Kroxigor of the First Spawning** — eldest still-living Lizardman, possible avatar of the Old One Quetzl, possibly a jungle spirit made manifest. Spawned at Tlanxla in the First Spawnings before the Great Catastrophe, before Chaos first entered the world with the destruction of the Old Ones' Polar Gates. Albino, marked by the divine hands of the Old Ones; his hide is even thicker than other Kroxigor's, particularly those of later spawnings — Blessed Spawning of Quetzl. At the Defence of Itza, Nakai held the Bridge of Stars single-handed against four-score Daemons until reinforcements arrived; so great was the tally of Daemon-things he reaped that day that such creatures fear that site as they do no other place in the mortal world, hearing the death knell of their kin echoing down the millennia. He killed Clan Pestilens's plague-Rat Ogre but was infected by its dying gore — months in coma, then back to his feet and onwards. He aided Lord Xltep against Luthor Harkon's Vampire Coast in 930 IC. He appears anywhere out of the jungle during times of need and disappears just as quickly; his presence is a tangible portent that a battle of great import is about to take place. Drawn by lust for fighting; the longer the battle rages, the more likely his arrival. Should he withdraw, the path behind him is sealed by vines and creepers. Wields the **Sacred Blade of Quetzl** — sentient, hums with mystic power; it draws his hand to its targets, and blocks blows he is unaware of. The Skink Priests claim the Blade is Quetzl himself, and that Nakai is the war-god's instrument in the mortal world.

- **Special rules:**
  - **Saurus species package** — Disciplined, Cold-Blooded, Stubborn, Predatory Fighter, Fear, Locked Jaws (all from Saurus species inheritance, same as Kroxigor Ancient)
  - **Aquatic** (Kroxigor amphibious biology)
  - **Tail Club** *(rear arc, foot-only — see weapon profiles)*
  - **Mark of the Old Ones** *(intrinsic — albino spawning, baked-in)* — at the start of each battle, Nakai generates **D3+1 Old Ones' Favour** tokens. Each token may be spent to **reroll any single D6** rolled by Nakai. Re-rolled result stands. Maximum **one Mark of the Old Ones per army** (Nakai in the army means no other character can take this spawning — and he is mutually exclusive with Gor-Rok, who also bears the Mark).
  - **Blessed Spawning of Quetzl** *(intrinsic — already encoded in NA 2+; flavor: even tougher hide than any other Kroxigor)*
  - **Avatar of Quetzl** *(identity rule — divine framing)* — Nakai is **Immune to Psychology** and **Immune to Fear / Terror**. Many Skink Priests believe he is the Old One Quetzl made manifest in mortal form; whatever the truth, he fights with serene divine certainty.
  - **Drawn to Battle** *(identity rule — the Wanderer)* — Nakai cannot be deployed normally. He begins the battle in **reserves** (off-board, undisclosed jungle aerie). At the start of each friendly turn from turn 2 onwards, the controlling player rolls one D6 to determine if Nakai appears: **turn 2: 6+; turn 3: 5+; turn 4: 4+; turn 5: 3+; turn 6+: automatic**. When the roll succeeds, place Nakai anywhere within **6" of a battlefield edge or within 6" of any forest / jungle terrain feature**, more than **9" from any enemy unit**. He may charge on the turn he arrives (he arrives drawn by the lust for fighting; battle is already joined). Lore-direct: "drawn by a lust for fighting, and as a battle rages on, the likelihood of his appearing greatly increases... appears out of nowhere, and disappears just as quickly, erupting from the jungle or marshes."
  - **Daemon-Reaper of the Bridge of Stars** *(identity rule — anti-Chaos veteran)* — Nakai's attacks against units with the **Daemon** keyword gain **+1 to wound** and **+1 AP**. Lore-direct: "killed four-score Daemon-things at the Bridge of Stars; such creatures fear that site as they do no other place in the mortal world, hearing the death knell of their kin echoing down the millennia."
  - **Plague-Survivor** *(identity rule — regeneration; survived heinous wounds)* — at the end of every friendly turn, Nakai recovers **1 lost Wound** (up to a maximum of his starting Wounds total). Captures lore-direct survival of wounds that would prove fatal for any other creature, including the Pestilens-plagued months-long coma he ultimately recovered from.
  - **Vanishing Path** *(identity rule — the jungle answers him)* — when Nakai is removed as a casualty, place a **Difficult Terrain** marker on the position he occupied at the moment of his removal. The marker remains for the rest of the battle. Vines and creepers erupt to seal the path he walked. Lore-direct: "should Nakai withdraw from battle, the path behind him will be blocked by vines and creepers."
  - **Eldest of the First Spawning** *(character shift — blanket, per §9.3)* — while Nakai is in your list, **all Kroxigor units count as Core** instead of Special. The Sacred Kroxigor of the First Spawning is the apex avatar of his caste; in his presence, every spawn-brother takes the field as the army's natural rank-and-file core, not a specialist heavy-melee asset. (Generic Kroxigor Ancient's per-unit *Old of the Spawning* shift — see Kroxigor Ancient entry — is the lower-tier equivalent.)

> **Lore notes:** The Wanderer, Sacred Kroxigor of the First Spawning — eldest still-living Lizardman, possible avatar of the Old One Quetzl, possibly a jungle spirit made manifest. Albino, marked by the divine hands of the Old Ones at the First Spawnings before the Great Catastrophe. Held the Bridge of Stars single-handed against four-score Daemons; killed Clan Pestilens's plague-Rat Ogre and survived the dying gore in months-long coma; aided Lord Xltep against Luthor Harkon's Vampire Coast. **Drawn to Battle** is the marquee identity: Nakai cannot be deployed normally — he begins in reserves and rolls to arrive each turn from turn 2 onwards (escalating: 6+, 5+, 4+, 3+, auto from turn 6), placing within 6" of a board edge or any forest / jungle feature, more than 9" from enemy. Captures the canonical "appears out of nowhere drawn by lust for fighting, more likely the longer the battle rages." High-variance: against a fast army he may never arrive; in a grinding war of attrition, he is guaranteed. **Avatar of Quetzl** (Imm Psych + Imm Fear/Terror) = divine certainty. **Sacred Blade of Quetzl** is sentient — rerolls all failed melee to-hit (the Blade draws his hand) and grants Ward 5+ vs melee (the Blade blocks unseen blows). **Daemon-Reaper of the Bridge of Stars** lands +1 to wound and +1 AP vs Daemon — close-quarters anti-Daemon specialist counterpart to Oxyotl's ranged role. **Plague-Survivor** regenerates 1 W per turn (the Pestilens plague couldn't kill him outright). **Vanishing Path** seals his retreat — when removed, his last position becomes Difficult Terrain ("vines and creepers seal the path he walked"). **Mark of the Old Ones** intrinsic, mutually exclusive with Gor-Rok in the same army.
