# Knights of the Realm


> The Bretonnian backbone — sworn knights of the realm, sons of every duchy and minor lord, tested and proven in their *errantry* and now invested with the Knight's Vow. Each carries a heavy lance, a heater shield bearing his coat of arms, full plate harness, and rides a barded warhorse from the duchy's stables. They charge in *lance formation* — the trained wedge that cleaves enemy lines, the lance-tip biting deepest at the moment of impact, the Lady's hand turning aside the worst the enemy can offer in return. Where Empire knights are paid professionals organised into orders, Bretonnian knights are *sworn vassals* riding in the name of their duke, of the king, and of the Lady of the Lake. The knight is everything; the peasant is incidental.

- **Keywords:** Bretonnia, Human, Cavalry, Core, Knights of the Realm
- **Unit type:** Cavalry
- **Unit size:** 5-12 (wider upper bound than Imperial Knights' 5-10 to accommodate Lance Formation depth — a 10-model wedge fills 4 rows; 12 models spill into a partial row 5)
- **Wounds per model:** **W1** — Warhorse mount W1 + rider conversion (KotR W1 → +0 step), per §1 *Combined Wounds*. Attackers use highest WS-D (4) and highest T (3)
- **Natural Armour:** —
- **Worn armour (default, fixed kit):** Heavy armour + Heavy Shield + barding (per §8 Armour Save Stacking, one step per source)
- **Combined save: 2+** (heavy armour 4+ → Heavy Shield 3+ → barding 2+)
- **Equipment (fixed kit, no swap options):** Heavy Lance + Heavy Shield + heavy armour + barding
- **Vow upgrade options (replace Knight's Vow at list-build):**
  - **Crusader's Vow** (+1 pt/model, whole-unit upgrade) — replaces Knight's Vow keyword. See §17 Faction Mechanics for full definition. Grants Immunity to Panic-source stress, reroll failed psychology-source recovery rolls, and +1D6 charge distance roll (discard lowest). Useful for KotR units committed to anti-Chaos / anti-Undead / anti-Greenskin crusade campaigns
  - *(Questing Vow not available — Questing Vow forbids Heavy Lance per §17, and KotR are lance-specialist units; a unit of knights who give up the lance becomes Questing Knights, drafted as a separate unit in §17 Special)*
  - *(Grail Vow not available at unit level — the Grail Vow is exceptionally rare; Grail-Vow knights typically appear as individual characters or future Grail Knights units rather than as a Knight's-Vow upgrade)*
- **Command options:**
  - **Leader** (+4 pts) — the unit's *Knight Gallant*; champion for Rally / challenge / Leader-snipe purposes
  - **Standard bearer** (+8 pts — Magic Standard up to 40 pts; Bretonnian Knights get the elite cavalry banner budget, matching Imperial Knights)
  - **Musician** (+4 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls (the unit's herald, signalling charges and rallies on horn)
- **Special rules:**
  - Disciplined (see §8)
  - **Knight's Vow** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — Blessing-eligibility marker. No additional effect at this vow tier. Replace with Crusader's Vow at list-build (see Vow upgrade options above)
  - **Blessing of the Lady** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — intrinsic via the Knight's Vow. Ward Save 6+ vs S ≤ 4, 5+ vs S ≥ 5, applying to rider and mount alike. Lost on flee from combat
  - **Lance Formation eligible** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — counts as Lance whenever the unit's geometry forms a triangular wedge of 6+ models in 3+ rows; +2" reach + +1 AP on melee weapons during the charge round
  - **Purebred Warhorse** (see §8) — applies to the mount profile. The Warhorse does not suffer the -2 M penalty for being barded; KotR retain M 10 with full barding, unlike Imperial Knights' barded warhorses (M 8). Speed-preservation rule, no offensive effect

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Heavy Lance** *(default, 1H with shield — fixed kit, no swap)* — **2" reach | A 1 | on charge (first round): S+2 (= S 5), AP -2, D 2; subsequent rounds: S+0, AP 0, D 1** — the iconic Bretonnian shock weapon. With the Lance Formation +2" reach extension, Heavy Lance becomes 4" reach on the charge round, allowing rows 2 and 3 of the wedge to strike alongside the wedge tip `[1H Lance]`
- **Warhorse's Hooves and Bite** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — the mount contributes its own attack at the standard barded-warhorse profile `[Natural]`

> **Lore notes:** The defining Bretonnian Core unit — sworn knights of the realm, sons of every duchy, charging in lance formation under the Lady's hand. Mechanical identity is **faction-mechanic-driven, not unit-internal**: the Knight's Vow grants Blessing eligibility, the **Blessing of the Lady** provides the divine Ward, and **Lance Formation** provides the offensive identity. No internal special rule beyond these markers — deliberately so. **Equipment is fixed** — no weapon swap options. The lance is the chivalric weapon, the weapon of investiture, the weapon the Lady blessed; swapping belongs to Questing Knights who renounce it, not to the realm's standard knight. **Purebred Warhorse** keeps the destrier at M 10 through barding — speed is the Bretonnian heavy cavalry's defining edge.
