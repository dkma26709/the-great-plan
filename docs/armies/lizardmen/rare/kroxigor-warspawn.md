# Kroxigor Warspawn

> The largest and most vicious of the Kroxigors, marked by Sotek as warriors. Where the regular Kroxigor is a labour-and-war hybrid, the Warspawn exists *purely* for combat — diamond-tipped Warpicks raised to crack fortifications and steam-tanks alike, jaws strong enough to bite through armour and bone in one snap, scales so thick they shrug off blows the common Kroxigor would feel. Spawned in fury when the star-cluster of Sotek's Maw blazed above Mallus during the Era of the Beast, opening hidden ziggurat chambers; previously rare, now more numerous, but always tethered to a Skink handler-cohort whose proximity focuses their single-minded war-fury.

## Special Rules

- Cold-Blooded
- Aquatic (see §8) — Kroxigor biology
- **Predatory Fighter** (see §8) — Saurus-kin biology (same as regular Kroxigor pass 2 retrofit)
- **Fear** *(Psychology — see §8)* — radius (6 + LiS)" = **9"**, same as regular Kroxigor
- **Locked Jaws** *(Saurus species rule)* — when a Warspawn model in this unit is removed as a casualty during the Combat phase, before removal it makes 1 final Saurus Jaws attack against the killing model (or closest enemy in base contact). Single attack, no Predatory Fighter cascade.
- **Emboldened Handlers** *(same as regular Kroxigor)* — while this unit has at least one model within 6" of a friendly Skink unit, that Skink unit gains +1 Res for all purposes
- **Stupidity** (conditional, same as regular Kroxigor) (see §8) — if no friendly Skink unit has a model within 6" of this unit at the start of its Movement-phase activation, this unit suffers Stupidity for that turn
- **Pure Combat Focus** *(Warspawn identity rule)* — Warspawn have no labour or non-combat utility. They cannot be added to a Skink Cohort as the Kroxigor attachment option (which would treat them as supporting infantry). They are deployed as a standalone Monstrous Infantry unit only.
