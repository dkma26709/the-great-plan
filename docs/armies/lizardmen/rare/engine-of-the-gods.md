# Engine of the Gods


> An Ancient Stegadon, venerable and revered, bearing on its back one of the rarest relics of the Old Ones — the Engine of the Gods. The Engine reshapes reality on the battlefield: warding nearby Lizardmen behind a shimmering aura, channeling additional arcane energy into the army's casters, and at the Skink crew's command unleashing searing celestial fire, knitting the Stegadon's own wounds, or warping reality around an enemy unit. Each Engine is sacred; the loss of one might irredeemably jeopardise the Great Plan. First deployed by Tehenhauin, the Prophet of Sotek, at the Siege of Quetza.

*Skink Priest-Crew share the Stegadon's M (they ride atop the howdah). Five Skinks, junior priesthood members trained in the rote glyph-sequence to activate the Engine. They have no ranged weapon — the Engine is their armament; their hand weapons are self-defence only.*

  - **Skink-Reared Bond** *(unit identity rule — inherited from Stegadon)* — while this Stegadon has at least one model within **6"** of a friendly Lizardmen Skink unit, that Skink unit gains **+1 Res** for all purposes
  - **Unstoppable Momentum** *(charge identity rule — inherited from Stegadon)* — Goring Horns charge strikes resolve as **D3 hits per enemy model in melee reach** (scales down on Degrading; see Stegadon damage table for the same scaling)

    - **Portent of Warding** *(passive aura, always-on)* — while this Stegadon is on the battlefield, this Stegadon and any friendly **Lizardmen** unit with at least one model within **6"** has a **6+ Ward Save**.
    - **Arcane Configuration** *(passive, always-on)* — while this Stegadon is on the battlefield, the Lizardmen player gains **+1 die to the general magic dice pool** at the start of each friendly Magic phase. The cosmic engine resonates across the army's casters.
    - **Burning Alignment** *(active — once per friendly Shooting OR Magic phase, the player chooses one of three effects)*:
      - **Searing Bolt** — pick one enemy unit within **24"** line of sight; that unit suffers **2D6 hits at S 5 / AP -2 / D 1 / Magical Attacks / Flaming**.
      - **Healing Light** — this Stegadon **recovers D3 wounds** (cannot exceed starting total).
      - **Reality Warp** — pick one enemy unit within **12"**; until start of the next friendly turn, that unit suffers **-1 to all to-hit rolls** (melee and ranged).
  - **Degrading** — same 3-tier damage table as regular Stegadon (see Stegadon entry); identical scaling on M, Crushing Bulk A, and Unstoppable Momentum D3/D2/1.

> **Lore notes:** An Ancient Stegadon bearing one of the rarest relics of the Old Ones — the Engine of the Gods. Three Lizardmen relic-carriers run distinct identities: Stegadon = ranged howdah platform; Arcanadon = cannon + caster amplifier; Engine of the Gods = passive support + active versatility. **Portent of Warding** (6+ Ward to nearby Lizardmen) and **Arcane Configuration** (+1 die to the magic pool) are the always-on supports; **Burning Alignment** is the active menu, picked once per phase to capture the AoS lore variety — Searing Bolt (canonical "bolts of lightning or heavenly fire"), Healing Light ("healing light reinvigorating Lizardmen"), or Reality Warp (the "slowing time" / reality-warp aspect). No misfire — the venerable Engine is too sacred for table-level dice failure. Ancient stat bumps reflect "only the most ancient of these beasts are considered sufficiently solid... to bear the weight of an Engine of the Gods."
