# Lore of Heavens


> Celestial magic — the movement of stars, the fall of comets, the crack of lightning. Heavens wizards read the threads of fate and call down the sky. The lore's identity is twofold: fate manipulation (rerolls, hexes, foresight) and devastating anti-armour damage (Lightning Attacks).

**Lore Attribute — Astral Alignment:** A Heavens caster who is not in combat (no model within melee reach of an enemy) may reroll one die of their casting roll on their next cast this turn. The wizard reads the alignment of the stars and chooses which die to discard. Lost the moment they are engaged — you cannot contemplate the heavens with a blade swinging at your throat.

> **Damage spells from this lore have the Lightning Attacks special rule** (see §8 *Special Rules Glossary*) — ignoring armour saves entirely. This is inherent to any Heavens damage spell regardless of the lore attribute.

**Signature Spell — Harmonic Convergence**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit may reroll all to-hit, to-wound, and armour save rolls of 1. The stars align — minor adjustments to fate that compound across an entire unit's worth of dice.

#### Basic (Tier 1) — Lore Access 1+

**Iceshard Blizzard**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 24" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit suffers -1 to hit (melee and ranged) and +1 stress. A localised blizzard of ice shards — disorienting, blinding, and demoralising even if no one is killed.

**Cerulean Shield**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains Ward Save (5+) against ranged attacks. A shimmering celestial barrier deflects arrows and bolts — useless in melee, invaluable under fire.

**Wind Blast**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 7 | 24" | Hex | Instant |

Target enemy unit is pushed D6" directly away from the caster. If the unit contacts impassable terrain or another unit during the push, it suffers D6 S3 hits. A howling gale — not lethal in itself, but it disrupts positioning, can shatter formations, and punishes tightly packed armies.

**Urannon's Thunderbolt**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 8 | 24" | Magic Missile | Instant |

D6 S5 hits with Lightning Attacks (ignore armour saves). A single devastating bolt called down from the heavens — the signature damage spell of every Heavens wizard. Basic tier but high casting value: any Heavens caster can learn it, but landing it reliably demands real magical ability.

#### Intermediate (Tier 2) — Lore Access 2+

**Curse of the Midnight Wind**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 8 | 24" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit must reroll all 6s on to-hit, to-wound, and armour save rolls. The inverse of Harmonic Convergence — where the stars align for your allies, they conspire against your enemies. Devastating against elite units that rely on every roll counting.

**Portent of Far-Seeing**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 7 | Self | Augment | Until start of next turn |

The caster and all friendly units within 12" may reroll one failed dispel or recovery roll per turn. Foresight rippling outward — the wizard sees the threads and whispers guidance to those nearby.

**Storm of Cronos**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 9 | 18" | Direct Damage | Instant |

All enemy units within 18" of the caster suffer D3 S4 hits with Lightning Attacks (ignore armour saves). A ring of celestial lightning radiating outward from the wizard — punishes anything that gets too close, in any direction. The caster stands at the eye of the storm.

#### Advanced (Tier 3) — Lore Access 3+

**Chain Lightning**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 11 | 24" | Direct Damage | Instant |

D6 S6 hits with Lightning Attacks (ignore armour saves) on the target unit. Then the lightning chains: on a 4+, the nearest enemy unit within 6" suffers the same hits. On a 5+, the next nearest. On a 6+, the next. Each link in the chain is a separate attack — the bolt arcs between metal, moisture, and flesh, seeking the path of least resistance.

**Comet of Casandora**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 12 | 36" | Summoning | Persistent |

Place a marker anywhere within range. At the start of each subsequent turn, roll D6: on 1-3 the comet grows (add +1 to the comet's Strength for the eventual impact, starting at S5); on 4-6 it strikes — all units within 2D6" of the marker suffer 2D6 hits at the comet's current Strength with Lightning Attacks (ignore armour saves). All units hit suffer +2 stress. The marker cannot be dispelled once placed — the comet is already falling. The longer it hangs in the sky, the worse the impact. Armies must scatter or gamble.

**Sapphire Arch**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 10 | 24" | Conveyance | Special |

Remove a friendly unit from the battlefield entirely — it passes through a celestial gateway into the space between stars. At the start of any subsequent friendly Magic phase, the caster may place the unit anywhere on the table more than 9" from any enemy model. If the caster dies before the unit returns, the unit is lost — the arch collapses and the travellers are stranded between worlds.
