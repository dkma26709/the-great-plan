# Temple Guard


> The elite of the Saurus spawnings. Selected at birth, trained to guard the Slann with absolute devotion. WS5 puts them on par with the finest warriors of other races — and rightly so.

> **Sworn Protectors.** The Temple Guard's purpose is absolute: the Slann must survive.
>
> - **Bodyguard.** When an enemy attack (melee, ranged, spell with a single-unit target, or Sniper) is being resolved, if this unit and a friendly Slann are *both* valid targets, the attacker **must** choose this unit instead of the Slann. Does not prevent attacks where the Slann is the only valid target (no Temple Guard in range / arc / LOS).
> - **Slann's Resolve.** While this unit has at least one model within **3" of a friendly Slann**, stress thresholds (Shaken, Wavering, Broken) are treated as **+1 higher**, AND the unit **cannot be pushed back** by losing combat resolution. Without a nearby Slann these bonuses do not apply — the unit remains Stubborn but is otherwise normal.

> **Lore notes:** Ld 12 — the unflinching devotion of Slann-consecrated guardians. WS5 distinguishes them clearly from regular Saurus. Natural Armour 5+ (sacred scales) plus worn light armour and shield combines to a 3+ save — the one Saurus unit that *does* wear layered protection. Saurus strength allows them to wield the Celestite Halberd one-handed, keeping a shield in the off-hand. Sworn Protectors packages the bodyguard role explicitly: Temple Guard soak attacks aimed at their Slann, and their presence makes the Slann's own unit unbreakable.
