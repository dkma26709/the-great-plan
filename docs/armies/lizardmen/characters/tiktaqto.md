# Tiktaq'to


> **Master of Skies, Eye in the Heavens** — pre-eminent Skink Chief of Hexoatl, greatest of all Terradon Riders, answerable only to the Skink Priests and Slann Mage-Priests. Spends long periods on aerial patrol — Terradon aeries set throughout the jungles, half-deserted ruins, floating islands tethered by mystic chains, forts carved into the tallest trees. Atop his particularly fierce Terradon Zwup, Tiktaq'to leads breath-taking aerial manoeuvres through dense vegetation and steep-sided valleys so narrow the Terradons brush the edges with each wing-beat. Famed for snatching the Blue Viper Savage Orc Warboss from the battlefield and dropping him from thousands of feet — the Warboss's high-pitched screaming and indignant death broke the tribe's will to fight. Led the vital delaying action against the hordes of Vashnaar the Tormentor. Wields the Blade of Ancient Skies (carved from the dense fossilised jawbone of a monstrous extinct sky-borne predator, jagged-edged tips) and bears the Mask of Heavens (an heirloom triple-blessed by the Skink Priests, allowing the wearer to meld his consciousness with the Terradons; flock and rider fight as one entity, dodging arrow-clouds and trees alike).

  - **Master of Skies** *(identity rule — pre-eminent Terradon command)* — friendly **Terradon Rider** units within **18"** of Tiktaq'to gain **+1 to hit** in shooting (including Drop Rocks attacks) and may **re-roll the D3 hits roll** on their Drop Rocks attacks (taking the second result). Lore-direct: "directs his Terradon patrols from far above, with a view otherwise afforded only to the gods" + "leads them in such breath-taking manoeuvres."
  - **Mask of Heavens** *(identity rule — flock-mind, melded consciousness)* — Tiktaq'to and any friendly **Terradon Rider** unit with at least one model within **12"** of Tiktaq'to gain **-1 to be hit by ranged attacks** while flying. The Mask binds the riders' minds to their mounts; the flock weaves and dodges as one entity. Lore-direct: "they dodge through clouds of arrows, emerging unscathed... fly through the jungle as though they were a single entity."
  - **Eye in the Heavens** *(identity rule — sharp-eye scout)* — at the start of every friendly Shooting phase, Tiktaq'to nominates one visible enemy unit within **24"** and line-of-sight. Until end of phase, all friendly **Skink** units shooting that nominated unit gain **+1 to hit**. (Same mechanic as the generic Skink Chief's Target Designation, but the trigger range is 24" — Tiktaq'to's vantage is godlike.)
  - **Hit-and-Run Tactician** *(identity rule — aerial mobility)* — at the end of any Combat phase in which Tiktaq'to is engaged but neither side is destroyed, Tiktaq'to may immediately disengage and make a **free Fly move of up to 14"**, ignoring the normal rules for breaking from combat. He may not end this move within 1" of an enemy unit. Lore-direct: "leads bold hit-and-run attacks... gone before the enemy can raise a bow."
  - **Sky-Pack** *(character shift — blanket, per §9.3)* — while Tiktaq'to is in your list, **all Terradon Riders units count as Core** instead of Special. The pre-eminent Terradon Chief commands every aerie's flock as the army's leading aerial vanguard, not its specialist scout-unit. The Mask of Heavens binds rider and beast into one — and the *whole flock* obeys his airborne directives.
