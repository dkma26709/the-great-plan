# Battle Wizard

> Junior tier of the Imperial wizard ladder — graduates of the Colleges of Magic at Altdorf, recently certified by their respective College Master. Each Battle Wizard channels one Wind only — Light, Metal, Life, Fire, Heavens, Death, Shadow, or Beasts; selected at the College and unchangeable thereafter. Field-deployed in support of the army, often the only caster a smaller Empire force can field. The Wizard Lord's apprentice — competent, formally trained, decades from College Master rank.
