# Kroxigor


> Massive, powerful, and dim. The muscle of the Lizardmen — directed by Skink handlers, devastating when they connect. You don't kill a Kroxigor by outfighting it. You kill it by hacking through an enormous amount of living meat.

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Kroxigor, Monstrous Infantry, Special
- **Unit type:** Monstrous Infantry
- **Unit size:** 3-6
- **Points:** 85 per model
- **Natural Armour:** 5+ (thick hide and bony ridges)
- **Equipment (default):** Moonstone Club — see Armoury
- **Equipment options:**
  - **Ancient Weapons** (free swap) — replaces the Moonstone Club. See Armoury.
- **Command options:** none (traditional for Monstrous Infantry)
- **Special rules:**
  - **Emboldened Handlers** — while this unit has at least one model within **6" of a friendly Skink unit**, that Skink unit gains **+1 Res** for all purposes. The massive handlers are a comforting bulwark — nearby Skinks think faster, hold firmer, and panic less.
  - **Stupidity** (conditional) (see §8) — if no friendly Skink unit has a model within 6" of this unit at the start of its Movement-phase activation, this unit suffers **Stupidity** for that turn. Without the handlers to focus them, the Kroxigor lumber aimlessly toward the nearest perceived threat.

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Ancient Weapons** *(option, free swap)* — see Armoury for specific profiles | **A 3**
- **Jaws** *(natural, always-available secondary)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 5 | AP 0 | D 1 — the Kroxigor's bite as an auxiliary attack `[Natural]`

> **Lore notes:** **W7** / T5 / US 4 / LiS 3 — big, strong, and incredibly hard to put down. The W bump from 6 to 7 (revised 2026-04-25 alongside the Ogre wound-scale calibration) preserves "regular Kroxigor are physically larger than rank-and-file Ogres" since rank-and-file Ogre Bulls sit at W 6; Kroxigor's muscle-bound saurian frame edges out Ogre tubby bulk by one wound at the rank-and-file tier. The save is deliberately modest at 5+; their durability comes from the wound pool and toughness, not armour. A knight in full plate (3+ save, W 1) and a Kroxigor (5+ save, **W 7**) are different *kinds* of tough. The knight deflects blows. The Kroxigor absorbs them. S5 (down from S6) combined with the Moonstone Club (+2 S, 3" reach, AP -2, D2) still brings them to S7 on the charge — enough to wound most enemies on 2s or 3s. WS3 + I2 reflects "dim brute who can swing a club just fine when properly pointed." The Emboldened Handlers / Stupidity pair is a single coin flipped two ways — Kroxigor near Skinks are a boon to everyone; Kroxigor without Skinks are a liability. This is the central Kroxigor tension, now mechanically explicit.
