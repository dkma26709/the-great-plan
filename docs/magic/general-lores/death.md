# Lore of Death


> Decay, fear, and the slow ebb of the soul. Where Life restores, Death **drains**. Where Light removes stress, Death **applies** it. The lore specialises in three things no other lore does: wound siphoning between enemy and ally, direct stress damage that bypasses combat entirely, and precision character attacks. Death is a psychic lore — cold whispers, grave-dust, the will giving up before the body. Its magic almost never projects as a missile; it settles, blankets, and drains.

**Lore Attribute — Presence of Doom:** whenever this wizard successfully casts a Death spell, roll a **D6**. On a **5+**, one enemy unit within **12"** of the caster immediately suffers **+1 stress**. No per-turn cap — the hand of death reaches further on some casts than others. Clean mirror of Light's Radiance (which removes stress from friends on cast).

**Signature Spell — Caress of Laniph**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 6 | 18" | Direct Damage, Instant |

Target enemy **single model** (character, champion, or any solo model — not a rank-and-file model in a unit). The target suffers **D3 wounds with no armour save allowed** (ward saves permitted). The cheap sniper — Death's workmanlike spell, always available, threatens every exposed hero on the field. A character with decent Wounds and a ward save weathers one cast; a glass-cannon wizard opposite just died.

#### Basic (Tier 1) — Lore Access 1+

**Spirit Leech**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 7 | 18" | Hex, Instant |

Target enemy unit suffers **+D3 stress** immediately. Pure soul-drain — the nerve gives way before the body is ever touched. Combines viciously with the lore attribute: on a cast that triggers Presence of Doom, this spell can dump +D3+1 stress onto a single unit in one cast.

**Aspect of the Dreadknight**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains the **Fear** keyword (see §8) until start of next turn. If the target already causes Fear, it upgrades to causing **Terror** for the duration instead. A grim silhouette settles over the unit — the enemy sees corpses even where there are soldiers.

**Siphon Life**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 7 | 18" | Hex, Instant |

Target enemy unit suffers **D3 wounds with no saves allowed** (ward saves permitted). Then **one friendly unit within 12"** of the caster restores the **same number of wounds** (wounded models first, per Regrowth rules). One life taken, one life given — roll the D3 once and apply to both sides. Mechanically unique: only Death transfers wounds this way.

**Pall of Despair**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 8 | 18" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit **cannot Rally** until start of next turn — any stress on the unit stays on it through the Rally sub-phase. Makes stress stick. Directly weaponises the lore attribute: Presence of Doom applies stress, Pall of Despair prevents its removal. One of the cleanest combos in the lore.

#### Intermediate (Tier 2) — Lore Access 2+

**Wind of Death**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 9 | 24" | Magic Missile |

**D6 hits at S5, AP -2, D2.** The lore's only true magic missile — narrower but heavier than Fire's typical offerings (which favour 2D6 hits at lower damage). Rewards precision over spread. **Magical Attacks.**

**Curse of Years** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 9 | 18" | Hex, Remains In Play |

On cast, target enemy unit suffers **-1 T** immediately. At the start of **each friendly Magic phase** the spell remains in play (including the turn it was cast), the Toughness penalty worsens by a further **-1** (no cap). When dispelled or the unit is destroyed, all penalties end. The flesh forgets how to function, a little more each day — a canny caster who can protect the spell from dispel dice will see the target unit crumble after three or four turns.

**Doom and Darkness**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 10 | 18" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit treats its Ld as **-2 lower** for all stress threshold calculations until start of next turn (Shaken at Ld-6, Wavering at Ld-4, Broken at Ld-2). Ld for combat resolution modifiers and other Ld-dependent rules is unchanged — only the *stress thresholds* shift. A morale crusher that makes existing stress bite twice as hard without adding a single point.

#### Advanced (Tier 3) — Lore Access 3+

**Soulblight**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 11 | 18" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit suffers **-1 S and -1 T** until start of next turn. The flesh remembers old failures; every sinew weakens. A phase-long neutering of an entire unit — often cast on the piece you intend to charge next turn.

**Fate of Bjuna**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 11 | 18" | Direct Damage, Instant |

Target enemy unit. **Each model** in the target unit must pass a **Toughness test** (roll a D6 — equal to or less than the model's T passes). On a failure, that model suffers **1 wound with no armour save allowed** (ward saves permitted). A blanket of grave-dust settles over the unit; the weaker flesh surrenders first. Scales naturally: a 20-strong T3 mob loses ~10 wounds, while a T5 monstrous regiment barely notices.

**Purple Sun of Xereus** *(RIP)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 13 | 18" | Direct Damage, Remains In Play, Template |

Place a **large round template** anywhere within 18" of the caster; on initial placement the template **scatters D3" in a random direction** (as Flame Storm). The spell remains in play until dispelled.

While in play, any model touched by the template — on initial placement, and at the start of **each friendly Magic phase** while the spell remains in play — must pass a **Toughness test**. On a failure, that model suffers **1 wound with no armour save allowed** (ward saves permitted).

At the start of each friendly Magic phase **after the cast-turn**, before resolving damage, the template moves **a scatter die's distance in a random direction**. Full template mechanics (partial coverage, placement rules, scatter die behaviour) will be formalised in §7.2 Shooting phase. **Magical Attacks.**

A slow drifting annihilation. Often wanders off-target — but while it sits on a unit, it grinds.

> **Design note on Death.** Death is deliberately the *loudest* morale lore — Presence of Doom, Spirit Leech, Pall of Despair, and Doom and Darkness all push the stress system directly, in ways no other lore does. Its damage is narrow (one magic missile, one single-model Direct Damage signature, two unit-scale Direct Damage spells at T3) but characterised by the inability to be saved against armour. Where Fire sprays hits and Heavens lands lightning, Death whispers — and what it touches simply stops living.
