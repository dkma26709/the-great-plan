# State Troops — Spearmen


> State Troops armed with long spear and Medium Shield, drilled to hold the line within a thicket of spear-points. Where Halberdiers chop and hook and Swordsmen fence, Spearmen present an unbroken hedgerow of leveled points — the front rank braces, the second rank stabs past the first, and the line absorbs charges that would shatter a sword-armed regiment. Particularly favoured in the northern provinces where cavalry encounters dictate the tactical calculus. The Detachment-system pairing is often Spearmen in the main block, Halberdier or Swordsmen detachment on the flanks.

- **Keywords:** Empire, Human, Infantry, Core, State Troops

- **Unit size:** 10-30

- **Natural Armour:** —
- **Equipment (default):** Spear (1H Reach) + Medium Shield
- **Worn armour options (pick one, additive):**
  - Light armour (+0.5 pt/model) → 6+ save
  - Medium armour (+1.5 pt/model) → 5+ save
- **Worn armour additive:** Medium Shield is part of the default kit (no additional cost — already included in the 7-pt base)
- **Command options:**
  - **Leader** (+5 pts) — champion. May take a pistol (+3 pts).
  - **Standard bearer** (+10 pts) — may carry a Magic Standard up to 25 pts
  - **Musician** (+5 pts)
- **Special rules:**
  - Disciplined (see §8)
  - **State Troops** — reroll recovery / rally rolls within 6" of friendly Imperial General or BSB
  - **Receive Charge** *(unit identity rule)* — when this unit is **charged in front arc** (per §6 Charges), the Spear weapon profile gains **+1 S** for the first round of the resulting combat only. Captures the lore-canonical "thicket of spear-points" anti-cavalry / anti-charge identity. The +1 S applies whether the charger is cavalry, infantry, monster, or any other charging unit type — the leveled spears bite harder when the enemy commits forward into them. Returns to baseline S 3 from round 2 onward (the bracing posture only generates the kinetic-energy advantage on the initial impact)

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Spear** *(default, 1H Reach — pairs with shield)* — **2" reach** | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — leveled spear; second-rank Spearmen stab past the front rank. **Receive Charge** identity rule applies — see Special Rules. Parry baseline 1H Reach(0) + Medium Shield(1) = **Parry(1)**, +1 WS-D in melee per §8 `[1H Reach]`

> **Lore notes:** Stats unchanged from baseline State Troops — Spearmen are differentiated by equipment and the **Receive Charge** identity rule, not by stat bumps. The 2" reach + Receive Charge captures both the *thicket* (second-rank stabs past front) and the *anti-charge* (S 4 vs charging units in round 1) lore vectors. Cleaner than baking the bonus into the weapon profile permanently — Spearmen reward defensive positioning (hold the line, let the enemy charge in) rather than offensive aggression. **Pricing 7** — same as Halberdier; Spear's S 3 baseline is weaker than Halberd's S+1 = S 4, but Receive Charge anti-charge bonus + 2" reach for second-rank attacks balances the cost. Default kit includes Medium Shield (Spearman lore is *spear-and-shield*).
