# Forest Goblin Arachnarok Spider

> The largest of the spider-spawn worshipped by the Forest Goblin tribes — colossal eight-legged predators bred (or conjured) by Forest Goblin shamans in deep jungle hollows, then ridden into battle bearing a small platform of goblin warriors on their backs. Forest Goblins of the deeper forests have a peculiar faith: they worship the **Spider God**, a chaos-aspect deity whose totems the goblins paint in spider-blood and venom. Arachnaroks are the apex of this faith made flesh — eight enormous legs, chitinous armoured plates, fangs that drip venom potent enough to paralyse a man twice over. They **Wall-crawl** through any terrain (the spider doesn't care about briars or undergrowth — it makes its own path). Unlike Saurus, Bastiladon, or Warsphinx, the Arachnarok is a *biological* monster — chitin-armoured (NA 4+), venom-tipped, fast for its size, animalistic and Imm to Psychology rather than disciplined or constructed. It does not break, because it doesn't think enough to fear. **Venom Surge** is the iconic anti-large attack: once per combat round the spider may deliver a venom-laden killing strike, devastating against monsters and large-frame infantry. The model itself is **the largest in the cross-faction set** — bigger than Stegadon, bigger than Carnosaur, bigger than Warsphinx — and the W 18 / M 8 / LiS 5 stat-line reflects that scale.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin faction-wide identity rule — Forest Goblin Crew flavour)* — at the start of each Movement phase, **roll D6** for the unit. The roll is **only made if the unit is not currently engaged in melee combat** — units in melee are too occupied to squabble. On a **1**, the goblin crew's natural aggression breaks discipline. **Roll D6 again**:
  - **1-3** — *"Get the Spider Movin'!"* The unit **must declare a charge against the nearest visible enemy** in charge range this Movement phase. The crew rouses the spider for an unplanned attack. **If no charge is possible**, treat as result 4-6 instead.
  - **4-6** — *"Spear in the Eye!"* The Forest Goblin Crew is too busy poking each other to coordinate ranged fire. The unit **may not Shoot this turn** — no Forest Goblin Shortbow shots, no Flinger fire if equipped. The Arachnarok itself moves and attacks normally; only the Crew's ranged contribution is disabled.

  The spider — Imm Psych — doesn't *care* about the bickering, but the goblins on its back are still goblins. Crew-only effect; the Arachnarok's morale and movement are unaffected.
- **Immune to Psychology** *(per §8, Arachnarok-specific)* — the spider's animal mind ignores Fear, Terror, friendly-unit-fleeing stress triggers. Combat-wound stress still applies; the spider isn't unkillable, just fearless. **No Stubborn** — the Arachnarok doesn't *resist* in the disciplined sense; Imm Psych alone captures the animal-mind that doesn't break because it doesn't think to.
- **Poisoned Attacks** *(per §8, both spider AND crew)* — Arachnarok and Forest Goblin Crew melee + ranged attacks gain **+1 to-Wound**. The spider's venom drips from fangs and legs; the goblins coat their weapons (spear and shortbow) in milked spider-venom from the Arachnarok's own glands. Faction-flavour: Forest Goblins are the *poison-everything* sub-tribe of Greenskins.
- **Wall-crawler** *(per §8 — promoted to a general rule during the Forest Goblin Spider Riders draft, 2026-05-19)*. The Arachnarok ignores Movement penalties from Difficult / Dangerous Terrain and may crawl over vertical impassable features (cliffs, walls). Cannot end movement inside horizontal impassable terrain (water, lava, void).
