# §12 Open Questions


1. **Weapon- and unit-specific charge enhancements:** +1 I baseline and Impact attacks (resolved in the Charges sub-phase) are locked. Open: whether specific weapons (lances etc.) or unit types need further charge modifiers, within the constraint that "chargers must not always attack first."
1a. **Flame Template dimensions:** drafted at 8" long × 3" wide at the base. No playtest data yet on whether this footprint is right. Worth revisiting once breath weapons and flame-template spells see table time.
2. **Stress number calibration:** System locked (Res-based thresholds, unified wound-to-stress, D3 recovery, no Res tests, and — per the 2026-07-13 audit — **uncapped combat-loss stress**; the old +3 cap was removed since the casualty trigger was already uncapped). Open: whether the specific numbers — Res − 4 / Res − 2 / Res thresholds, 2 wounds = 1 stress, D3 base recovery, +2 stress for Terror, +1 for being targeted/shot/spelled — balance well in play, and in particular whether uncapped blowout rounds break high-Res elites too easily. Playtest-dependent.
3. **Magic system — remaining work:** Core mechanics (§7.3), stress interaction (§5), Magic Resistance (§8), Remains In Play (§7.3), Summoning (§7.3), Ward Piercing (§8), Armour-Seeking (§8), all eight general lores, and both Lizardmen faction lores (Geomancy and Cosmic Architecture, Slann-only) are drafted. Still needed: Disciplines of the Old Ones (Slann purchasable upgrades), bound spells/magic items, and an audit pass for range diversity across the lores (currently most spells default to 18").
4. ~~**Characteristic tests:** not yet formalised.~~ **Resolved (2026-07-13).** Formalised in §2 *Characteristic Tests*: roll-under D6 against the (modified) characteristic; **natural 6 always fails, natural 1 always passes**; modifiers adjust the characteristic, never the die; tests are per-model; combined models test the highest profile value (— routes to the primary profile); Res is never tested. Existing spell wordings (Death, Shadow) already match the semantics.
5. **Points costs:** Framework drafted in §13 (statContribution × woundFactor). Calibrated against the current Lizardmen roster; refinement of per-rule contribution values will be playtest-driven.

6. ~~**Splitting WS into Offensive and Defensive (deferred discussion):**~~ **Resolved.** WS is split into **MA** (attack) and **MD** (defence) across all profiles, with the §2 To Hit (Melee) table comparing attacker's MA to defender's MD. Asymmetric profiles (frenzied glass-cannons, evasive skirmishers, bred-warrior Saurus) now express their identity directly via the split.

7. ~~**Pursuit mechanic — currently undefined.**~~ **Resolved.** Movement-phase pursuit drafted in §4 *Pursuit* (after Disengaging / Free Strike). Triggered when an enemy unit's last model breaks the pursuer's melee reach via voluntary movement (Disengage / Advance / March). Free reaction; **D3"** toward the closest model of the disengager. **Frenzy**, **Predatory Fighter**, and **Pursuit Predator** make pursuit mandatory; **Pursuit Predator** additionally rolls **D6"** instead of D3". The same three rules also force Follow up on Fall Back (no Hold option) — §4 *Fall Back* updated; §8 Frenzy and Predatory Fighter entries gained the mandatory-pursuit clauses. The Jaguar Standard (§10 Lizardmen Magic Items) adds +D3" to the pursuit roll alongside its charge-distance bonus, restored to 25 pts.

8. **2026-07-13 audit decisions — playtest-sensitive values:** the hole-audit pass locked several mechanics whose specific numbers are first-draft: **risky fire** into melee (-1 to hit, natural 1s strike the nearest engaged friendly — §7.4); **per-model destruction** when a Broken flee crosses the board edge (§5); the **committed surge** on failed charges (advance up to M along the declared path, halting outside mutual reach — §6); **Impact wounds counting toward CR** (§4); **Ambushers arriving just outside melee reach** (§8). Mechanics locked; values and feel to be validated at the table.
