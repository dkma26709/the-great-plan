# Slann Mage-Priest


> Corpulent, toad-like, serene. Borne aloft on a floating stone palanquin levitated by arcane energies that have held since the time of the Old Ones. Attended always by a single Skink chosen at spawning, who stands upon the palanquin to defend the Slann's body while the Mage-Priest's mind is elsewhere — reading the threads of the Great Plan, rearranging the subtle architecture of reality, warding back Daemons who press against the skin of the aether. A Slann alive at the battle-line is an entire nation's weight of tradition and cosmic memory on a single stone seat. Few in number, a dying race — no new Slann have spawned since the Old Ones departed.

Knows the **Signature Spell from each of the 3** (free; do not count against additional picks) + picks **3 additional spells** from the combined spell pool (any tier within Lore Access limits — 6 known spells baseline: 3 Signatures + 3 picks).   - **Contemplation** — the Slann does not personally engage in melee. Larger-Lord-on-larger-mount baselines (Oldblood on Carnosaur, etc.) typically extend Inspiring Presence ranges; the Slann sits one tier above that as the apex commander

> **Lore notes:** **Pass 2 (2026-04-30)** — refines the Slann's combat profile around the *floating-stone-throne* concept, adds Inspiring Presence as the army-commander identity, and introduces the Amulet of the Old Ones as the canonical survival mechanic. Stats deliberately frail on *physical* axes — **WS-A 0**, BS 0, S 3, T 4, I 1 — with strength entirely in magic and presence. WS-A 0 is new (was 1) and reflects the lore-true reality that the Slann does not personally wield a weapon: physical combat is something done **at** the Slann, not **by** the Slann. **Palanquin Lightning** replaces the prior Skink Attendant melee proxy entirely — the celestite array fires independently at fixed 2+ to-hit (lightning's targeting array is independent of the Slann's WS), A 3 / S 5 / AP -1 / D D3 / Magical / I 1. The Skink Attendant rule is dropped: the figure persists in lore narrative (Privileged Skinks attend the Slann) but no longer needs mechanical encoding. Cleaner profile, lore-true mechanic. W 8 matches the Oldblood for Lord-tier durability — the Slann's *physical* durability is mortal-toad scale; the Slann's *survival* mechanism is the **Amulet of the Old Ones** (teleport-to-safety on lethal blow). **Res 12** leverages our above-10 Res cap for the single most resolute battlefield presence in the faction — a Slann on the field holds stress thresholds mortal characters cannot reach. Ward Save **4+** baseline is Shield of the Old Ones — the Slann is inherently magically protected (the palanquin itself is unarmoured mechanically; the save represents magical saturation). **Inspiring Presence (24")** is new (pass 2) — double the standard 12" range, reflecting the Slann's role as the army's *cosmic* commander. Lore: "Privileged Skinks attend to the Slann... project their sight and will through certain Skinks, particularly Priests and Oracles" — the Slann directs the entire battle-line through telepathic projection and the Geomantic Web alignment, not through shouting at nearby units. Comparison: standard Lord-on-larger-mount profiles (Saurus Oldblood on Carnosaur) typically extend Inspiring Presence ranges; Slann sits one tier above as the apex commander. **Mastery of All Winds** is the core Slann identity, grounded in 6th-ed lore ("may use any Lore and mix them"). Faction lore access includes both **Cosmic Architecture** and **Geomancy** — the Slann is the sole caster who commands faction-exclusive disciplines. Disciplines menu is tight (12 options, cap 2 picks) and axes along three vectors: **lore-access** (Loremaster / Breadth / Greater Mastery / Wandering Deliberations), **raw magic-stat bumps** (Rumination / Cogitation), and **specialised effects** (Convergence / Unfathomable Presence / Soul of Stone / Reservoirs of Mystery / Fury of the Ancients / Higher State of Consciousness). Four cut from earlier drafts: Unassailable Form (redundant with Higher State), Cupped Hands of the Old Ones (is a magic item, not a discipline — note the Amulet of the Old Ones is now drafted as the Slann-exclusive magic item), Drain Magic (too strong as a discipline), Generation of the First Spawning (doesn't fit our pool-magic system). **Magic Resistance** explicitly NOT added as intrinsic — the existing magic stats (Dispel Base 5, Dispel Bonus +4, Dispel Range 36") + the **Unfathomable Presence** Discipline (-2 to enemy cast rolls within 18") already cover the lore vector ("enemy wizards find the mightiest incantations they try to employ unravelling before them"). Adding intrinsic MR would be redundant. **Points 400 → 430** absorbs the Inspiring Presence (24") bump and the Palanquin Lightning melee weapon (real combat output, vs prior trivial Attendant proxy). Magic profile bumped from Cast Base/Dispel Base/Channelling 4 and Cast Bonus/Dispel Bonus +3 → 5s and +4s to establish a clear tier gap above the Empire Wizard Lord (max Level 4 at Cast Base 4, Dispel Base 3, Channelling 3, Cast Bonus +3, Dispel Bonus +2, Dispel Range 24") — Slann is unambiguously the apex caster in the ruleset. BSB option available (+25) without the magic-item restriction that applies to other characters' BSB — Slann BSB keeps full 100-pt magic item allowance alongside any magic banner. **Lizardmen species inheritance:** Slann do NOT inherit Saurus/Kroxigor pass 2 traits (Snapping Jaws, Tail Sweep, Locked Jaws, AP-1 Jaws bumps, Aquatic) — Slann are toad-like, not crocodilian; their species traits (Cold-Blooded, Fear) are already on profile.
