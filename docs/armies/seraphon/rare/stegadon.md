# Stegadon


> A living siege engine. Bony plates thick enough to turn cannonballs, horns that can gore a knight and his horse in one thrust, and a howdah full of Skinks raining death from above. The Stegadon is the Lizardmen exception to the "no armour" identity — but even here, the protection is biological. Those dorsal plates grew, they weren't forged.

**Stegadon (Beast):**

| M | WS-A | WS-D | BS | S | T | W | I | Res | LiS | US |
|---|----|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|-----|----|
| 6 | 3 | 3 | 0 | 6 | 6 | 12 | 2 | 4 | 4 | =W |

*Stegadon US equals its **current remaining Wounds** (per the Monster-US convention — see §1). A full-strength Stegadon contributes US 12 to its unit and the battlefield; a heavily-wounded Stegadon contributes proportionally less. **Note: A (Attacks) is not a profile stat — each weapon carries its own Attack count** (see Weapon profiles below). Framework-wide pass to move A onto weapons is pending; Stegadon is the first profiled unit under the new convention.*

**Skink Crew (×5 per Stegadon, fixed — fires the Howdah weapon):**

|---|----|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|-----|----|

*Skink Crew share the Stegadon's M (they are in the howdah — they do not move independently). Their profiles are used for resolving attacks, wounds, and howdah weapon shots; primary-profile wound allocation and Mixed Unit rules apply per §1.*

**Points:** **285** per Stegadon + 5 Skink Crew bundle

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurian, Monster, Rare (Stegadon); Lizardmen, Skink, Infantry, Rare (Crew)
- **Unit type:** Mixed Unit (Monster + Skink Infantry support, per §1)
- **Unit composition:** 1 Stegadon + **5 Skink Crew** (fixed ratio)
- **Natural Armour:** 3+ (armoured dorsal plates — the tankiest save in the roster; these plates genuinely turn cannon shot)
- **Ward Save:** — (none baseline)
- **Equipment:**
  - Stegadon: Goring Horns, Crushing Bulk, Armoured Tail (natural weapons — see profiles)
  - Howdah weapon (player picks one at list building): Giant Bow OR Giant Blowpipes (see weapon profiles)
  - Skink Crew: hand weapons (self-defence only in melee)
  - No shields
- **Command options:** none (single-beast unit)
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Terror** (see §8) — causes Terror, not just Fear
  - **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) — direct lore: "Stegadons care little for other creatures and fear nothing." The Stegadon rampages without registering enemy intimidation; Fear, Terror, and other Psychology-keyword effects do not apply. Stress from mechanical sources (lost combat, taking wounds) still accumulates normally.
  - Mixed Unit (see §1)
  - **Skink-Reared Bond** *(unit identity rule)* — while this Stegadon has at least one model within **6" of a friendly Lizardmen Skink unit**, that Skink unit gains **+1 Res** for all purposes. Captures the lore-canonical "Stegadons grow to become very protective of those Skinks they have known since hatchlings" — the massive armoured bulk is a comforting bulwark; nearby Skinks think faster, hold firmer, and panic less. Mirrors the Kroxigor's Emboldened Handlers rule pattern, in a different cross-species direction.
  - **Unstoppable Momentum** — this unit's Goring Horns charge strike resolves as **D3 hits per enemy model in melee reach**, instead of a normal single hit per attack. Not swinging — ploughing. The bow wave of a massive body hitting a formation at speed.
  - **Degrading** — performance degrades as wounds are lost. See damage table below.

- **Crushing Bulk** *(Stegadon, always-available)* — 1" reach | **A D6** | S 6 | AP -1 | D 1 — the sheer tonnage pressing forward, stepping on things, knocking them aside `[Natural]`

- **Giant Bow** *(Howdah, if chosen)* — 36" range | S 6 | AP -3 | D D3 — a single bolt the size of a spear, devastating one-shot anti-armour

> **Lore notes:** **Pass 2 (2026-04-30)** adds **Immune to Psychology** ("Stegadons care little for other creatures and fear nothing" — direct lore for psychology resistance) and **Skink-Reared Bond** ("Stegadons grow to become very protective of those Skinks they have known since hatchlings" — +1 Res aura on friendly Skink units within 6", mirroring Kroxigor's Emboldened Handlers pattern). Death Throes considered (lore: "heart beats for long hours thereafter") but skipped per design call — current Degrading + Unstoppable Momentum already encode the Stegadon's mass-effect identity without bookkeeping. Lizardmen species inheritance from Saurus pass 2 (Snapping Jaws / Tail Sweep / Locked Jaws / Predatory Fighter) explicitly NOT propagated — Stegadons are herbivore-Saurian (primordial reptile), not Saurus crocodile-kin biology. T 6, W 12, Save 3+ — extremely tanky, though T reduced by 1 during review to avoid overlap with Bastiladon which is the true "fortress" save-profile. The save is earned: those dorsal plates are near-impervious to normal weapons. Terror (not just Fear) reflects the sheer mass-effect presence — a Stegadon on the field is something the enemy reorganises around, not ignores. LiS 4 (not 5 as initially drafted) — Stegadons are big but not city-block big; a Monster in the LiS-4 tier. US = W remaining makes objective-scoring and targeting dynamic as the Stegadon takes damage, matching the Monster-scale presence feedback loop. Unstoppable Momentum D3-per-enemy-model on charge is the horn signature, scaled down to D2 then 1 per enemy model as the beast falters. **Pricing 275 → 285** absorbs the Imm Psych + Skink-Reared Bond additions; 5 Skink Crew remain bundled with the Stegadon beast, priced via §13 extended wound factor at the revised 0.5/W coefficient (W12 factor 7.48). Framework note: this was the first unit profiled under the **A-on-weapons convention** — A is no longer a stat on the profile line but lives on each weapon.
