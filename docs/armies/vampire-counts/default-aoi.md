# Default AoI:


> The classic Vampire Counts army — a Vampire Lord (or one of his lieutenants) commanding a court of risen dead. Skeletons and Zombies form the rank-and-file horde; Grave Guard the elite Wight-bound counter-strike; Necromancers and Vampires sustain the binding through Invocation of Nehek and other Lore of Necromancy spells. The Vampire is the army's heart — kill the master and the dead bleed off the table to Crumble. Tactical pivot: every game is a balance between resurrecting losses fast enough and protecting the casters who do the resurrecting.

**Composition Table** *(default constraints per §9.1)*:

| Category | Constraint |
|---|---|
| Lords | Maximum 25% of army points |
| Heroes | Maximum 25% of army points |
| Core | Minimum 25% of army points |
| Special | Maximum 50% of army points |
| Rare | Maximum 25% of army points |

**Category Assignments:**

| Category | Units |
|---|---|
| **Lords** | Vampire Lord |
| **Heroes** | Vampire *(Hero)*, Necromancer |
| **Core** | Skeleton Warriors, Zombies |
| **Special** | Grave Guard |
| **Rare** | *(no units drafted yet — Black Knights, Varghulf, Terrorgheist, Mortis Engine, Crypt Horrors, Vargheists, Dire Wolves, Cairn Wraiths, Hexwraiths pending future drafts)* |

**Army-wide rules:** None. (Themed AoIs may add — e.g., a *Strigoi Bloodline* AoI might shift Crypt Horrors to Core when drafted, a *Sylvanian Watch* AoI might add army-wide Strider (Forest), a *Vampire Coast* alliance AoI might unlock pirate / undead-coast units.)

**Mandatory picks:** **At least one model with the *Lore of Necromancy* spell-list (Vampire Lord, Vampire Hero, or Necromancer)** — without a caster, the Undead horde Crumbles on contact and the army's binding is unsustainable. The Vampire-Court identity *requires* the Necromancy economy. (This is one of the few cases where the default AoI enforces a mandatory pick at all — fundamental to the faction's mechanical viability.)

**Banned picks:** None.

**Magic lore access:** Vampire Lord and Vampire Hero access the **Lore of Necromancy** (faction-flavoured; full lore expansion pending). Vampire Lord may additionally access one general lore at character drafting (typically Death or Shadow). Necromancers access Necromancy only.

**Named-hero access:** No named characters drafted yet.

**Faction-specific army-build hooks:**
- **Undead keyword** (per §8) — all rank-and-file and Vampire characters carry it. No psychology-source stress, no Rally, Crumble at end of Combat phase.
- **Vampiric Hunger** intrinsic on all Vampire models (Bite-feeds 1 W per turn — see Faction Mechanics).
- **Bone Tide / Numberless / Lingering Souls** — per-unit Necromancy-economy modifiers (Skeletons cheaper to resurrect; Zombies restore +2 W per spell; Grave Guard resist resurrection at +1 CV cost).
