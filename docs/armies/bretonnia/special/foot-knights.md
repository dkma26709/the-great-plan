# Foot Knights

> The dismounted noble caste — knights who fight on foot when terrain or duty demands. Most prevalent in the dukedom of **Montfort**, where the Grey Mountains preclude mounted manoeuvre and Duke Folcard's anti-Greenskin campaigns muster the noble caste into the pine-forest passes alongside their levies. Equally common during siege warfare, where a horse is no use and a knight's voice is everything: the noble bellows commands from the ramparts, and generations of enforced servitude ensure those commands are obeyed without question. Foot Knights wear the same plate as their mounted brothers, carry shield and sword (or a great weapon for those who prefer the two-handed reach), and stand under the Lady's blessing. Where Knights of the Realm are the army's hammer and Pegasus Knights its hawks, Foot Knights are its **anchor and its voice** — the noble who plants himself beside the peasant line and *makes* it hold.

## Special Rules

- Disciplined
- **Knight's Vow** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — confers eligibility for the **Blessing of the Lady**
- **Blessing of the Lady** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — intrinsic via the Knight's Vow. Ward Save 6+ vs S ≤ 4, 5+ vs S ≥ 5. Lost on flee from combat
- **Bellowing Orders** *(unit identity rule)* — Friendly Bretonnia **Peasant** units within **12"** of any model in this unit treat the Foot Knights as friendly Bretonnia characters with the **Knight's Vow** keyword for **Peasant's Duty** purposes (per Men-at-Arms / Longbowmen / Bidowers profiles) — the peasant unit gains the effects of **Inspiring Presence (6")** (per §8 Resolve and Command) at this extended 12" range, regardless of whether other vow-bearing characters are within the standard 6" pin distance. **Additionally**, at the **Start of Turn** during natural recovery (per §5 Removal), each such peasant unit reduces its accumulated stress by **1 additional point** beyond its D3 recovery roll. The chivalric voice carries: a noble standing within bowshot of a peasant block is enough to keep the levy in line, and his shouted commands shave a little of the panic from each stressful interval

## Options

**Equipment options (swap, pick at most one):**
- **Great Weapon** (+2 pts/model — replaces hand weapon AND shield; cannot pair with shield) — combined save drops to **3+** (heavy armour only)

**Whole-unit vow upgrade options (mutually exclusive — pick at most one):**
- **Crusader's Vow** (+2 pts/model whole-unit upgrade) — replaces Knight's Vow with Crusader's Vow (see §17 Faction Mechanics). Grants Immunity to Panic-source stress, reroll failed psychology-source recovery rolls, and +1D6 charge distance roll (discard lowest). Pricing matches the Pegasus Knights / KotR Crusader's Vow whole-unit upgrade tier

**Command options:**
- **Leader** (+5 pts) — *Knight Captain*; the senior Foot Knight, often a Paladin's bannerman or a sergeant-of-the-castle veteran
- **Standard bearer** (+10 pts — Magic Standard up to 40 pts; chivalric banner budget, matching KotR / Questing Knights)
- **Musician** (+5 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls
