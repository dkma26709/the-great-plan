# Kroxigor


> Massive, powerful, and dim. The muscle of the Lizardmen — directed by Skink handlers, devastating when they connect. Two stories tall and slabs of rock-hard muscle, a Kroxigor moves with thundering tread, scales thicker than any Saurus's, jaws built for tearing flesh from bone. They emerge silent from swamp and waterhole; they swing weapons taller than a Saurus Warrior in wide arcs that splatter man-sized foes beyond recognition; their tails end in spiked clubs that punish anyone who tries to flank. You don't kill a Kroxigor by outfighting it. You kill it by hacking through an enormous amount of living meat — a feat one mercenary captain logged at twenty-three crossbow bolts to fell a single specimen.

- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - Aquatic (see §8) — at home in waterways and swamps; emerges silent from the waterhole, the swamp, the river-bend
  - **Fear** *(Psychology — see §8)* — "the sight of a Kroxigor alone is often enough to unnerve even the bravest intruder"; the blood-curdling roar, the thundering tread, the crocodilian silhouette. Range (6 + LiS)" = **9"** (LiS 3 — Kroxigor are visibly massive at distance).
  - **Locked Jaws** *(Lizardmen biology)* — when a Kroxigor model in this unit is removed as a casualty during the Combat phase, before removal it makes 1 final Jaws attack against the killing model (or closest enemy in base contact). Single attack, no further cascade.
  - **Emboldened Handlers** — while this unit has at least one model within **6" of a friendly Skink unit**, that Skink unit gains **+1 Res** for all purposes. The massive handlers are a comforting bulwark — nearby Skinks think faster, hold firmer, and panic less.
  - **Stupidity** (conditional) (see §8) — if no friendly Skink unit has a model within 6" of this unit at the start of its Movement-phase activation, this unit suffers **Stupidity** for that turn. Without the handlers to focus them, the Kroxigor lumber aimlessly toward the nearest perceived threat.

> **Lore notes:** **Pass 2 (2026-04-29)** — substantial buff package matching the lore: Kroxigor are explicitly "two stories tall," "scales thicker than emperor's bodyguard armour," "twenty-three crossbow bolts to fell," and "swallowing whole" man-sized foes. Direct stat changes: **W 7 → 8** (Yin-Tuan voyage quote: "twenty-three crossbow bolts" — direct numeric evidence of W beyond 7), **WS-D 3 → 2** (lore: "respond more slowly to external stimuli," "lumbering," "thundering tread" — the Kroxigor is a huge, slow target), **NA 5+ → 4+** (lore: "thick scales which covered its carcass provided better protection than even the lacquered scale armour worn by the emperor's bodyguard"). The combination encodes the lore-true profile: Kroxigor are *easy to hit but hard to actually hurt* — the WS-D 2 means mid-skill attackers connect on 3+, but each hit chips against W 8 + NA 4+ + T 5. Twenty-three crossbow bolts to fell is now the *expected* difficulty, not a heroic outlier. **Saurus Jaws AP 0 → -1, D 1 → D D3** captures the canonical "tearing flesh from bone" + "swallowed Captain Rossi whole" detail — the bite is now a meaningful threat against multi-W targets. **Tail Club** (new) is the explicit weaponized tail from canon ("ending in a spiked or spade-like club"); rear-arc-only natural attack at S 5 / AP -1 punishes flanking, makes surrounding Kroxigor genuinely costly. **Moonstone Club Wide-Arc Swings** is the headline weapon mechanic: A = enemy models in base contact within 3" reach in front arc, capped at min 2 / max 6. Captures lore-perfectly the "easily reach over any intervening Skinks, allowing the reptilian giants to pulp more than their share of any foe" + "splatter a man-sized creature beyond all recognition." A 2 minimum keeps Kroxigor effective vs single tough targets (Carnosaurs, Oldbloods); A 6 cap prevents runaway scaling against deep formed infantry — the Kroxigor's swing has a physical arc-radius limit. **D 2 → D D3** on the Club spikes damage to 3 against multi-W foes (Carnosaur, Saurus Oldblood, multi-W cavalry) while preserving D 2 average against rank-and-file. **Fear** captures "even the bravest intruder unnerved" + "blood-curdling roar that reverberates across the jungle." **Aquatic** captures the amphibious, swamp-emerging identity ("submerged in waterholes," "came out of the swamp") without bolting on a contrived no-Stand-and-Shoot rule. **Locked Jaws** is the soft Lizardmen biology inherit — same crocodilian persistence as Saurus species. **Emboldened Handlers / Stupidity pair preserved** — the central Kroxigor tension. **Pricing 85 → 140** absorbs W bump, WS-D nerf-to-balance-defence, NA 4+ bump, Wide-Arc Swings, D D3 Club, Tail Club, Saurus Jaws upgrades, Fear, Aquatic, Locked Jaws — substantial identity density bump on a Monstrous Infantry chassis. Net mechanical fingerprint: *easy to hit, hard to hurt, devastating when swinging into a packed front rank, punishing to flank*. Lore-true to the Yin-Tuan and Claudia Piedua quotes alike — the lumbering crocodile-warrior emerging from the swamp.
