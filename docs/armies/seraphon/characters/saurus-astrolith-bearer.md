# Saurus Astrolith Bearer


> A Saurus warrior gifted a sacred Astrolith icon — a disc of glyph-inscribed Celestite, dominated by a mysterious globe at its centre. Not the greatest fighter of his Constellation, nor its most capable general; but deemed by the Slann to bear a great destiny, and so given the icon. Whilst the Saurus are not prideful creatures, the trust placed in them gives them a great deal of satisfaction: they mercilessly slaughter any who approach, the icon raised high while the Winds of Azyr blow strong around it.

- **Special rules:**
  - Disciplined (see §8)
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Predatory Fighter** (see §8) — Saurus species
  - **Fear** (see §8) — Saurus species
  - **Locked Jaws** *(Saurus species rule)* — when this Bearer is removed as a casualty during the Combat phase, before removal it makes 1 final Saurus Jaws attack against the killing model (or closest enemy in base contact). Single attack — no Predatory Fighter cascade.
  - **Stubborn** (see §8) — the Astrolith Bearer holds his ground; the icon stays raised
  - **Astrolith** *(identity rule, intrinsic — cannot be dropped or transferred)* — while this Bearer is alive and not Broken, all friendly **Wizards** with at least one model within **12"** of the Bearer gain **+1 to their casting rolls**. The icon channels celestial energy into nearby Seraphon casters; the Winds of Azyr blow strong around the Astrolith.

> **Lore notes:** A Saurus warrior gifted a sacred Astrolith icon — chosen by the Slann not as the greatest fighter, but as one deemed by the Old Ones to bear a great destiny. Identity is the icon, not the duel. **Astrolith** is the lore-direct mechanic: friendly Wizards within 12" gain +1 to cast rolls — captures "Seraphon who tap into this energy find their powers greatly enhanced in the icon's presence." Distinct from the Arcanadon's +1 Channelling aura (clean carve: Astrolith = cast bonus, Arcanadon = channel bonus). Foot only — the Astrolith disc is a heavy ceremonial relic, held aloft. Cannot also carry the army Battle Standard; one Astrolith Bearer per army.
