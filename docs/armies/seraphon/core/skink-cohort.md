# Skink Cohort


> Small, quick, cunning. Everything a Saurus isn't. Drilled to fight in disciplined rank-and-file — fragile individually, but packed tight with shields and, if the chief commands, venomed javelins.

| M | WS-A | WS-D | BS | S | T | W | I | Res | LiS | US |
|---|----|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|-----|----|
| 8 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 4 | 6 | 1 | 1 |

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Skink, Infantry, Core
- **Unit type:** Infantry (formed-capable; no Skirmish state)
- **Unit size:** 10-30
- **Points:** 6 per model
- **Natural Armour:** 6+ (wet-slick scaly hide)
- **Equipment (default):** Hand weapon, Medium Shield
- **With shield:** combined save **5+** (Natural 6+ + Medium Shield +1 step, per §8 Armour Save Stacking)
- **Equipment options (additive, not replacements):**
  - **Moonstone Javelins** for the whole unit (+1 pt per model) — see Armoury
  - **Poisoned Attacks** on all weapons (melee and ranged) for the whole unit (+1 pt per model) — see §8
  - **Kroxigor attachment** (up to 1 Kroxigor model per 6 Skinks in the unit, at full Kroxigor cost each — see *Kroxigor Handlers* below)
- **Command options:**
  - **Patrol Leader** (champion, +5 pts) — acts as unit leader for Rally, challenges, and Leader-snipe purposes (no stat buff)
  - **Standard bearer** (+5 pts)
  - **Musician** (+5 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - Aquatic (see §8)
  - Skittish (see §8)
  - **Cohort Synchronicity** *(unit identity rule)* — while this unit has **10 or more models** and is in **Formed** formation, it may **reroll failed melee to-hit rolls of 1**. Captures the lore-canonical "members of a single cohort display an almost uncanny level of coordination and instinctive collaboration" + "deceptively fast skirmishers who coordinate strikes with impressive synchronicity through a chorus of chirps and clicks." Lockstep cohort signalling — chirps, clicks, crest-colour shifts — sharpens the unit's collective swing-rhythm into a tightly-coordinated rake of strikes.
  - **Kroxigor Handlers** — when the unit includes one or more Kroxigor models, each Kroxigor is a full member of the unit. In combat and when taking hits, each model uses its own profile, stats, and save (Skinks as Skinks, Kroxigor as Kroxigor). The unit's base movement uses the slowest model's M (so the Skinks effectively pace themselves to the Kroxigor's M7 — they scamper around the handlers to keep coherency). The unit retains the **Infantry** unit type and gains the **Monstrous Infantry** keyword for targeting purposes. *Paired with Skinks* is automatically in effect on any embedded Kroxigor (they are always within 6" of a Skink unit — this one).

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Hand weapon** *(default, 1H)* — 1" | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 `[1H Blade]`
- **Moonstone Javelin** *(option, adds ranged capability)* — 8" range | S 3 | AP -1 | D 1 | Quick to Fire | Poisoned Attacks (baseline, stacks with unit-wide Poisoned Attacks upgrade redundantly — see Armoury)

> **Lore notes:** WS3 is the bump that distinguishes the drilled cohort from the loose-order Skirmishers — a cohort skink, braced in the line with a shield and a handler nearby, can actually trade blows with peasant-grade human infantry. Still loses to elites. The real teeth is Poisoned Attacks (+1 to wound) as a whole-unit upgrade, or the Kroxigor attachment for a literal handful of crushing muscle in the line. **Pass 2 (2026-04-29)** adds **Cohort Synchronicity** — the cohort-only reroll-1s-to-hit when in Formed with 10+ models, captures the canonical chirp-and-crest cohort signalling lore. Modest melee buff, conditional on holding ranks. **Snapping Jaws / Tail Sweep / Locked Jaws / Fear** explicitly **NOT** propagated to Skinks — Skinks are skirmishing scouts not predators (jaws are small, tails are for balance not weapons, no death-bite biology, no intimidation). The Lizardmen species inheritance for Saurus / Kroxigor stops at the Saurus-tier-and-above. Pricing 5 → 6 absorbs Cohort Synchronicity. A-on-weapons convention applied.
