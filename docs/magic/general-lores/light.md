# Lore of Light


> Divine radiance. A protective lore first, a damage lore second — the light steadies the faithful, wards against darkness, and burns the corrupt where it falls upon them. Where Heavens is celestial and impersonal, Light is holy and disciplined. Its wizards stand still to channel properly, and their magic rewards troops that hold their ground rather than those that run ahead.

**Lore Attribute — Burning Purity:** this wizard gains **+1 to their casting roll** on a Light spell if they did **not move** during this turn's Movement phase (neither Advanced, Marched, Charged, Wheeled, nor voluntarily Fled). The disciplined, contemplative wizard channels light more effectively. Most wizards want to move, so this is a genuine trade-off — the bonus is earned by committing to stillness.

**Signature Spell — Shield of Light**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains **Ward vs Spells (6+)** until start of next turn. A modest magical shielding — the cornerstone of Light's protective identity.

#### Basic (Tier 1) — Lore Access 1+

**Steady the Faithful**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment, Instant |

Target friendly unit immediately removes **D3 stress**. The light steadies their resolve — morale that was wavering finds focus. Distinct from Life's physical healing: this mends the mind, not the body.

**Guiding Light**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 6 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit may reroll failed to-hit rolls of 1 in both melee and ranged attacks until start of next turn. A subtle clarity — the wizard illuminates targets, gives the unit sight of their foe.

**Pha's Protection**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 7 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains **+1 to all armour and ward saves** until start of next turn. Walls of brilliance deflect incoming blows. Subject to the normal Armour and Ward stacking caps (2+ armour, 4+ ward from stacking).

#### Intermediate (Tier 2) — Lore Access 2+

**Net of Amyntok**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 8 | 24" | Hex | Until start of next turn |

Target enemy unit's **Movement is halved** (round up) and the unit cannot declare a **March** action until start of next turn. Bands of golden light anchor them to the ground. Pairs thematically with the disciplined caster — the light judges those who move too freely.

**Timewarp**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 9 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit gains **+2 Movement and +2 Initiative** until start of next turn. Time dilates around them — they move faster, react faster. A major positional and strike-order buff.

**Speed of Light**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 8 | Self | Augment (caster only), Instant |

The caster and their unit (if attached to one) may immediately make a free **M + D6 move** directly away from the nearest enemy model. A bolt of brilliance that carries them to safety. Does not prevent the caster from casting again this phase; does not trigger any pursuit, Elusive-style penalty, or stress from Fleeing (this is a controlled magical escape, not flight).

#### Advanced (Tier 3) — Lore Access 3+

**Banishment** *(RIP, template, stationary)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 11 | 24" | Template (large round), Remains In Play |

On cast: aim a point within range. The template **scatters D3"** in a random direction and lands. Every model whose base is under the final template position takes **1 hit at S5, AP -2, D2**.

Each subsequent controlling-player Magic phase while in play: the template **remains stationary**. All models currently under it take **1 hit at S5, AP -2, D2**.

A pillar of searing light fixed in place. Devastating to anything caught beneath it and impossible to ignore — the opponent must dispel it, detour around it, or eat the damage every turn. Unlike Fire's drifting Flame Storm, Banishment's threat is predictable, and that predictability shapes the opponent's movement.

**Pha's Judgement**

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 11 | 24" | Magic Missile |

**D6 hits at S5, AP 0, D2, Ward Piercing (3)**. Against units with the **Daemon** or **Undead** keyword: the spell instead deals **D6+2 hits** (same profile). The lore's anti-unnatural capstone — Ward Piercing 3 strips virtually any ward save, and the extra hits against the corrupt push the damage further.

**Light of Battle** *(RIP, aura)*

| CV | Range | Type |
|----|-------|------|
| 10 | Self | Augment (aura), Remains In Play |

*Remains In Play.* While in play, friendly units with any model within **12"** of the caster are **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) and may **reroll failed recovery and rally rolls**. A standing aura of clarity and resolve — being within the light holds an army together through horror. The caster becomes a morale anchor, and positioning them near the main line is a major tactical consideration.

**Beacon of Absolution**

| CV | Range | Type | Duration |
|----|-------|------|----------|
| 11 | 18" | Augment | Until start of next turn |

Target friendly unit immediately **removes all stress**, becomes **Immune to Psychology** (see §8), and gains **Ward vs Spells (5+)** until start of next turn. A total mental and magical cleansing — the light rewinds the damage of fear and magic in one absolute moment. Ideal for a unit about to break, a target under magical pressure, or any desperate save-the-crucial-unit moment.
