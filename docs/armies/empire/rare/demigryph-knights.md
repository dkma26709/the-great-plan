# Demigryph Knights


> The Empire's Rare-slot shock cavalry. Demigryphs are griffon-dogs — lesser kin of the great Griffon, tameable where the true Griffon will not bear a rider unless it accepts you. The Reiksguard and certain Elector Count households keep Demigryph kennels. When they charge, they're claws-and-beak as much as lance — Monstrous Cavalry scale, able to run down knights and monstrous infantry alike. The Empire's answer to Chaos Knights, Ogres, and anything else that outmasses a Warhorse. Ridden only by Inner Circle Knights — no junior knight earns a Demigryph.

- **Keywords:** Empire, Human, Monstrous Cavalry, Rare
- **Unit type:** Monstrous Cavalry
- **Unit size:** 3-6
- **Combined pool per model:** W 4 (rider 1 + mount 3). Attackers use highest WS (4) and highest T (4) per §1 *Rider and Mount Profiles*
- **Natural Armour:** —
- **Worn armour (default):** Full plate + Heavy Shield + barding (per §8 Armour Save Stacking, one step per source; cap 2+)
- **Combined save: 2+** (full plate 3+ → Heavy Shield 2+ → barding at cap 2+)
- **Equipment (default):** Polearm *(Empire Halberd profile — 1H with Heavy Shield)* + full plate + Heavy Shield + barding
- **Equipment options:**
  - **Heavy Lance** (+6 pts/model — replaces Polearm; Heavy Shield retained) — charge-focused shock weapon; trades the polearm's consistent damage for a charge-only damage spike
- **Upgrade option:**
  - **Knightly Order designation** (whole unit, flat cost per Order — see Imperial Knights for the 5-option menu: Reiksguard / White Wolf / Panther / Blazing Sun / Everlasting Light). Orders interact with Demigryph Knights identically; same per-unit costs
- **Command options:**
  - **Leader** (+5 pts)
  - **Standard bearer** (+10 pts — Magic Standard up to 50 pts)
  - **Musician** (+5 pts)
- **Special rules:**
  - Disciplined (see §8)
  - **Fear** (see §8) — Demigryphs are griffon-kin; their scent and predatory motion unsettle lesser creatures
  - **Griffon-kin Instinct** *(identity rule)* — mount's **Demigryph Claws** and **Rending Beak** attacks both gain **+1 to wound** when targeting any model with the **Monstrous Infantry, Monstrous Cavalry, Monster**, or **Warbeast** keyword. Lore: Demigryphs hunt larger prey than Warhorses would dare — the beast's instinct sharpens against worthy quarry

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Polearm (Empire Halberd)** *(rider default, 1H with shield)* — **2" reach | A 1 | S+1 (= S 5) | AP -1 | D 1** — consistent damage every round; the polearm's +1 S on S 4 rider brings strikes to S 5. Note: the rider's Polearm carries the same **Killing Blow (Cavalry / Monstrous Cavalry, 2)** trait as the State Troops Halberdiers' weapon — the hook works whoever swings it `[1H Reach]`
- **Heavy Lance** *(rider option, +6 pts — replaces Polearm, shield retained, 1H)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | on charge (first round): S+2 (= S 6), AP -2, D 2; subsequent rounds: S+0 (= S 4), AP 0, D 1
- **Demigryph Claws** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 4 | AP -1 | D 1 — slashing forelimbs, the consistent-damage half of the Demigryph's natural arsenal `[Natural]`
- **Rending Beak** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 4 | AP -2 | **D D3** — the Demigryph's hooked beak tears flesh in long strips; lore-faithful spike-damage attack matching the "rending" identity. Griffon-kin Instinct applies, making the beak especially lethal against MonstInf/MonstCav/Monster/Warbeast targets `[Natural]`

> **Lore notes:** Demigryph Knights are the Empire's closest parallel to Seraphon Aggradon Lancers — both are Monstrous Cavalry shock units, both ridden by elite warriors, both the faction's heavy-hitter answer to enemy elites. Comparison contrast: Aggradons are slow / tanky / harder-hitting (M 7, T 5, W 6 combined, S 5 claws, Blood Frenzy); Demigryphs are fast / better-armoured / lighter-hitting (M 10, T 4, W 4 combined, S 4 claws, Griffon-kin Instinct). Saurus on Aggradons = slow, plodding, unstoppable once committed. Inner Circle Knights on Demigryphs = faster, better-armoured, disciplined, controllable, smaller wound pool. Aggradons hit harder per attack (S 5 vs S 4), Demigryphs hit more targets better (Griffon-kin +1 wound against MonstInf/MonstCav/Monster/Warbeast). Points gap 80 vs 120 justified by W 4 vs W 6 (quadratic factor), T 4 vs T 5, and Blood Frenzy's identity value — Demigryphs' better save (2+) and higher M/I partially offset. Rider baseline = WFB Inner Circle stats (S 4 / I 4) already; no further Inner Circle upgrade available (Demigryph Knights start at veteran tier). Mount baseline M 8 → 10 unbarded matches Warhorse bump and Horned One tier; with barding (default kit) the Demigryph drops to M 8 per the §8 Barding rule. Combined 2+ save via full plate + shield + barding. **Fear** baseline differentiates from Imperial Knights (no Fear). **Griffon-kin Instinct** ties to the unit-type keyword ladder (same convention as Carnosaur's Apex Predator) — consistent cross-faction pattern. Knightly Order upgrades cascade from Imperial Knights with the same menu and costs.
