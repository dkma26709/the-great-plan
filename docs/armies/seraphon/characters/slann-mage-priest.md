# Slann Mage-Priest


> Corpulent, toad-like, serene. Borne aloft on a floating stone palanquin levitated by arcane energies that have held since the time of the Old Ones. Attended always by a single Skink chosen at spawning, who stands upon the palanquin to defend the Slann's body while the Mage-Priest's mind is elsewhere — reading the threads of the Great Plan, rearranging the subtle architecture of reality, warding back Daemons who press against the skin of the aether. A Slann alive at the battle-line is an entire nation's weight of tradition and cosmic memory on a single stone seat. Few in number, a dying race — no new Slann have spawned since the Old Ones departed.

> **Lore notes:** Slann stats are deliberately frail on *physical* axes — WS 1, BS 0, S 3, T 4, I 1 — with strength entirely in magic and presence. W 8 matches the Oldblood for Lord-tier durability, reflecting the Slann's primordial-magic sustenance (not a "caster needs to live" concession — the Slann *is* harder to kill by virtue of what it is). **Ld 12** leverages our above-10 Ld cap for the single most resolute battlefield presence in the faction — a Slann on the field holds stress thresholds mortal characters cannot reach. Ward Save **4+** baseline is Shield of the Old Ones — the Slann is inherently magically protected (the palanquin itself is unarmoured mechanically; the save represents magical saturation). The **Skink Attendant** is a clean mechanical wrapper around 6th-ed Contemplation: the Slann has a melee profile without pretending to be a melee character, and the Attendant exists narratively without being a separately-targeted unit member — he dies when the Slann dies, not before and not after. **Mastery of All Winds** is the core Slann identity, grounded in 6th-ed lore ("may use any Lore and mix them"). Faction lore access includes both **Cosmic Architecture** and **Geomancy** — the Slann is the sole caster who commands faction-exclusive disciplines. Disciplines menu is tight (12 options, cap 2 picks) and axes along three vectors: **lore-access** (Loremaster / Breadth / Greater Mastery / Wandering Deliberations), **raw magic-stat bumps** (Rumination / Cogitation), and **specialised effects** (Convergence / Unfathomable Presence / Soul of Stone / Reservoirs of Mystery / Fury of the Ancients / Higher State of Consciousness). Four cut from earlier drafts: Unassailable Form (redundant with Higher State), Cupped Hands of the Old Ones (is a magic item, not a discipline), Drain Magic (too strong as a discipline), Generation of the First Spawning (doesn't fit our pool-magic system). Points **400** base — Lord-tier pricing anchored to the Oldblood (260) + magic package (~140 flat for the Cast Base 5 / Dispel Base 5 / Channelling 5 / Cast Bonus +4 / Dispel Bonus +4 / Lore Access 4 bundle) + Ward Save + Attendant + Mastery lore pool; Disciplines stack +20 to +50 each on top. Magic profile bumped from Cast Base/Dispel Base/Channelling 4 and Cast Bonus/Dispel Bonus +3 → 5s and +4s to establish a clear tier gap above the Empire Wizard Lord (max Level 4 at Cast Base 4, Dispel Base 3, Channelling 3, Cast Bonus +3, Dispel Bonus +2, Dispel Range 24") — Slann is unambiguously the apex caster in the ruleset. BSB option available (+25) without the magic-item restriction that applies to other characters' BSB — Slann BSB keeps full 100-pt magic item allowance alongside any magic banner.
