# Stegadon

> A living siege engine. Bony plates thick enough to turn cannonballs, horns that can gore a knight and his horse in one thrust, and a howdah full of Skinks raining death from above. The Stegadon is the Lizardmen exception to the "no armour" identity — but even here, the protection is biological. Those dorsal plates grew, they weren't forged.

## Special Rules

- Cold-Blooded
- **Terror** (see §8) — causes Terror, not just Fear
- **Immune to Psychology** (see §8) — direct lore: "Stegadons care little for other creatures and fear nothing." The Stegadon rampages without registering enemy intimidation; Fear, Terror, and other Psychology-keyword effects do not apply. Stress from mechanical sources (lost combat, taking wounds) still accumulates normally.
- Mixed Unit
- **Skink-Reared Bond** *(unit identity rule)* — while this Stegadon has at least one model within **6" of a friendly Lizardmen Skink unit**, that Skink unit gains **+1 Res** for all purposes. Captures the lore-canonical "Stegadons grow to become very protective of those Skinks they have known since hatchlings" — the massive armoured bulk is a comforting bulwark; nearby Skinks think faster, hold firmer, and panic less. Mirrors the Kroxigor's Emboldened Handlers rule pattern, in a different cross-species direction.
- **Unstoppable Momentum** — this unit's Goring Horns charge strike resolves as **D3 hits per enemy model in melee reach**, instead of a normal single hit per attack. Not swinging — ploughing. The bow wave of a massive body hitting a formation at speed.
- **Degrading** — performance degrades as wounds are lost. See damage table below.
