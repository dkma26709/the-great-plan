# Skink Cohort


> Small, quick, cunning. Everything a Saurus isn't. Drilled to fight in disciplined rank-and-file — fragile individually, but packed tight with shields and, if the chief commands, venomed javelins.

| M | WS-A | WS-D | BS | S | T | W | I | Res | LiS | US |
|---|----|----|----|---|---|---|---|----|-----|----|
| 6 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 2 | 1 | 4 | 6 | 1 | 1 |

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Skink, Infantry, Core
- **Unit type:** Infantry (formed-capable; no Skirmish state)
- **Unit size:** 10-30
- **Points:** 5 per model
- **Natural Armour:** 6+ (wet-slick scaly hide)
- **Equipment (default):** Hand weapon, Medium Shield
- **With shield:** combined save **5+** (Natural 6+ + Medium Shield +1 step, per §8 Armour Save Stacking)
- **Equipment options (additive, not replacements):**
  - **Moonstone Javelins** for the whole unit (+1 pt per model) — see Armoury
  - **Poisoned Attacks** on all weapons (melee and ranged) for the whole unit (+1 pt per model) — see §8
  - **Kroxigor attachment** (up to 1 Kroxigor model per 6 Skinks in the unit, at full Kroxigor cost each — see *Kroxigor Handlers* below)
- **Blessed Spawnings:** up to 3 per unit, per-model costs from §10 menu (Skink rate; Kroxigor models in the unit pay the Kroxigor rate per their own per-model line). Each spawning beyond the first promotes the unit one FoC tier (Core → Special → Rare; max Rare). The whole unit (Skinks + any attached Kroxigor) takes a single shared spawning per slot.
- **Command options:**
  - **Patrol Leader** (champion, +4 pts) — acts as unit leader for Rally, challenges, and Leader-snipe purposes (no stat buff)
  - **Standard bearer** (+4 pts)
  - **Musician** (+4 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - Aquatic (see §8)
  - Skittish (see §8)
  - **Cohort Synchronicity** *(unit identity rule)* — while this unit has **10 or more models** and is in **Formed** formation, it may **reroll failed melee to-hit rolls of 1**. Captures the lore-canonical "members of a single cohort display an almost uncanny level of coordination and instinctive collaboration" + "deceptively fast skirmishers who coordinate strikes with impressive synchronicity through a chorus of chirps and clicks." Lockstep cohort signalling — chirps, clicks, crest-colour shifts — sharpens the unit's collective swing-rhythm into a tightly-coordinated rake of strikes.
  - **Kroxigor Handlers** — when the unit includes one or more Kroxigor models, each Kroxigor is a full member of the unit. In combat and when taking hits, each model uses its own profile, stats, and save (Skinks as Skinks, Kroxigor as Kroxigor). The unit's base movement uses the slowest model's M (so the Skinks effectively pace themselves to the Kroxigor's M6 — they scamper around the handlers to keep coherency). The unit retains the **Infantry** unit type and gains the **Monstrous Infantry** keyword for targeting purposes. *Paired with Skinks* is automatically in effect on any embedded Kroxigor (they are always within 6" of a Skink unit — this one).

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Hand weapon** *(default, 1H)* — 1" | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 `[1H Blade]`
- **Moonstone Javelin** *(option, adds ranged capability)* — 8" range | S 3 | AP -1 | D 1 | Quick to Fire | Poisoned Attacks (baseline, stacks with unit-wide Poisoned Attacks upgrade redundantly — see Armoury)

> **Lore notes:** Drilled rank-and-file Skinks — fragile alone, dangerous massed in formation. WS 3 is the bump that distinguishes the cohort from the loose-order Skirmishers: a cohort Skink braced in the line with a shield and a handler nearby can actually trade blows with peasant-grade infantry. The real teeth is Poisoned Attacks as a whole-unit upgrade, or a Kroxigor attached as a handful of crushing muscle in the line. **Cohort Synchronicity** (reroll 1s to hit, conditional on Formed + 10+) captures the canonical chirp-and-crest cohort signalling. Snapping Jaws / Tail Sweep / Locked Jaws / Fear deliberately do **not** propagate from Saurus — Skinks are skirmishing scouts, not predators (small jaws, balancing tails, no death-bite biology, no intimidation). Species inheritance stops at Saurus-tier-and-above.
