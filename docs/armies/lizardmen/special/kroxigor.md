# Kroxigor


> Massive, powerful, and dim. The muscle of the Lizardmen — directed by Skink handlers, devastating when they connect. Two stories tall and slabs of rock-hard muscle, a Kroxigor moves with thundering tread, scales thicker than any Saurus's, jaws built for tearing flesh from bone. They emerge silent from swamp and waterhole; they swing weapons taller than a Saurus Warrior in wide arcs that splatter man-sized foes beyond recognition; their tails end in spiked clubs that punish anyone who tries to flank. You don't kill a Kroxigor by outfighting it. You kill it by hacking through an enormous amount of living meat — a feat one mercenary captain logged at twenty-three crossbow bolts to fell a single specimen.

- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - Aquatic (see §8) — at home in waterways and swamps; emerges silent from the waterhole, the swamp, the river-bend
  - **Predatory Fighter** (see §8) — Saurus-kin biology; unmodified to-hit rolls of 6 in melee generate an additional attack
  - **Fear** *(Psychology — see §8)* — "the sight of a Kroxigor alone is often enough to unnerve even the bravest intruder"; the blood-curdling roar, the thundering tread, the crocodilian silhouette. Range (6 + LiS)" = **9"** (LiS 3 — Kroxigor are visibly massive at distance).
  - **Locked Jaws** *(Lizardmen biology)* — when a Kroxigor model in this unit is removed as a casualty during the Combat phase, before removal it makes 1 final Jaws attack against the killing model (or closest enemy in base contact). Single attack, no further cascade.
  - **Emboldened Handlers** — while this unit has at least one model within **6" of a friendly Skink unit**, that Skink unit gains **+1 Res** for all purposes. The massive handlers are a comforting bulwark — nearby Skinks think faster, hold firmer, and panic less.
  - **Stupidity** (conditional) (see §8) — if no friendly Skink unit has a model within 6" of this unit at the start of its Movement-phase activation, this unit suffers **Stupidity** for that turn. Without the handlers to focus them, the Kroxigor lumber aimlessly toward the nearest perceived threat.

> **Lore notes:** Two stories tall, slabs of rock-hard muscle, scales thicker than the Emperor's bodyguard's armour. The mechanical fingerprint is *easy to hit, hard to actually hurt*: WS-D 2 (lumbering, thundering tread — the huge, slow target) plus W 8 + NA 4+ + T 5 means twenty-three crossbow bolts is the *expected* difficulty, not a heroic outlier. **Moonstone Club Wide-Arc Swings** is the headline weapon: A = enemy models in base contact within the 3" reach in front arc (min 2 / max 6) — captures "easily reach over any intervening Skinks... pulp more than their share." Saurus Jaws at AP -1 / D D3 captures the "swallowed Captain Rossi whole" scale; **Tail Club** punishes flanking, the explicit weaponised tail from canon. **Emboldened Handlers / conditional Stupidity** is the central Kroxigor tension — the muscle is a comfort to Skinks nearby, and it lumbers aimlessly without them.
