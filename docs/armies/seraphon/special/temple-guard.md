# Temple Guard


> The elite of the Saurus spawnings. Sworn at the spawning pool to guard the Slann Mage-Priests and the temple-cities, Temple Guard dedicate every fibre of their being to the task — armed with heavy ornamental Celestite Halberds, plated with bronze, and helmed in the horned skulls of Lustrian predators. Some are as old as the Slann they protect; when one falls, his sacred skull-helm is salvaged to await a new spawning who will take it up and inherit a portion of his predecessor's strength and martial skill. Their scales are thicker than rank-and-file Saurus — capable of turning aside blows that would penetrate a Saurus Warrior's hide. They stand sentry silent and motionless for centuries if needed, dust settling upon their reptilian forms — yet erupt into sudden violence at the first sign of threat. Masters of defensive warfare, almost invulnerable within their interlocked shield walls.

> **Sworn Protectors.** The Temple Guard's purpose is absolute: the Slann must survive.
>
> - **Bodyguard.** When an enemy attack (melee, ranged, spell with a single-unit target, or Sniper) is being resolved, if this unit and a friendly Slann are *both* valid targets, the attacker **must** choose this unit instead of the Slann. Does not prevent attacks where the Slann is the only valid target (no Temple Guard in range / arc / LOS).
> - **Slann's Resolve.** While this unit has at least one model within **3" of a friendly Slann**, stress thresholds (Shaken, Wavering, Broken) are treated as **+1 higher**, AND the unit **cannot be pushed back** by losing combat resolution. Without a nearby Slann these bonuses do not apply — the unit remains Stubborn but is otherwise normal.

> **Lore notes:** The elite Saurus spawning, sworn at the pool to guard the Slann and the temple-cities — masters of defensive warfare, almost invulnerable within their shield walls. The high WS (5/5) is itself the inherited-skill mechanic — sacred skull-helms passed down generations of Temple Guard, no separate rule needed. **NA 4+** captures the canonical "thick scales that withstand attacks penetrating Saurus Warriors' hides," and lands the unit at a 2+ combined save with Stardrake Shield (a flavour rename of Medium Shield). **Shield Wall** captures the silent, motionless vigil that erupts into sudden violence: when not charging, +1 WS-D pairs with Stubborn to make Temple Guard genuine anvils. **Sworn Protectors** packages the bodyguard role — Temple Guard soak attacks aimed at their Slann and make the Slann's own unit unbreakable. Inherits Locked Jaws / Tail Sweep / Fear from the Saurus species.
