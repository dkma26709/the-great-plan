# Ripperdactyl Riders

> Skink Braves bonded to Ripperdactyls — aggressive, single-minded predatory flyers that hunt a designated target to the exclusion of everything else. Where Terradons carry bombs and volleys, Ripperdactyls *commit*: jaw-first into the chosen prey, beak and claw until the quarry is dead. Each unit is ritually imprinted before battle on a specific enemy unit — the Blot Toad ritual — and the Ripperdactyls remember that imprint with predatory focus.

## Special Rules

- Cold-Blooded
- Fast Cavalry
- **Fly (12)** (see §8)
- Fear (Ripperdactyls are Fear-causing)
- **Predatory Instinct** — this unit **must declare a charge against the nearest visible enemy unit within charge range** in its Movement phase, if able. The Ripperdactyls hunt on sight and cannot be restrained by the rider — the rider's role is to redirect momentum, not suppress it.
- **Toad Rage** — at the start of the battle, after deployment and before turn 1 begins, the controlling player places a **Blot Toad Marker** on one enemy unit of their choice. The marker remains for the rest of the battle. Whenever this unit makes attacks against the marked unit:
- **+1 to hit** on all attacks (rider and mount)
- The Ripperdactyl mount may **reroll failed wound rolls** The Blot Toad is a Lustrian amphibian whose glandular secretion is rubbed on the Ripperdactyl's beak and eye-ridges before battle — the mount fixates on whatever the marker was placed on and hunts that quarry above all else.

## Options

**Equipment options:** none baseline

**Blessed Spawnings:** up to 3 per unit, per-model costs from §10 menu. Each spawning beyond the first promotes the unit one FoC tier (Special → Rare; max Rare). Spawning effects apply to the Skink Brave rider only — the Ripperdactyl mount profile is untouched (Itzl exception aside).

**Command options:**
- Patrol Leader (champion, +4 pts, no stat buff)
- No Musician, no Standard — Ripperdactyls are too aggressive for formation coordination; the rider hangs on and points
