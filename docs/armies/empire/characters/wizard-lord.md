# Wizard Lord


> Trained at the Colleges of Magic in Altdorf — the eight Imperial Colleges (Light, Metal, Life, Fire, Heavens, Death, Shadow, Beasts) each produce wizards of a specific Wind. Wizard Lords are senior College masters: decades of arcane study, a reputation that earns them battlefield command, and unambiguous mastery of one Wind. A Wizard Lord cannot mix Winds — a Light Lord channels Hysh, a Heavens Lord channels Azyr, and so on. Battlefield role: ranged magical artillery, support buffs, or hex coverage depending on the chosen College. The Empire's high-tier caster — below the Slann's apex throughput, but with full eight-Wind selection at list-build (including Life, which the Skink-caste convention restricts).
