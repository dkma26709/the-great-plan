# Bretonnia — Test Profiles


> **Design principle:** Cross-faction demonstration roster — fourth non-Seraphon faction after Empire, Ogres, and Vampire Counts. Bretonnia tests the **cavalry-dominant army** archetype: nearly every list is mounted, peasant levies are cheap chaff, and the army's identity is concentrated in the noble caste. Where Empire tested rank-and-flank human infantry, Ogres tested Monstrous-Infantry-as-whole-army, and Vampire Counts tested the Undead paradigm, Bretonnia tests our **Rider/Mount profile system at scale** (most units are mounted), the **charge mechanic** with the iconic Lance Formation, and the **faction-wide Ward-Save-as-divine-blessing** pattern via the Lady of the Lake. Drafted 2026-04-26 with a friend playtest as the immediate target — completeness bar is higher than for prior cross-faction stress tests.

> **Heft calibration note for Bretonnia.** Two distinct heft tiers within the faction: **knights** (chivalric nobles, professional warriors, often Lord-blessed) take Empire-elite calibration — Res +1 from WFB reference (typically Res 8 → 9 for rank-and-file knights), W stays at reference (1 for rank-and-file knights, modest bumps for Heroes/Lords per the §16 cross-faction Hero W +1 convention). **Peasants** (Men-at-Arms, Peasant Bowmen) take Empire-rank-and-file calibration — Res +1 from reference (Res 6 → 7), W and other stats unchanged. Lord-tier characters: **Bretonnian Lord W 3 → 5** (combat-lord identity — leads from front, +1 W over Imperial General to capture the chivalric front-line role), **Prophetess W 3 → 4** (caster Lord, matches Wizard Lord baseline). Hero-tier (Paladin W 2 → 3, Damsel W 2 → 3) match Empire Captain / Battle Wizard post-bump. WS / BS / S / T / I / A close to reference throughout — humans are humans; Bretonnia knights inflate similarly to Empire knights, peasants similarly to Empire State Troops.

> **Faction-wide rule reminders.** All units in the Bretonnian roster carry one of three vow / status markers determining Blessing eligibility:
> - **Knights of the Realm** — carry the **Knight's Vow** (Blessing eligibility only; pure marker)
> - **Questing Knights** — carry the **Questing Vow** (Blessing + Stubborn)
> - **Damsels of the Lady, Prophetesses of the Lady** — **the Lady's chosen** (Blessing intrinsic, no vow needed; the Lady chose them directly as her handmaidens)
> - **Bretonnian Lords, Paladins** — carry the **Knight's Vow** by default (assume the Vow when commanding a Bretonnian army — chivalric nobles are sworn knights)
> - **Men-at-Arms, Peasant Bowmen, Trebuchet crew** — commoners; no vow, no Blessing
>
> **Lance Formation** is a **sub-state of the standard Formed formation** (per §6) available only to mounted Bretonnian Knight units in a triangular wedge. See Faction Mechanics below — Lance is faction-specific to Bretonnia even though Empire and Lizardmen also field heavy cavalry; the chivalric tradition produces Lance discipline that other factions' knights lack.
