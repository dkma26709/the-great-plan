# Ironguts

> Elite Ogre Bulls — veterans, scarred survivors, Tyrant's chosen. Larger than rank-and-file Bulls (though still smaller than Kroxigor in our scaling), wielding ogre-scale great weapons (a human great weapon looks like a hand weapon to an Ogre). Light armour over the gut-plate. In tribal lore, Ironguts are the Tyrant's bodyguard, the elite shock unit that cracks enemy elite formations and answers Chaos Warriors / Saurus Temple Guard / High Elf Sword Masters at the elite-infantry tier.

## Special Rules

- **Bull Charge** *(see Ogre Bulls — identity rule)* — D3 Impact Hits per model at S 5 AP -1 D 1, I 10
- **Bone Crackers** *(identity rule)* — when Ironguts attack with great weapons in melee and roll an **unmodified 6 to wound**, the attack gains **+1 AP** (cumulative — base AP -2 becomes AP -3 on those wounds). Represents the brutal swing of an Ogre-scale great weapon finding the perfect angle through plate
