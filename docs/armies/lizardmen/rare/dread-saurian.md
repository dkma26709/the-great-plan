# Dread Saurian

> The apex. Larger than a Carnosaur, larger than a Stegadon, dorsal fins brushing the top of the jungle canopy as it moves. Jaws that can tear apart an adult Stegadon. Roars that echo across the titled plazas of Lizardmen cities and the deepest reaches of Lustria. A Dread Saurian is sacred to the Lizardmen — few in number, often dwelling within mountainous Old One temples — and its march is the arrival of an extinction event. Priced accordingly: the single biggest Monster in the setting.

## Special Rules

- Cold-Blooded
- **Terror** (see §8) — apex scale
- **Stubborn** (see §8) — maps Immunity (Psychology) from reference; the Dread Saurian does not panic
- **Bloodlust** *(Saurian identity rule — biology consistency with Carnosaur, Aggradon Lancers)* — once this Dread Saurian scores at least one unsaved wound in melee, it gains the **Frenzy** special rule (see §8) for the rest of the battle. The killing fury, once kindled, never abates — Frenzy cannot be lost by losing a combat round, by stress thresholds, or by any other rule that would normally strip Frenzy. Lore-direct: "constantly hungry... unending hunger... wreaks terrible havoc once let loose upon the battlefield."
- **Earth-Shaking Roar** *(identity rule)* — once per battle, at the start of any friendly Combat or Magic phase, the Dread Saurian unleashes its roar. All enemy units with any model within **18"** immediately gain **+2 stress**. The roar is heard for kilometres — in reference lore, across city plazas and through the jungle canopy. Represents the psychological mass-effect of the apex announcing itself
- **Mixed Unit** (see §1) — only if Howdah taken
- **Degrading** — 3-tier damage table below

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Rending Jaws** *(natural, front)* — **2" reach | A 4 | S 8 | AP -4 | D D3** — the apex bite; armour-piercing at AP -4, damage D D3 per hit, enough on raw numbers to tear apart a Stegadon without needing a dedicated armour-ignoring rule `[Natural]`
- **Crushing Claws** *(natural, always-available)* — 1" reach | **A 4** | S 7 | AP -2 | D 1 — massive slashing forelegs, tearing through ranks alongside the bite `[Natural]`
- **Trampling Mass** *(natural, always-available)* — 1" reach | **A D6** | S 6 | AP -1 | D 1 — anything small enough gets underfoot; the apex doesn't step around things, it steps through them `[Natural]`
- **Thunder Tail** *(natural, rear arc only)* — 3" reach | **A 3** | S 7 | AP -2 | D D3 — sweeping tail strike; keeps flankers honest at substantial damage `[Natural]`
- **Hand weapon** *(Howdah Skink Crew self-defence)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | AP 0 | D 1 `[1H Blade]`
