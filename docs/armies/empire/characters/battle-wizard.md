# Battle Wizard


> Junior tier of the Imperial wizard ladder — graduates of the Colleges of Magic at Altdorf, recently certified by their respective College Master. Each Battle Wizard channels one Wind only — Light, Metal, Life, Fire, Heavens, Death, Shadow, or Beasts; selected at the College and unchangeable thereafter. Field-deployed in support of the army, often the only caster a smaller Empire force can field. The Wizard Lord's apprentice — competent, formally trained, decades from College Master rank.

*Lvl 1 caster (LA 1 — Basic spell access only). May upgrade to **Lvl 2** at list-build for +30 pts: LA 2 reaches Intermediate spells within the chosen Wind, and bumps Channels and Cast Bonus by +1 each. Mark of the Wind = one general lore picked at list-building. Signature Spell known free + **1 additional spell** from the chosen lore (any tier within LA limits — Lvl 1 = Basic only; Lvl 2 = Basic or Intermediate).*

**Points:** **75** base (Lvl 1, hand weapon, no armour). **+30** to upgrade to Lvl 2 (= 105 pts).

> **Lore notes:** Stats from human caster baseline — WS / BS / S 3 / T 3 / I 3 (reference, no inflation; humans at base stats are genuinely fragile casters). **W 2 → 3** Hero-tier wound bump (revised 2026-04-25 alongside the §16 Vampire Counts Hero W bumps for cross-faction Hero-tier wound consistency). **Ld 7 → 8** for our stress-system calibration. Magic Profile is the entry tier of the Empire wizard ladder — Lvl 1 (LA 1) at CaB 2 / DiB 1 / Ch 1 / CB +0 / DB +0 / DR 18" is the weakest caster in the ruleset to date, befitting Lvl 1 College graduates. **Lvl 2 upgrade (+30 pts)** bumps Ch +1, CB +1, and unlocks LA 2 — at this tier the Battle Wizard's magic profile matches the Skink Priest exactly (CaB 2 / DiB 1 / Ch 2 / CB +1 / DB +0 / DR 18" / LA 2), with breadth-of-Wind (8 vs Skink's 7) and durability (W 3 / T 3 / Ld 8 vs Skink's W 2 / T 2 / Ld 6) as the residual differentiators. Skink Priest at 70 pts has Aquatic + Arcane Vassal (Slann relay) + M 8 mobility on top — the comparison pricing 105 vs 70 books in those Skink-specific advantages. Points **75** base / **105** upgraded — Hero-tier caster pricing. The level-up mechanic tests our LA-as-list-build-upgrade pattern (Skink Priest / High Priest are fixed at LA 2 / LA 3; Empire wizards have variable LA via the +30 / +35 upgrade) — useful contrast for cross-faction list-build flex. **Cross-faction Hero-caster parity:** Battle Wizard now matches the §16 Necromancer at 75 / 105 pricing — both are single-lore Hero casters with the same level-upgrade ladder; the Battle Wizard picks one of the eight Winds at list-build, the Necromancer is locked to Necromancy.
