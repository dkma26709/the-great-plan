# State Troops — Spearmen

> State Troops armed with long spear and Medium Shield, drilled to hold the line within a thicket of spear-points. Where Halberdiers chop and hook and Swordsmen fence, Spearmen present an unbroken hedgerow of leveled points — the front rank braces, the second rank stabs past the first, and the line absorbs charges that would shatter a sword-armed regiment. Particularly favoured in the northern provinces where cavalry encounters dictate the tactical calculus. The Detachment-system pairing is often Spearmen in the main block, Halberdier or Swordsmen detachment on the flanks.

## Special Rules

- Disciplined
- **State Troops** — reroll recovery / rally rolls within 6" of friendly Imperial General or BSB
- **Receive Charge** *(unit identity rule)* — when this unit is **charged in front arc** (per §6 Charges), the Spear weapon profile gains **+1 S** for the first round of the resulting combat only. Captures the lore-canonical "thicket of spear-points" anti-cavalry / anti-charge identity. The +1 S applies whether the charger is cavalry, infantry, monster, or any other charging unit type — the leveled spears bite harder when the enemy commits forward into them. Returns to baseline S 3 from round 2 onward (the bracing posture only generates the kinetic-energy advantage on the initial impact)
