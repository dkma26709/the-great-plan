# Bruiser


> The Tyrant's enforcer — second-strongest in the tribe, a muscle-bound bully whose size and brutality are surpassed only by the Tyrant himself. Bruisers maintain order and discipline within the tribe, especially when the Tyrant's attentions are elsewhere; they also serve as the Tyrant's lieutenants in battle, ramming subordinate units forward by personal threat. The traditional Battle Standard carrier in Ogre warbands — the Bruiser carries the tribe's grisly trophies and signs of station, partly as honour, partly because subordinate Ogres are too afraid of him to do otherwise.
