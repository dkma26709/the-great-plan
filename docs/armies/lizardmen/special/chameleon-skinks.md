# Chameleon Skinks

> Elite Skink ambushers of the chameleon-caste. Their scales shift colour and pattern to match canopy, stone, or water — a Chameleon Skink at rest is almost invisible even to another Skink. They carry Dartpipes refined by the temple apothecaries, with venom measured precisely for a single considered shot rather than the spray of common Skink Blowpipes. Where the cohort stands and the skirmisher darts, the chameleon-caste *waits* — and then the cannon crew dies, the wizard forgets his spell mid-cast, and the knight topples from his saddle with a dart in his throat.

## Special Rules

- Cold-Blooded
- Aquatic
- Skirmishers
- Scouts
- Loner
- **Chameleon** (see §8)
- **Telescopic Eyes** — this unit's ranged attacks always have the **Sniper** keyword (see §8). The independently-tracking, telescopic eyes pick out vulnerable joints, helmet slits, command markings — the shot finds the target whether the Skink is still or moving.
- **Perfect Stillness** — if this unit did not perform any voluntary movement during its own Movement phase this turn, its ranged attacks gain **+1 to hit** for that turn. The longer the chameleon-caste holds position, the deeper the camouflage settles and the surer the shot — a still Chameleon Skink sees the wizard's hand twitch, the cannon crew's rammer, the officer's signal, and picks the shot that ends the exchange.
