# Mazdamundi


> Lord Mazdamundi of the Enlightened Sphere — the oldest and most powerful living Slann Mage-Priest, ruler of Hexoatl the Solar-City. Pupil of Lord Kroak at the Dawn of Creation, now his successor in the living world. Where the entranced Slann slumber, Mazdamundi has shaken off lethargy: in recent centuries he has become "the most proactive of all the Slann." A flick of his wrist has shifted continents, smashed cities, doomed armies. He once buried thousands of Skaven beneath a tidal wave of stone with a single word and gesture taught to him by Lord Kroak. He levitates his palanquin-throne atop **Zlaaq**, a colossal Ancient Stegadon that has served him for nearly five hundred years.

The closer the foe, the brighter the sun-standard burns.
  - **Plaque of Tepec** *(intrinsic, once per battle)* — Mazdamundi may declare the use of the Plaque after an enemy spell is successfully cast but before its effects resolve. The spell **automatically fails** (no dispel roll required); the Plaque shatters into a thousand shards.
  - **Plaque of Xoloc** *(intrinsic, once per battle)* — Mazdamundi may declare the use of the Plaque before rolling a casting roll. He gains **+D6 to that casting roll** (rolled and added). The Plaque crumbles to dust.
  - **Geomancer's Awakening** *(identity rule, once per battle)* — at the start of any friendly Magic phase, Mazdamundi may cast **one Geomancy spell with no Cast Dice required** — the spell auto-succeeds at its base CV (no Miscast roll, no Dispel attempt allowed). Captures the lore-direct "raised one hand and using a word and gesture taught by Lord Kroak, used Geomancy to bury thousands beneath a wave of stone." A once-per-game guaranteed cataclysm.

> **Lore notes:** The strongest *living* Slann — Lord Kroak's pupil at the Dawn of Creation, now his successor. While most Slann slumber in entranced contemplation, Mazdamundi has shaken off lethargy: "the most proactive of all the Slann," moving continents and dooming armies with a flick of the wrist. He levitates his palanquin atop **Zlaaq**, an Ancient Stegadon that has served him for nearly five hundred years. **Geomancer's Awakening** is the marquee identity: once per game, cast a Geomancy spell with no Cast Dice required (auto-succeeds at base CV, no Miscast, no Dispel allowed) — captures the lore-direct "raised one hand and using a word and gesture taught by Lord Kroak, used Geomancy to bury thousands beneath a wave of stone." Four Disciplines baked in (Focus of Mystery for Loremaster Geomancy, Greater Mastery for 4 lores, Soul of Stone, Focused Rumination), four intrinsic relics (Cobra Mace as sentient retaliatory weapon, Sunburst Standard's dual-range deflection, Plaque of Tepec auto-dispel, Plaque of Xoloc +D6 cast bonus). Magic-power-wise, Kroak still leads on flexibility / reach / miscast safety; Mazdamundi has more *raw* spells known via Loremaster Geomancy. Lore-consistent: Kroak was the strongest caster ever, Mazdamundi is the strongest living one.
