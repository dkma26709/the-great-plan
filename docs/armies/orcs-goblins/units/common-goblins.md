# Common Goblins

> The open-plain Goblin rank-and-file — three to four feet of scrawny green malice, more eager to stab a fallen enemy than to face a standing one. Where Orcs charge in to *enjoy* the brawl, Common Goblins shuffle forward in a chittering mass because there are too many of them packed in to do anything else. Their courage is purely arithmetic: as long as the unit is twenty bodies deep, no individual Goblin is sure he'll be the one to die, and the mob holds its nerve through sheer crowd-anonymity (**Stubborn while the unit numbers 20+**). The moment the mob thins, the math reverses and panic feeds on itself (**-1 Res for the rest of the battle once below half starting strength**). Goblins are bad at fair fights — MA 2 captures the cringing, off-balance hack — but they have an instinct for the dishonest one: any enemy turning its back, or already breaking, gets a clean opportunistic blow (**Stab in the Back — +1 to Wound vs rear-arc or Shaken/Wavering targets**). A Common Goblin spear-block is not a unit you point at a Chaos Warrior wall and expect to win. It is a unit you point at a flank, a wavering line, or a wounded character, and expect to make the rest of the army's job easier.

## Special Rules

- Undisciplined
- **Animosity** *(Greenskin-wide faction trait — see §8 Psychology)*. D6 at start of each Movement phase when out of combat; on a 1, sub-roll D6 determines whether the unit charges nearest enemy or takes D3 self-hits at S 4.
- **Stab in the Back** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. This unit gains **+1 to Wound** against any target that is either (a) being attacked in its **rear arc** or (b) currently **Shaken or Wavering**. Triggers off either condition; does not stack with itself.
- **Cowardly Numbers** *(Goblin-typical — see §13 *Goblins*)*. While this unit has **20 or more models**, it is **Stubborn** (per §8 — ignores the state effects of Shaken and Wavering). Once this unit is reduced to **fewer than half its starting strength**, it suffers **-1 Res** for the rest of the battle. The two clauses do not interact directly — a unit that started with 20 models and drops to 9 loses its Stubbornness *and* takes the Res penalty; a unit that started with 16 never had Stubbornness in the first place but still takes the Res penalty once below 8.
- **No Loves a Scrap** *(Goblin sub-species — see §13)*. Common Goblins do not inherit the Greenskin faction-wide Loves a Scrap rule. Brawling drains Goblins; it does not refresh them.

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Hand Weapon** *(default, 1H — may pair with shield)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — short blade or club; the Goblin's instinctive sideways hack `[1H Blade]`
- **Spear** *(option swap, 1H — may pair with shield)* — 2" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — improvised spear-haft; the 2" reach lets a second rank stab past the first. No charge-defence bonus — Goblins are not drilled phalanx fighters, and a Goblin spear-wall would not be expected to hold a charge `[1H Reach]`
