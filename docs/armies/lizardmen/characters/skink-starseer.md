# Skink Starseer


> The greatest of Skink spellcasters — second only to the Slann in arcane matters. Where the Skink High Priest is a generalist Lord caster (single chosen lore, LA 3), the Starseer is a *specialist*: Master-tier in the Lore of Heavens, divining the future through star-patterns, anticipating stellar events with cosmic ramifications. Borne aloft on the **Palanquin of Constellations** — a floating throne of carved Celestite, augur-stones glowing with the constellations' shifts. Several Starseers serve each Slann; powerful Starseers can travel far from their masters and lead Lizardmen armies independently when no Starmaster is present.

  - **Arcane Vassal** *(keyword — same mechanic as Skink Priest / High Priest tradition)* — friendly Slann may cast spells through this Starseer's model position (see Slann's Arcane Vassal rule for full mechanic). The Starseer is the celestial-relay variant of the Skink-priesthood antenna — the Slann's psychic projection focuses through the Starseer's seeing-stones and augurs.
  - **Foresight of the Cosmos** *(identity rule)* — **once per game**, the Starseer may re-roll any single D6 rolled by themselves or any friendly unit with at least one model within **24"** of the Starseer. The re-rolled result stands. However, if the **re-rolled result is worse than the original**, the Starseer **suffers 1 wound** (the misread portent backlashes — lore-direct: "they are not beyond fault and can sometimes misread the portents, helping their enemies instead").
  - **Astromancer's Stave** *(identity rule, intrinsic)* — at the start of each friendly Movement phase, the Starseer may pick **one friendly Lizardmen unit with at least one model within 12"** of the Starseer. Until end of phase, that unit **adds +D3 to its charge distance roll** when it declares a charge. The Stave alters the local flow of time — Lizardmen move quicker, the enemy moves no faster. Lore-direct: "containing the power to alter the flow of time, often speeding the charges of their fellow Lizardmen."

> **Lore notes:** The Heavens specialist — Master-tier in the Lore of Heavens, second only to the Slann in arcane matters, divining the future through star-patterns. Distinct carve from the High Priest: where the High Priest is a generalist Lord caster (any single lore, LA 3 Advanced), the Starseer is the locked-Heavens specialist at LA 4 Master. Borne aloft on the **Palanquin of Constellations** — intrinsic Fly (8), the throne built into the profile rather than a mount option. **Foresight of the Cosmos** is the marquee identity with a lore-baked risk: once-per-game any-D6 reroll within 24", but a worse re-roll wounds the Starseer (lore-direct: "they are not beyond fault and can sometimes misread the portents, helping their enemies instead"). **Astromancer's Stave** alters the local flow of time — +D3 charge distance to one nearby Lizardmen unit per turn, captures "speeding the charges of their fellow Lizardmen." Bears **Arcane Vassal** like the rest of the Skink priesthood.
