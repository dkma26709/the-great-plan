# Kroxigor Ancient


> The oldest Kroxigor. Pale, bone-plated, ossified scales hardened with centuries into dense natural armour. Wisest of an otherwise simple-minded kind — knows the tempo of battle by feel alone, always found in the right place at the right time, often at the eleventh hour. Where rank-and-file Kroxigor wait for a Skink handler's signal, the Ancient reads the whole battlefield unprompted.

- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Aquatic** (see §8) — same Kroxigor amphibious biology as rank-and-file; "emerges silent from the waterhole, the swamp, the river-bend"
  - **Predatory Fighter** (see §8) — Saurus-kin biology; unmodified to-hit rolls of 6 in melee generate an additional attack
  - **Locked Jaws** *(Saurus species rule)* — when this Ancient is reduced to 0 W during the Combat phase, before being removed it makes 1 final Saurus Jaws attack against the killing model (or closest enemy in base contact). Single attack, no Predatory Fighter cascade. Same crocodilian death-bite as regular Kroxigor.
  - **Emboldened Handlers** — **does NOT apply** to the Ancient; the Ancient is too old to need Skink handlers
  - **Fear** (see §8)
  - **Eleventh Hour** *(identity rule)* — while any friendly Lizardmen unit within **12"** of the Ancient meets the **crisis criteria** (Monster / Warbeast / Character at half or fewer Wounds; or formation unit in Disordered / Broken state), the Kroxigor Ancient gains **+1 Attack on every weapon profile** and **cannot accumulate stress counters** (existing stress persists; no new stress). Trigger is continuous — bonus lapses the moment no nearby friendly unit meets the criteria, and resumes when criteria re-trigger. Represents "always in the right place at the right time, often at the eleventh hour" — the Ancient fights hardest when the line is crumbling, steadying it
  - **Old of the Spawning** *(character shift — per-unit, per §9.3)* — one **Kroxigor** unit in your army counts as **Core** instead of Special while a Kroxigor Ancient is in your list. Stacks across multiple Ancients (two Ancients → two Kroxigor units shift to Core). The eldest of the spawn-brothers calls his kin to the army's natural front line — the Kroxigor become rank-and-file backbone in his presence, not a specialist heavy-melee asset

> **Lore notes:** The oldest Kroxigor — pale, bone-plated, ossified scales hardened with centuries into dense natural armour. Wisest of an otherwise simple-minded kind; reads the tempo of battle by feel alone, always found in the right place at the right time. **NA 3+** literalises the "dense ossified plates" lore (Stegadon-grade biological save). **Emboldened Handlers does NOT apply** — the Ancient is too old to need Skink handlers. **Eleventh Hour** is the identity rule — output scales with the *army's condition*, not the turn count. When friendly Monsters / Heroes are bleeding or formations are crumbling within 12", the Ancient gains +1 Attack on every weapon profile and accumulates no new stress. The Ancient fights hardest when the line is crumbling, steadying it; when your line holds, the Ancient is merely strong. Inherits Locked Jaws / Tail Club / Aquatic from the Kroxigor species.
