# Knights Errant

> The young knights of Bretonnia — sons of nobility just released from squire's training, sworn the Knight's Vow but not yet tested in real war. Each rides his first true campaign on the Errantry Tour, the year of wandering combat that proves him a true knight. Mounted on the family's destrier, kitted in his father's hand-me-down plate, lance gripped tight, eager to win glory by the sword. The young knight is faster to charge than the seasoned Knight of the Realm — and harder to restrain from a fight. Where Knights of the Realm are the duke's hammer, Knights Errant are the duke's *eager* hammer: they hit just as hard, but they want to hit *more*.

## Special Rules

- Disciplined
- **Knight's Vow** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — Blessing-eligibility marker. No additional effect at this vow tier. Replace with Crusader's Vow at list-build (see Vow upgrade options above)
- **Blessing of the Lady** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — intrinsic via the Knight's Vow. Ward Save 6+ vs S ≤ 4, 5+ vs S ≥ 5, applying to rider and mount alike. Lost on flee from combat
- **Lance Formation eligible** (see §17 Faction Mechanics) — counts as Lance whenever the unit's geometry forms a triangular wedge of 6+ models in 3+ rows; +2" reach + +1 AP on melee weapons during the charge round
- **Purebred Warhorse** (see §8) — applies to the mount profile. Negates the -2 M penalty for barding; Knights Errant retain M 10 with full barding
- **Impetuous** *(identity rule)* — at the start of this unit's activation, if one or more enemy units are within charge range and visible in the unit's front arc, **roll a D6**:
- **4+** — the young knights cannot resist. The unit **MUST declare a charge** against one of those enemy units (controlling player's choice of target if multiple)
- **1-3** — the unit holds discipline this activation and may move, charge voluntarily, or take any other action normally
- **On charge** (whether forced by the Impetuous roll or declared voluntarily), the unit gains the following bonuses for that charge round:
- **Immunity to Fear / Terror stress** — the young knight is too excited to be afraid; stress that would normally accumulate from Fear or Terror keyword sources during the charge round is ignored
- **Charge distance reroll** — the controlling player may **reroll one of the charge distance dice** before adding to M (Formed: rerolls the D6; Loose: rerolls one of the 2D6, take the second result)

## Options

**Command options:**
- **Leader** (+4 pts) — *First Knight*; the most senior errant of the unit, often a Hero-tier knight on his second or third Errantry Tour
- **Standard bearer** (+8 pts — Magic Standard up to 20 pts; the family's heraldic banner, often that of the young knight's father or sponsor)
- **Musician** (+4 pts) — +1 to recovery and Rally rolls

## Weapon Profiles

### Close Combat

- **Heavy Lance** *(default, 1H with shield — fixed kit, no swap)* — **2" reach | A 1 | on charge (first round): S+2 (= S 5), AP -2, D 2; subsequent rounds: S+0, AP 0, D 1** — identical profile to KotR's lance. With Lance Formation +2" reach extension on charge, becomes 4" reach + AP -3 in the wedge. The young knights wield the same lance as their seniors — in their hands the weapon is no less deadly, only less disciplined `[1H Lance]`
- **Warhorse's Hooves and Bite** *(mount natural, each model)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | S 3 | AP 0 | D 1 — standard barded-warhorse profile, same as KotR `[Natural]`
