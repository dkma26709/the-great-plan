# Bastiladon


> An ancient armoured saurian whose shell is a walking fortress — the heaviest natural armour in the Lizardmen roster, shell plates thick enough to bounce cannon balls and indifferent to flanking attacks. Slow, implacable, and near-invincible defensively. Its back carries an enchanted relic, selected by the Slann before battle: the Ark of Sotek releasing serpent-swarms, the Revivification Crystal knitting wounds in nearby Lizardmen, or the Solar Engine channeling celestial energy into a searing beam.

- **Keywords:** Lizardmen, Saurian, Monster, Rare (Bastiladon); Lizardmen, Skink, Infantry, Rare (Crew)
- **Unit type:** Mixed Unit (Monster + Skink Infantry crew, per §1)
- **Unit composition:** 1 Bastiladon + Skink Crew (fixed count per relic — 4 for Ark of Sotek, 3 for Revivification Crystal or Solar Engine)
- **Natural Armour:** **2+** (living battle-fortress — the heaviest save in the Lizardmen roster)
- **Equipment (beast):** natural weapons (Slab-Paws, Crushing Bulk, Crushing Tail) + **one Howdah relic** (picked at list-build)
- **Howdah relic options:**
  - **Ark of Sotek** (default, +0 pts, 4 Crew) — see weapon profile below. Two-part effect: Summoning + Sotek's Sting.
  - **Revivification Crystal** (+10 pts, 3 Crew) — aura. All friendly Lizardmen units with any model within **6"** gain **Regeneration (6+)** (see §8). No shooting attack. The crystal's pulsing light knits bone and scale in whatever Lizardmen stand near it.
  - **Solar Engine** (+40 pts, 3 Crew) — two-part effect:
    - **Aura of Chotec:** all friendly Lizardmen units with any model within **6"** gain **+1 Initiative** for the rest of the battle while a Bastiladon carrying this engine is alive
    - **Beam of Chotec** — shooting attack fired from the engine by the Skink Crew. See weapon profile below
- **Command options:** none
- **Special rules:**
  - Cold-Blooded (see §8)
  - **Terror** (see §8)
  - **Stubborn** (see §8)
  - Mixed Unit (see §1)
  - **Impervious Defence** — this unit has **no flank or rear arc** for combat-resolution purposes. All attacks against the Bastiladon resolve as if made to the front arc for Flank (+1 CR) and Rear (+2 CR) bonuses — those bonuses do not apply. The Bastiladon's shell is identical on every side; it cannot be outflanked, only outnumbered.

**Weapon profiles (A on weapons):**

- **Hawkish Beak** *(Bastiladon, natural, front arc only)* — **1" reach** | **A 1** | **S 6** | **AP -2** | **D D3** — direct lore: "hawkish beaks deliver a bite that can cleave a full-grown bola-bola tree in two, or sever an armoured knight and his entire horse in half." Front-arc-only — turtle-style mouth at the front of the shell; the Bastiladon must face its target to bite. Solid anti-Cavalry / anti-Infantry profile, deliberately below the Crushing Tail in tier (the lore positions the tail as the *best* weapon, not the beak) `[Natural]`
- **Slab-Paws** *(Bastiladon, natural, always available)* — 1" reach | **A 2** | S 5 | AP -1 | D 1 — the armoured forelegs stomp and shove
- **Crushing Bulk** *(Bastiladon, natural, always available)* — 1" reach | **A D6** | S 5 | AP -1 | D 1 — the beast's sheer mass pressing forward, stepping on things, knocking them aside `[Natural]`
- **Crushing Tail** *(Bastiladon, natural, rear arc only — the Bastiladon's BEST weapon per lore)* — **3" reach** | **A 1** | **S 8** | **AP -3** | **D D3** — direct lore: "a massive knot of compacted bone covered over with armoured plates and bony stubs... can be swung with such devastating momentum that it can shatter granite or pulverize man-sized opponents into oblivion, leaving only blood-splattered craters... bone-breaking bludgeon, the impact can break the ribs of any Lustrian apex predator that would dare to attack a foe so armoured and armed." S 8 AP -3 is the apex Saurian-tail tier (comparable to Carnosaur's Massive Jaws S 7 AP -3, slightly above on raw S to reflect the "best weapon" framing). Rear-arc-only restriction preserved per Saurian-tail convention — the apex devastation only triggers when the enemy commits to flanking, but when it triggers it is genuinely fearsome. Pre-pass-2 was S 6 / AP -2 (under-tuned for the lore; Hawkish Beak was missing entirely) `[Natural]`
- **Ark of Sotek** *(howdah relic, default — two-part effect)*:
  - **Summoning (end of each friendly turn):** nominate a friendly **Jungle Swarms** unit within 6" of this Bastiladon. Roll a D6 — on a **4+**, add **one Jungle Swarm base** to the nominated unit. The unit may exceed its starting size by this means. If the new base cannot be placed legally (insufficient room, no model available), the summoning is lost with no effect.
  - **Sotek's Sting (any friendly Shooting phase):** the Ark may be activated once per Shooting phase. Roll a **D6"** range — all enemy units with any model within that distance of the Bastiladon suffer **2D6 hits at S 2, AP 0, D 1**, distributed per shooting wound allocation rules. Usable even if the Bastiladon moved, charged, or is engaged in melee. A writhing burst of serpents boiling from the Ark onto the nearest targets.
- **Beam of Chotec** *(Solar Engine howdah relic — shooting attack)* — **24" range | Magical Attacks | Flaming | AP -1 | D 1** | fired by the Skink Crew once per Shooting phase (resolves using the Crew's BS).
  - **Roll D6 when firing** to determine the shot count and Strength:
    - **1** — D3 hits at S 3
    - **2–3** — D6 hits at S 4
    - **4–5** — 2D6 hits at S 5
    - **6** — 2D6 hits at S 6, **and** the target unit suffers **-1 WS and -1 BS** (minimum 1) until the start of the target's next friendly turn
  - Magical Attacks and Flaming keywords apply to every hit regardless of the D6 roll. Slow to Fire does NOT apply — the engine is always ready to fire; the crew merely aims.
- **Hand weapon** *(Skink Crew self-defence)* — 1" reach | **A 1** | AP 0 | D 1 `[1H Blade]`

> **Lore notes:** **Pass 2 (2026-04-30)** — adds the **Hawkish Beak** weapon (was missing — direct lore: "hawkish beaks deliver a bite that can cleave a tree, sever a knight and horse"; front-arc only) and bumps **Crushing Tail** to S 8 / AP -3 (was S 6 / AP -2 — under-tuned for the lore-canonical "best weapon" framing). Weapon hierarchy now correctly reflects the lore: Crushing Tail (S 8 AP -3) > Hawkish Beak (S 6 AP -2) > Slab-Paws / Crushing Bulk (S 5 AP -1). The tail's rear-arc-only restriction preserves the Saurian convention (Stegadon Armoured Tail / Carnosaur Sweeping Tail are both rear-arc) and creates the "flank a Bastiladon at your peril" tactical situation — the apex devastator triggers only when the enemy commits to flanking. Lizardmen species inheritance from Saurus pass 2 explicitly NOT propagated — Bastiladon is herbivore-Saurian (turtle/ankylosaurus biology), not Saurus crocodile-kin. Locked Jaws considered but skipped (hawkish-beak biology, not crocodile-jaws). Skink-Reared Bond NOT propagated (Bastiladon's Skink relationship per lore is transactional/training, not the protective bond Stegadon shares with hand-reared Skinks). Imm Psych NOT added (Bastiladon lore is "engages threats with stomping," not "fears nothing"; Stubborn already covers the morale-hold angle). NA 2+ is the highest save in the entire roster — the Bastiladon is the only unit where biological armour rivals the best forged plate of any faction. Combined with T 7 (matching Stegadon; the two tankiest biological profiles are equal in raw resilience, the Bastiladon pulls ahead on save, the Stegadon on wound pool), W 10, and Impervious Defence's no-flank-or-rear, the Bastiladon is genuinely hard to dislodge; it is a moving terrain piece with a relic on top. The four-weapon melee profile (Hawkish Beak + Slab-Paws + Crushing Bulk + Crushing Tail) covers all engagement angles: front-arc beak for armoured cavalry, front-arc bulk for infantry-pulping, rear-arc tail for flanker-punishment. The howdah-relic choice is the unit's identity flex, each with a distinct play-style: Ark of Sotek (summoning + AoE, for Jungle-Swarm-farming armies), Revivification Crystal (support Regen aura, for tarpit armies), Solar Engine (magic-missile substitute + Initiative aura, for non-Slann lists that want a ranged platform). Beam of Chotec is now a **Magical-Attacks shooting weapon** (not a bound spell) — simpler, no magic-system integration needed, variable output via D6. **Pricing 270/275/305 → 280/285/315** absorbs the Hawkish Beak addition and the Crushing Tail S/AP bump. Points scale via §13 extended wound factor (W10 = 6.48 per revised linear extension at 0.5/W past W8); stat sum 38.5 × 6.48 = 250 beast, bundled with 4 Crew (Ark) or 3 Crew (Crystal/Engine) per the GW-model-correct count.
